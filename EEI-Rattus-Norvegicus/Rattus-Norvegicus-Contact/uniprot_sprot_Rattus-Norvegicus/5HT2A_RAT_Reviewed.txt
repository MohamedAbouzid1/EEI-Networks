CC   -!- FUNCTION: G-protein coupled receptor for 5-hydroxytryptamine
CC       (serotonin) (PubMed:2300586, PubMed:2854054). Also functions as a
CC       receptor for various drugs and psychoactive substances, including
CC       mescaline, psilocybin, 1-(2,5-dimethoxy-4-iodophenyl)-2-aminopropane
CC       (DOI) and lysergic acid diethylamide (LSD) (By similarity). Ligand
CC       binding causes a conformation change that triggers signaling via
CC       guanine nucleotide-binding proteins (G proteins) and modulates the
CC       activity of downstream effectors (By similarity). HTR2A is coupled to
CC       G(q)/G(11) G alpha proteins and activates phospholipase C-beta,
CC       releasing diacylglycerol (DAG) and inositol 1,4,5-trisphosphate (IP3)
CC       second messengers that modulate the activity of phosphatidylinositol 3-
CC       kinase and promote the release of Ca(2+) ions from intracellular
CC       stores, respectively (By similarity). Beta-arrestin family members
CC       inhibit signaling via G proteins and mediate activation of alternative
CC       signaling pathways (By similarity). Affects neural activity,
CC       perception, cognition and mood (By similarity). Plays a role in the
CC       regulation of behavior, including responses to anxiogenic situations
CC       and psychoactive substances (By similarity). Plays a role in intestinal
CC       smooth muscle contraction, and may play a role in arterial
CC       vasoconstriction (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P28223,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P35363, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2300586,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2854054}.
CC   -!- ACTIVITY REGULATION: G-protein coupled receptor activity is regulated
CC       by lipids: oleamide increases HTR2A-mediated activity.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P28223}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (via C-terminus) with MPDZ and PATJ. May interact
CC       (via C-terminus) with MPP3, PRDX6, DLG4, DLG1, CASK, APBA1 and MAGI2.
CC       Interacts with GRM2 and DRD2; this may affect signaling.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P28223}.

CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2300586, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2854054}; Multi-pass
CC       membrane protein {ECO:0000305}. Cell projection, axon
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11960784}. Cytoplasmic vesicle
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11960784}. Membrane, caveola
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11960784}. Cell projection, dendrite

CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Detected in adult intestine, especially in mucosal
CC       epithelium, longitudinal and circular layers of muscularis externa and
CC       myenteric plexuses. Highly expressed in Paneth cells, and detected at
CC       lower levels in enterocytes (at protein level). Detected in brain
CC       cortex. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11960784, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2300586}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The PDZ domain-binding motif is involved in the interaction
