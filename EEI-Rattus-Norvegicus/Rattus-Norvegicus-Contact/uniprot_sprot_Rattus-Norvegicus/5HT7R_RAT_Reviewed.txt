CC   -!- FUNCTION: G-protein coupled receptor for 5-hydroxytryptamine
CC       (serotonin), a biogenic hormone that functions as a neurotransmitter, a
CC       hormone and a mitogen (PubMed:8394362, PubMed:8397408, PubMed:8398139).
CC       Ligand binding causes a conformation change that triggers signaling via
CC       guanine nucleotide-binding proteins (G proteins) and modulates the
CC       activity of downstream effectors (PubMed:8394362). HTR7 is coupled to
CC       G(s) G alpha proteins and mediates activation of adenylate cyclase
CC       activity (PubMed:8394362). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8394362,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8397408, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8398139}.
