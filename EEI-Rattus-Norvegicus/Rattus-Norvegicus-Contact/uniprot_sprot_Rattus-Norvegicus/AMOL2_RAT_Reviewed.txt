CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulates the translocation of phosphorylated SRC to
CC       peripheral cell-matrix adhesion sites. Required for proper architecture
CC       of actin filaments. Plays a role in coupling actin fibers to cell
CC       junctions in endothelial cells and is therefore required for correct
CC       endothelial cell morphology via facilitating transcellular transmission
CC       of mechanical force resulting in endothelial cell elongation (By
CC       similarity). Required for the anchoring of radial actin fibers to CDH1
CC       junction complexes at the cell membrane which facilitates organization
CC       of radial actin fiber structure and cellular response to contractile
CC       forces (By similarity). This contributes to maintenance of cell area,
CC       size, shape, epithelial sheet organization and trophectoderm cell
CC       properties that facilitate blastocyst zona hatching (By similarity).
CC       Inhibits the Wnt/beta-catenin signaling pathway, probably by recruiting
CC       CTNNB1 to recycling endosomes and hence preventing its translocation to
CC       the nucleus. Participates in angiogenesis. Activates the Hippo
CC       signaling pathway in response to cell contact inhibition via
CC       interaction with and ubiquitination by Crumbs complex-bound WWP1 (By
CC       similarity). Ubiquitinated AMOTL2 then interacts with LATS2 which in
CC       turn phosphorylates YAP1, excluding it from the nucleus and localizing
CC       it to the cytoplasm and tight junctions, therefore ultimately
CC       repressing YAP1-driven transcription of target genes (By similarity).
CC       Acts to inhibit WWTR1/TAZ transcriptional coactivator activity via
CC       sequestering WWTR1/TAZ in the cytoplasm and at tight junctions (By
CC       similarity). Regulates the size and protein composition of the podosome
CC       cortex and core at myofibril neuromuscular junctions (By similarity).
CC       Selectively promotes FGF-induced MAPK activation through SRC (By
CC       similarity). May play a role in the polarity, proliferation and
CC       migration of endothelial cells. {ECO:0000250,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8K371, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y2J4}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of a complex composed of AMOTL2, MAGI1 and CDH5, within
CC       the complex AMOTL2 acts as a scaffold protein for the interaction of
CC       MAGI1 with CDH5 (By similarity). The complex is required for coupling
CC       actin fibers to cell junctions in endothelial cells (By similarity).
CC       Within the complex AMOTL2 (via its N-terminus) interacts with CDH5 (By
CC       similarity). Interacts (via N-terminus) with MAGI1 (By similarity).
CC       Interacts (via N-terminus) with ACTB; the interaction facilitates
CC       binding of cell junction complexes to actin fibers in endothelial cells
CC       (By similarity). Interacts with CDH1; the interaction may facilitate
CC       binding of radial actin fibers to cell junction complexes (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with SRC (By similarity). Interacts with YAP1;
CC       the interaction is required for ubiquitination of AMOTL2 and
CC       localization of YAP1 to tight junctions (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       WWP1; the interaction facilitates WWP1 interaction with the Crumbs
CC       complex and subsequent WWP1 translocation to the plasma membrane (By
CC       similarity). WWP1 interaction with the Crumbs complex promotes WWP1
CC       monoubiquitination of AMOTL2 which subsequently activates the Hippo
CC       signaling pathway (By similarity). When ubiquitinated interacts with
CC       LATS2 (via UBA domain); the interaction promotes LATS2 phosphorylation
CC       of YAP1 (By similarity). Interacts (via PPXY motif) with WWTR1/TAZ (via
CC       WW domain); the interaction promotes WWTR1/TAZ localization to the
CC       cytoplasm and thereby inhibition of its transcriptional properties (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with PHLDB2; interaction may facilitate PHLDB2
CC       localization to the myotube podosome cortex that surrounds the core (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:A0A8I3QA39,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8K371, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y2J4}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Recycling endosome
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:A1YB07}. Cytoplasm
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y2J4}. Cell projection, podosome
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8K371}. Cell junction
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8K371}.
CC   -!- PTM: Monoubiquitinated at Lys-342 and Lys-403 by Crumbs complex-bound
CC       WWP1 (By similarity). De-ubiquitinated at Lys-342 and Lys-403 by USP9X;
CC       the interaction may be promoted by cell contact inhibition (By
CC       similarity). Deubiquitination of AMOTL2 negatively regulates Hippo
CC       signaling activation (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y2J4}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylation at Tyr-107 is necessary for efficient binding to
CC       SRC and synergistically functioning with SRC to activate the downstream
CC       MAPK pathway. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:A1YB07}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the angiomotin family. {ECO:0000305}.
