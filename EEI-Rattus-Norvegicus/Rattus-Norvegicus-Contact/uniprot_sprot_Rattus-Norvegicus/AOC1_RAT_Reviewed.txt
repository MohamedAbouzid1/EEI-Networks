CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the oxidative deamination of primary amines to the
CC       corresponding aldehydes with the concomitant production of hydrogen
CC       peroxide and ammonia. Its preferred substrates in vitro are the
CC       diamines histamine and 1-methylhistamine and it could therefore play a
CC       role in allergic and immune responses. Has a broad specificity for
CC       diamines and can also act on cadaverine and putrescine, two products of
CC       amino acid catabolism. It could also act on polyamines, like spermidine
CC       and spermine though less efficiently, and regulate various biological
CC       processes. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19801}.
