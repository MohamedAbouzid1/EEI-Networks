CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the oxidative deamination of primary amines to the
CC       corresponding aldehydes with the concomitant production of hydrogen
CC       peroxide and ammonia. Has a preference for the primary monoamines
CC       methylamine and benzylamine (PubMed:15744061). Could also act on 2-
CC       phenylethylamine but much less efficiently. At endothelial cells
CC       surface can also function as a cell adhesion protein that participates
CC       in lymphocyte extravasation and recirculation by mediating the binding
CC       of lymphocytes to peripheral lymph node vascular endothelial cells in
CC       an L-selectin-independent fashion (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q16853, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15744061}.
