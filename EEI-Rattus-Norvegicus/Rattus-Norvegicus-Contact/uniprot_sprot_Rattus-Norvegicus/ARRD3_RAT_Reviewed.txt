CC       expression of adrenergic receptors and probably also other G protein-
CC       coupled receptors. Plays a role in NEDD4-mediated ubiquitination and
CC       endocytosis af activated ADRB2 and subsequent ADRB2 degradation. May
CC       recruit NEDD4 to ADRB2. Alternatively, may function as adapter protein
CC       that does not play a major role in recruiting NEDD4 to ADRB2, but
CC       rather plays a role in a targeting ADRB2 to endosomes.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q96B67}.
