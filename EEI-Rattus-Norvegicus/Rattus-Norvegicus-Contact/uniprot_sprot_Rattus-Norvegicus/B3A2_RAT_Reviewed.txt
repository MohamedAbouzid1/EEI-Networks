CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium-independent anion exchanger which mediates the
CC       electroneutral exchange of chloride for bicarbonate ions across the
CC       cell membrane (PubMed:2371270). Plays an important role in osteoclast
CC       differentiation and function (By similarity). Regulates bone resorption
CC       and calpain-dependent actin cytoskeleton organization in osteoclasts
CC       via anion exchange-dependent control of pH (By similarity). Essential
CC       for intracellular pH regulation in CD8(+) T-cells upon CD3 stimulation,
CC       modulating CD8(+) T-cell responses (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P13808, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2371270}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
