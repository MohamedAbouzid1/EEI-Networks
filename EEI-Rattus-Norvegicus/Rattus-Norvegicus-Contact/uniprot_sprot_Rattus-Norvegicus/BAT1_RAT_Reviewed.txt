CC   -!- FUNCTION: Associates with SLC3A1 to form a functional transporter
CC       complex that mediates the electrogenic exchange between cationic amino
CC       acids and neutral amino acids, with a stoichiometry of 1:1 (By
CC       similarity) (PubMed:10506124). Has system b(0,+)-like activity with
CC       high affinity for extracellular cationic amino acids and L-cystine and
CC       lower affinity for intracellular neutral amino acids (By similarity).
CC       Substrate exchange is driven by high concentration of intracellular
CC       neutral amino acids and the intracellular reduction of L-cystine to L-
CC       cysteine (By similarity). Required for reabsorption of L-cystine and
CC       dibasic amino acids across the brush border membrane in renal proximal
CC       tubules (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P82251,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10506124}.

CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Disulfide-linked heterodimer composed of the catalytic light
CC       chain subunit SLC7A9 and the heavy chain subunit SLC3A1. The
CC       heterodimer is the minimal functional unit. Assembles in
CC       heterotetramers (dimers of heterodimers) and higher order oligomers;
CC       the oligomerization is mediated by SLC3A1 likely to prevent degradation
CC       and facilitate heteromer trafficking to the plasma membrane (By
CC       similarity) (PubMed:10506124). Interacts with CAV1 (PubMed:16358225).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P82251, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10506124,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16358225}.
