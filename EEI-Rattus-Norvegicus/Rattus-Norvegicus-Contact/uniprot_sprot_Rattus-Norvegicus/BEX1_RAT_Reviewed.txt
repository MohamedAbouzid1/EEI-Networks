CC   -!- FUNCTION: Signaling adapter molecule involved in p75NTR/NGFR signaling
CC       (PubMed:16498402). Plays a role in cell cycle progression and neuronal
CC       differentiation (PubMed:16498402). Inhibits neuronal differentiation in
CC       response to nerve growth factor (NGF) (PubMed:16498402). May act as a
CC       link between the cell cycle and neurotrophic factor signaling, possibly
CC       by functioning as an upstream modulator of receptor signaling,
CC       coordinating biological responses to external signals with internal
CC       cellular states (PubMed:16498402). In absence of reductive stress, acts
CC       as a pseudosubstrate for the CRL2(FEM1B) complex: associates with FEM1B
CC       via zinc, thereby preventing association between FEM1B and its
CC       substrates (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9R224,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16498402}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with neurotrophin receptor p75NTR/NGFR
CC       (PubMed:16498402). Interacts with OMP (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9R224, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16498402}.
