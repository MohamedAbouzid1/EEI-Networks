CC   -!- FUNCTION: May be a signaling adapter molecule involved in NGFR/p75NTR-
CC       mediated apoptosis induced by NGF (PubMed:11124986). Plays a role in
CC       zinc-triggered neuronal death (PubMed:11124986). In absence of
CC       reductive stress, acts as a pseudosubstrate for the CRL2(FEM1B)
CC       complex: associates with FEM1B via zinc, thereby preventing association
CC       between FEM1B and its substrates (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9WTZ9, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11124986}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Self-associates. Binds to the DEATH domain of p75NTR/NGFR.

CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9WTZ9}.
