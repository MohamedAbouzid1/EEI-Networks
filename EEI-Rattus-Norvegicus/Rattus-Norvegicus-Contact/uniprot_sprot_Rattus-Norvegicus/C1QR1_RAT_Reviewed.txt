CC   -!- FUNCTION: Cell surface receptor that plays a role in various
CC       physiological processes including inflammation, phagocytosis, and cell
CC       adhesion. Plays a role in phagocytosis and enhances the uptake of
CC       apoptotic cells and immune complexes by acting as a receptor for
CC       defense collagens including surfactant protein A/SFTPA1, C1q, and
CC       mannose-binding lectin (MBL2). Plays a role in the regulation of
CC       endothelial cell function and adhesion by activating angiogenesis.
CC       Mechanistically, exerts its angiogenic function by associating with
CC       beta-dystroglycan, leading to SRC-dependent phosphorylation and
CC       subsequent recruitment of CBL. In turn, CBL provides a docking site for
CC       downstream signaling components, such as CRKL to enhance cell
CC       migration. Participates in angiogenesis also by acting as a receptor
CC       for the ECM pan-endothelial glycoprotein multimerin-2/MMRN2 and IGFBP7
CC       ligands. Both ligands play a non-redundant role in CD93-mediated
CC       endothelial cell function (By similarity). Acts as a key regulator of
CC       endothelial barrier function through modulating VEGFR2 function (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O89103,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NPY3}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Interacts with C1QBP; the association may represent
CC       a cell surface C1q receptor. Interacts with surfactant protein
CC       A/SFTPA1. Interacts with multimerin-2/MMRN2. Interacts with DAG1; this
CC       interaction plays an important role in endothelial cell migration.
CC       Interacts with CBL. Interacts with IGFBP7 (By similarity). Interacts
CC       with VEGFR2 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O89103,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NPY3}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NPY3};
CC       Single-pass type I membrane protein {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NPY3}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Widely expressed. Highly expressed in lung and
CC       heart. Expressed at lower level in brain, thymus, liver, spleen,
CC       intestine, kidney, adrenal gland, muscle and testis. Expressed on
CC       endothelial cells, platelets, undifferentiated monocytes and
CC       circulating natural killer cells.
CC   -!- PTM: N- and O-glycosylated. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NPY3}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylated on Tyr-619 and Tyr-635 by SRC; these
CC       phosphorylations promote endothelial cell adhesion and migration.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NPY3}.
