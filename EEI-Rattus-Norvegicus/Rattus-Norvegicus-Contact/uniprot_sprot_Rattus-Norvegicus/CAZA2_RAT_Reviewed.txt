CC   -!- FUNCTION: F-actin-capping proteins bind in a Ca(2+)-independent manner
CC       to the fast growing ends of actin filaments (barbed end) thereby
CC       blocking the exchange of subunits at these ends. Unlike other capping
CC       proteins (such as gelsolin and severin), these proteins do not sever
CC       actin filaments (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the F-actin capping complex, composed of a
CC       heterodimer of an alpha and a beta subunit. Component of the WASH
CC       complex, composed of F-actin-capping protein subunit alpha (CAPZA1,
CC       CAPZA2 or CAPZA3), F-actin-capping protein subunit beta (CAPZB),
CC       WASHC1, WASHC2, WASHC3, WASHC4 and WASHC5. Interacts with RCSD1/CAPZIP
CC       (By similarity). Directly interacts with CRACD; this interaction
CC       decreases binding to actin (By similarity). {ECO:0000250,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P47755}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the F-actin-capping protein alpha subunit
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
