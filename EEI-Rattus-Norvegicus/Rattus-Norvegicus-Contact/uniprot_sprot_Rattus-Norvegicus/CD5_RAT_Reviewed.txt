CC   -!- FUNCTION: Lymphoid-specific receptor expressed by all T-cells and in a
CC       subset of B-cells known as B1a cells. Plays a role in the regulation of
CC       TCR and BCR signaling, thymocyte selection, T-cell effector
CC       differentiation and immune tolerance. Acts by interacting with several
CC       ligands expressed on B-cells such as CD5L or CD72 and thereby plays an
CC       important role in contact-mediated, T-dependent B-cell activation and
CC       in the maintenance of regulatory T and B-cell homeostasis. Functions as
CC       a negative regulator of TCR signaling during thymocyte development by
CC       associating with several signaling proteins including LCK, CD3Z chain,
CC       PI3K or CBL (By similarity). Mechanistically, co-engagement of CD3 with
CC       CD5 enhances phosphorylated CBL recruitment leading to increased VAV1
CC       phosphorylation and degradation (By similarity). Modulates B-cell
CC       biology through ERK1/2 activation in a Ca(2+)-dependent pathway via the
CC       non-selective Ca(2+) channel TRPC1, leading to IL-10 production (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P06127,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P13379}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with CD72/LYB-2 (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       PTPN6/SHP-1. Interacts with CBL (By similarity). Interacts with CD5L
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P06127,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P13379}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P13379};
CC       Single-pass type I membrane protein {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P13379}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylated on serine, threonine and tyrosine residues
CC       following TCR stimulation. Phosphorylated by LCK on Tyr-449 and Tyr-483
CC       upon TCR engagement. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P06127,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P13379}.
