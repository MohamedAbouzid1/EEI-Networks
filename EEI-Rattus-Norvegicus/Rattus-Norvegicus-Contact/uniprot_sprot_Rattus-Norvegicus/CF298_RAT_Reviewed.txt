CC       outer dynein arm assembly. Seems to be important for initiation rather
CC       than maintenance of cilium motility. Required for correct positioning
CC       of cilia at the apical cell surface, suggesting an additional role in
CC       the planar cell polarity (PCP) pathway. May suppress canonical Wnt
CC       signaling activity. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q6DRC3}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with ZMYND10. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P57076}.
