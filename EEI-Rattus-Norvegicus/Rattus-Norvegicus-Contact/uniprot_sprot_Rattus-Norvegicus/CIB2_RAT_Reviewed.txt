CC   -!- FUNCTION: Calcium- and integrin-binding protein that plays a role in
CC       intracellular calcium homeostasis (By similarity). Acts as an auxiliary
CC       subunit of the sensory mechanoelectrical transduction (MET) channel in
CC       hair cells (By similarity). Essential for mechanoelectrical
CC       transduction (MET) currents in auditory hair cells and thereby required
CC       for hearing (By similarity). Regulates the function of hair cell
CC       mechanotransduction by controlling the distribution of transmembrane
CC       channel-like proteins TMC1 and TMC2, and by regulating the function of
CC       the MET channels in hair cells (By similarity). Required for the
CC       maintenance of auditory hair cell stereocilia bundle morphology and
CC       function and for hair-cell survival in the cochlea (By similarity).
CC       Critical for proper photoreceptor cell maintenance and function (By
CC       similarity). Plays a role in intracellular calcium homeostasis by
CC       decreasing ATP-induced calcium release (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O75838, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z309}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Monomer (By similarity). Homodimer (By similarity). Interacts
CC       with WHRN and MYO7A (By similarity). Interacts with ITGA2B (via C-
CC       terminus cytoplasmic tail region); the interactions are
CC       stabilized/increased in a calcium and magnesium-dependent manner (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with ITGA7 (via C-terminus cytoplasmic tail
CC       region); the interactions are stabilized/increased in a calcium and
CC       magnesium-dependent manner (By similarity). Interacts with TMC1 (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with TMC2 (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O75838, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z309}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z309}. Cell
CC       projection, stereocilium {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O75838}. Photoreceptor
CC       inner segment {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z309}. Cell projection, cilium,
CC       photoreceptor outer segment {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z309}. Cell
CC       membrane, sarcolemma {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z309}. Note=Colocalizes
CC       with ITGA7 at the myotendinous junctions (MTJ) and at the neuromuscular
CC       junctions (NMJ) (By similarity). Located mainly in stereocilia and at
CC       the apical surface of hair cells of the cochlea (By similarity).
CC       Localizes in the cuticular plate along and at the tip of the
CC       stereocilia of vestibular sensory hair cells (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O75838, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z309}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Expressed in auditory hair cells at P10 (at
CC       protein level). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28663585}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: The binding of either calcium or magnesium significantly
CC       increases the structural stability of the protein in comparison to apo-
CC       CIB (calcium- and magnesium-free form). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O75838}.
