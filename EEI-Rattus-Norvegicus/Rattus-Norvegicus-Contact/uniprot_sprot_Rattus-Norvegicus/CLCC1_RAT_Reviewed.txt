CC   -!- FUNCTION: Anion-selective channel with Ca(2+)-dependent and voltage-
CC       independent gating. Permeable to small monovalent anions with
CC       selectivity for bromide > chloride > nitrate > fluoride (By
CC       similarity). Operates in the endoplasmic reticulum (ER) membrane where
CC       it mediates chloride efflux to compensate for the loss of positive
CC       charges from the ER lumen upon Ca(2+) release. Contributes to the
CC       maintenance of ER Ca(2+) pools and activation of unfolded protein
CC       response to prevent accumulation of misfolded proteins in the ER lumen.
CC       Particularly involved in ER homeostasis mechanisms underlying motor
CC       neurons and retinal photoreceptors survival (By similarity)
CC       (PubMed:11279057). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q96S66,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99LI2, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11279057}.
