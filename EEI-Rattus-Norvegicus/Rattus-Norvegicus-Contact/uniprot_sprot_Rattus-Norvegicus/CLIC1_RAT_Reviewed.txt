CC   -!- FUNCTION: In the soluble state, catalyzes glutaredoxin-like thiol
CC       disulfide exchange reactions with reduced glutathione as electron
CC       donor. Reduces selenite and dehydroascorbate and may act as an
CC       antioxidant during oxidative stress response (By similarity). Can
CC       insert into membranes and form voltage-dependent multi-ion conductive
CC       channels. Membrane insertion seems to be redox-regulated and may occur
CC       only under oxidizing conditions. Involved in regulation of the cell
CC       cycle (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O00299}.
