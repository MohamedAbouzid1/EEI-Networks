CC   -!- FUNCTION: In the soluble state, catalyzes glutaredoxin-like thiol
CC       disulfide exchange reactions with reduced glutathione as electron donor
CC       (By similarity). Can insert into membranes and form non-selective ion
CC       channels almost equally permeable to Na(+), K(+) and Cl(-) (By
CC       similarity). Required for normal hearing (By similarity). It is
CC       necessary for the formation of stereocilia in the inner ear and normal
CC       development of the organ of Corti (By similarity). May play a role in
CC       the regulation of transepithelial ion absorption and secretion. Is
CC       required for the development and/or maintenance of the proper
CC       glomerular endothelial cell and podocyte architecture (By similarity).
CC       Plays a role in formation of the lens suture in the eye, which is
CC       important for normal optical properties of the lens (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O00299, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BXK9,
