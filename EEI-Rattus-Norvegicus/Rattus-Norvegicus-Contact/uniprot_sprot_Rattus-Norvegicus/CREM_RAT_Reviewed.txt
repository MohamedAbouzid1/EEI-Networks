CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcriptional regulator that binds the cAMP response
CC       element (CRE), a sequence present in many viral and cellular promoters.
CC       Isoforms are either transcriptional activators or repressors. Isoform
CC       Delta is an activator. Plays a role in spermatogenesis and is involved
CC       in spermatid maturation. Binding of isoform Tau (activator) to CRE is
CC       increased by CREB3L4. The CREM isoform Tau-CREB3L4 heterodimer
CC       functions through CRE and may recruit HIRA to CRE to regulate histone
CC       exchange (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Binds DNA as a dimer. Interacts with CDC34. Interacts with
CC       FHL5. Isoform delta forms a heterodimer with CREB3L4. May interact with
CC       TSSK4. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P27699, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q03060}.
