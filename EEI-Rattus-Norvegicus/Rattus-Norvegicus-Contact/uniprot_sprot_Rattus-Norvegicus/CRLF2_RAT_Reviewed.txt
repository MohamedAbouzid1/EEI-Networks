CC   -!- FUNCTION: Receptor for thymic stromal lymphopoietin (TSLP). Forms a
CC       functional complex with TSLP and IL7R which is capable of stimulating
CC       cell proliferation through activation of STAT3 and STAT5 (By
CC       similarity). Also activates JAK2 (By similarity). Implicated in the
CC       development of the hematopoietic system. {ECO:0000250,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11891057}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Heterodimer of CRLF2 and IL7R.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Membrane {ECO:0000305}; Single-pass type I
CC       membrane protein {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in all tissues examined including brain,
CC       thymus, lung, heart, muscle, stomach, small intestine, liver, kidney,
CC       spleen, testis and skin. Highest levels in thymus, liver and testis.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11891057}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The WSXWS motif appears to be necessary for proper protein
CC       folding and thereby efficient intracellular transport and cell-surface
CC       receptor binding.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The box 1 motif is required for JAK interaction and/or
CC       activation.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the type I cytokine receptor family. Type 5
CC       subfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
