CC   -!- FUNCTION: Postsynaptic adhesion molecule that binds to presynaptic
CC       neurexins to mediate both excitatory and inhibitory synapse formation.
CC       Promotes synapse development by acting as a cell adhesion molecule at
CC       the postsynaptic membrane, which associates with both neurexin-alpha
CC       and neurexin-beta proteins at the presynaptic membrane. Regulates the
CC       balance between excitatory and inhibitory synapses by inhibiting
CC       formation of excitatory parallel-fiber synapses and promoting formation
CC       of inhibitory synapses in the same neuron. May also be involved in
CC       ascorbate (vitamin C) uptake via its interaction with SLC23A2/SVCT2 (By
CC       similarity). Complex formation with APBA2 and APP, stabilizes APP
CC       metabolism and enhances APBA2-mediated suppression of beta-APP40
CC       secretion, due to the retardation of intracellular APP maturation (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99JH7,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BQT9}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Isoform CLSTN3beta]: Adipose-specific isoform that plays a
CC       key role in adaptive thermogenesis. Facilitates the efficient use of
CC       stored triglyceride by promoting multilocular morphology of thermogenic
CC       adipocytes: acts by inhibiting the activity of CIDEA and CIDEC on lipid
CC       droplets, thereby preventing lipid droplet fusion and facilitating
CC       lipid utilization. May also participate in adaptive thermogenesis by
CC       promoting sympathetic innervation of thermogenic adipose tissue: acts
CC       by driving secretion of neurotrophic factor S100B from brown
CC       adipocytes, stimulating neurite outgrowth from sympathetic neurons.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99JH7}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (via cadherin domains) with both alpha and beta
CC       isoforms of neurexins (NRXN1, NRXN2 and NRXN3) (By similarity).
CC       Directly interacts with APBA2. Forms a tripartite complex with APBA2
CC       and APP (By similarity). Interacts with low affinity with KLC1 (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with SLC23A2/SVCT2 (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99JH7, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BQT9}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: [Isoform CLSTN3beta]: Interacts with CIDEA; inhibiting the
CC       lipid transferase activity of CIDEA. Interacts with CIDEC; inhibiting
CC       the lipid transferase activity of CIDEC.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99JH7}.

CC       {ECO:0000255}. Endoplasmic reticulum membrane

CC       {ECO:0000255}. Golgi apparatus membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99JH7};
CC       Single-pass type I membrane protein {ECO:0000255}. Cell projection,

CC       postsynaptic specializations of asymmetric (type I) synapses with both
CC       axodendritic and axospinous localization.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99JH7}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: [Isoform CLSTN3beta]: Lipid droplet

CC       with APBA2 and APP, which seems to prevent the association with gamma-
CC       secretase. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BQT9}.
