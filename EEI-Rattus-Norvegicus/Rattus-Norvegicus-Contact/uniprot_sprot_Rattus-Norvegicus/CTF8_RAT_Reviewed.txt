CC   -!- FUNCTION: Chromosome cohesion factor involved in sister chromatid
CC       cohesion and fidelity of chromosome transmission. Component of one of
CC       the cell nuclear antigen loader complexes, CTF18-replication factor C
CC       (CTF18-RFC), which consists of CTF18, CTF8, DSCC1, RFC2, RFC3, RFC4 and
CC       RFC5. The CTF18-RFC complex binds to single-stranded and primed DNAs
CC       and has weak ATPase activity that is stimulated the presence of primed
CC       DNA, replication protein A (RPA) and proliferating cell nuclear antigen
CC       (PCNA). The CTF18-RFC complex catalyzes the ATP-dependent loading of
CC       PCNA onto primed and gapped DNA. It also interacts with and stimulates
CC       POLH, which is suggestive of a protein network that coordinates DNA
CC       repair, recombination and chromosome cohesion reactions with
CC       replication fork progression (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P0CG13}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the CTF18-RFC complex, which consists of CTF18,
CC       CTF8, DSCC1, RFC2, RFC3, RFC4 and RFC5. The CTF18-RFC complex does not
CC       interact with the Rad9/Rad1/Hus1 complex. The CTF18-RFC complex
CC       interacts with POLH. CTF18/CTF8/DSCC1 associate with PCNA. CTF8 exists
CC       as a dimer with DSCC1 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P0CG13}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P0CG13}.
CC       Note=Associates with chromatin during S phase.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P0CG13}.
