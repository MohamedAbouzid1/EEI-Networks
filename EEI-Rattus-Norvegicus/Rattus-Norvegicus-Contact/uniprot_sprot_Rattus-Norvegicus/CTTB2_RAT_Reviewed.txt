CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulates the dendritic spine distribution of CTTN/cortactin
CC       in hippocampal neurons, and thus controls dendritic spinogenesis and
CC       dendritic spine maintenance (PubMed:22262902). Associates with the
CC       striatin-interacting phosphatase and kinase (STRIPAK) core complex to
CC       regulate dendritic spine distribution of the STRIPAK complex in
CC       hippocampal neurons (PubMed:23015759). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22262902,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23015759}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with CTTN/cortactin SH3 domain (PubMed:22262902).
CC       Interacts with STRN, STRN4/zinedin and MOB4/phocein; this interactions
CC       mediate the association with the STRIPAK core complex and may regulate
CC       dendritic spine distribution of the STRIPAK complex in hippocampal
CC       neurons. Activation of glutamate receptors weakens the interaction with
CC       STRN and STRN4 (PubMed:23015759). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22262902,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23015759}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm, cell cortex
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:B9EJA2}. Cell projection, dendritic spine
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22262902}. Note=Remains associated with dendritic
CC       spines even after glutamate stimulation. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22262902}.
