CC   -!- FUNCTION: Protein kinase activator that acts as a key activator of the
CC       integrated stress response (ISR) following various stresses, such as
CC       iron deficiency, mitochondrial stress or mitochondrial DNA breaks.
CC       Detects impaired protein import and processing in mitochondria,
CC       activating the ISR. May also required for the induction of death
CC       receptor-mediated apoptosis through the regulation of caspase
CC       activation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: [DAP3-binding cell death enhancer 1]: Protein kinase
CC       activator that activates the ISR in response to iron deficiency: iron
CC       deficiency impairs mitochondrial import, promoting DELE1 localization
CC       at the mitochondrial surface, where it binds and activates EIF2AK1/HRI
CC       to trigger the ISR. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: [DAP3-binding cell death enhancer 1 short form]: Protein
CC       kinase activator generated by protein cleavage in response to
CC       mitochondrial stress, which accumulates in the cytosol and specifically
CC       binds to and activates the protein kinase activity of EIF2AK1/HRI (By
CC       similarity). It thereby activates the integrated stress response (ISR):
CC       EIF2AK1/HRI activation promotes eIF-2-alpha (EIF2S1) phosphorylation,
CC       leading to a decrease in global protein synthesis and the induction of
CC       selected genes, including the transcription factor ATF4, the master
CC       transcriptional regulator of the ISR (By similarity). Also acts as an
CC       activator of PRKN-independent mitophagy: activates the protein kinase
CC       activity of EIF2AK1/HRI in response to mitochondrial damage, promoting
CC       eIF-2-alpha (EIF2S1) phosphorylation, leading to mitochondrial
CC       localization of EIF2S1 followed by induction of mitophagy (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with DAP3. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: [DAP3-binding cell death enhancer 1]: Interacts (via TPR
CC       repeats) with EIF2AK1/HRI; activating the protein kinase activity of
CC       EIF2AK1/HRI, thereby promoting the integrated stress response (ISR).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: [DAP3-binding cell death enhancer 1 short form]: Homooctamer;
CC       oligomerization is required to activate EIF2AK1/HRI. Interacts (via TPR
CC       repeats) with EIF2AK1/HRI; activating the protein kinase activity of
CC       EIF2AK1/HRI, thereby promoting the integrated stress response (ISR).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: [DAP3-binding cell death enhancer 1]:
CC       Mitochondrion {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}. Mitochondrion outer
CC       membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}. Mitochondrion inner membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}. Note=Imported in the mitochondrial
CC       matrix in absence of stress, leading to its degradation by LONP1.
CC       Localizes at the mitochondrial surface in response to iron deficiency:
CC       iron deficiency impairs mitochondrial import, promoting localization at
CC       the mitochondrial surface and stabilization. Associates with the
CC       mitochondrion inner membrane in response to mitochondrial stress,
CC       leading to its proteolytic processing by OMA1, and generation of the
CC       AP3-binding cell death enhancer 1 short form (DELE1(S) or S-DELE1).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: [DAP3-binding cell death enhancer 1 short form]:
CC       Cytoplasm, cytosol {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}. Note=This short form
CC       is generated by proteolytic processing by OMA1 in response to
CC       mitochondrial stress, leading to translocation to the cytosol.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The TPR repeats bind to and activate EIF2AK1/HRI.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- PTM: [DAP3-binding cell death enhancer 1]: Unstable protein in absence
CC       of stress: imported in the mitochondrial matrix following processing by
CC       the mitochondrial-processing peptidase (MPP), where it is degraded by
CC       LONP1. Stabilized in response to iron deficiency: iron deficiency
CC       impairs mitochondrial import, promoting localization at the
CC       mitochondrial surface and stabilization. Cleaved by OMA1 in response to
CC       mitochondrial stress, generating the DAP3-binding cell death enhancer 1
CC       short form (DELE1(S) or S-DELE1) that accumulates in the cytosol and
CC       activates the protein kinase activity of EIF2AK1/HRI. Protein cleavage
CC       by OMA1 can take place at different positions, and apparently does not
CC       require a specific sequence motif. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- PTM: [DAP3-binding cell death enhancer 1 short form]: Ubiquitinated and
CC       degraded by the SIFI complex once the mitochondrial stress has been
CC       resolved, thereby providing stress response silencing. Within the SIFI
CC       complex, UBR4 initiates ubiquitin chain that are further elongated or
CC       branched by KCMF1. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14154}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the DELE1 family. {ECO:0000305}.
