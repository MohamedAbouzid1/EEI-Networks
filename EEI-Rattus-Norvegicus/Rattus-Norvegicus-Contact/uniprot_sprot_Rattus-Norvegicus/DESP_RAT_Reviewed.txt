CC       MAPK14/p38 MAPK signaling cascade and increase in TGFB1 protein
CC       abundance (PubMed:26858265). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:26858265}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Interacts with COL17A1 (via cytoplasmic region) (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with DSC2 (By similarity). Interacts with PKP1
CC       (By similarity). Interacts with PKP2 (By similarity). Interacts weakly
CC       with TMEM65 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:E9Q557,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P15924}.

CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P15924}. Cell membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:E9Q557}. Cytoplasm
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P15924}. Note=Localizes to desmosome precursor
CC       particles in the cytoplasm. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:E9Q557,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P15924}.
