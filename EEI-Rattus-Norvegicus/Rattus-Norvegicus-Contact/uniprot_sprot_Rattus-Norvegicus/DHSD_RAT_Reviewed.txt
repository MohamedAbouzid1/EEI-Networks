CC   -!- FUNCTION: Membrane-anchoring subunit of succinate dehydrogenase (SDH)
CC       that is involved in complex II of the mitochondrial electron transport
CC       chain and is responsible for transferring electrons from succinate to
CC       ubiquinone (coenzyme Q) (By similarity). SDH also oxidizes malate to
CC       the non-canonical enol form of oxaloacetate, enol-oxaloacetate. Enol-
CC       oxaloacetate, which is a potent inhibitor of the succinate
CC       dehydrogenase activity, is further isomerized into keto-oxaloacetate
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O14521,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q95123}.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Carbohydrate metabolism; tricarboxylic acid cycle.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O14521}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of complex II composed of four subunits: the
CC       flavoprotein (FP) SDHA, iron-sulfur protein (IP) SDHB, and a cytochrome
CC       b560 composed of SDHC and SDHD. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O14521}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Mitochondrion inner membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O14521}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000255}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the CybS family. {ECO:0000305}.
