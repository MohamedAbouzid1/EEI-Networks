CC   -!- FUNCTION: Heterotetrameric enzyme that catalyzes the condensation of
CC       farnesyl diphosphate (FPP), which acts as a primer, and isopentenyl
CC       diphosphate (IPP) to produce prenyl diphosphates of varying chain
CC       lengths and participates in the determination of the side chain of
CC       ubiquinone. Supplies nona and decaprenyl diphosphate, the precursors
CC       for the side chain of the isoprenoid quinones ubiquinone-9 (Q9) and
CC       ubiquinone-10 (Q10) respectively. The enzyme adds isopentenyl
CC       diphosphate molecules sequentially to farnesyl diphosphate with trans
CC       stereochemistry (By similarity). May play a role during cerebellar
CC       development (By similarity). May regulate mitochondrial respiratory
CC       chain function (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q33DR3,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q86YH6}.
