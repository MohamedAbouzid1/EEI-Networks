CC   -!- FUNCTION: Plays a role as a transcriptional activator or repressor.
CC       Inhibits several cytokine signaling pathways, such as TGFB1, activin-
CC       A/INHBA and BMP4 by interfering with the transcriptional stimulatory
CC       activity of transcription factors, such as MSX2, FAST2, SMAD2 and SMAD3
CC       during hematopoietic cell differentiation. Plays a role in terminal
CC       differentiation of interneurons, such as amacrine and bipolar cells in
CC       the developing retina. Likely to play a regulatory role in the
CC       development of the ventral forebrain. May play a role in craniofacial
CC       patterning and morphogenesis and may be involved in the early
CC       development of diencephalic subdivisions.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56177, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q64317}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with SMAD4 (via homeobox DNA-binding domain).
CC       Interacts (via homeobox DNA-binding domain) with POU4F2; this
CC       interaction suppresses DLX1-mediated transcriptional activity in
CC       postnatal retina and enhances retinal ganglion cell (RGC)
CC       differentiation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56177,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q64317}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56177}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The homeobox DNA-binding domain is necessary for its nuclear
CC       localization, transcriptional and erythroid differentiation activities.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56177}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the distal-less homeobox family. {ECO:0000305}.
