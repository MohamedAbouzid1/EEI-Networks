CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of a nucleotide salvage pathway that eliminates
CC       epigenetically modified 5-hydroxymethyl-dCMP (hmdCMP) in a two-step
CC       process entailing deamination to cytotoxic 5-hydroxymethyl-dUMP
CC       (hmdUMP), followed by its hydrolysis into 5-hydroxymethyluracil (hmU)
CC       and 2-deoxy-D-ribose 5-phosphate (deoxyribosephosphate). Catalyzes the
CC       second step in that pathway, the hydrolysis of the N-glycosidic bond in
CC       hmdUMP, degrading this cytotoxic nucleotide to avoid its genomic
CC       integration. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O43598}.
