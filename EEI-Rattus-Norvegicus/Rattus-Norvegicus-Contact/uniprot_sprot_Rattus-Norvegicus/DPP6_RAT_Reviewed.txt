CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulatory subunit of Kv4/D (Shal)-type voltage-gated rapidly

CC       PubMed:16123112, PubMed:19279261, PubMed:19901547). Modulates the
CC       activity and gating characteristics of the potassium channel KCND2 amd
CC       KCND3 (PubMed:12575952, PubMed:16123112, PubMed:19279261,
CC       PubMed:19901547). Promotes cell surface expression of the potassium
CC       channel KCND2 (PubMed:12575952). Has no dipeptidyl aminopeptidase
CC       activity (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P42658,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12575952, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16123112,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19279261, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19901547}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer (in vitro) (By similarity). Interacts with KCND2
CC       (PubMed:12575952). Identified in a complex with KCND2 and KCNIP2. Forms
CC       an octameric complex composed of four DPP6 subunits bound to the KCND2
CC       tetramer (By similarity). Interacts with KCND3; this interaction
CC       modulates the channel gating kinetics namely channel activation and
CC       inactivation kinetics and rate of recovery from inactivation (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P42658,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z218, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12575952}.
