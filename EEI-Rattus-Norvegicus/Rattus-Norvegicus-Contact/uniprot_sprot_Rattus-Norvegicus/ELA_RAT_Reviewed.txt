CC   -!- FUNCTION: Endogenous ligand for the apelin receptor (APLNR)
CC       (PubMed:28137936). Hormone required for mesendodermal differentiation,
CC       blood vessels formation and heart morphogenesis during early
CC       development and for adult cardiovascular homeostasis (PubMed:26611206,
CC       PubMed:28137936). Drives internalization of APLNR. Acts as a motogen by
CC       promoting mesendodermal cell migration during gastrulation by binding
CC       and activating APLNR. Acts as an early embryonic regulator of cellular
CC       movement with a role in migration and development of cardiac progenitor
CC       cells. May act as a chemoattractant for the activation of angioblast
CC       migration toward the embryonic midline, i.e. the position of the future
CC       vessel formation, during vasculogenesis. Positively regulates sinus
CC       venosus (SV)-derived endothelial cells migration into the developing
CC       heart to promote coronary blood vessel sprouting. Plays a role in
CC       placental vascular development; promotes placental trophoblast invasion
CC       and spiral artery remodeling in the uterus (By similarity). Involved in
CC       the regulation of maternal cardiovascular homeostasis to prevent
CC       gestational hypertension and for potent cardioprotective functions
CC       during heart failure (PubMed:26611206). Mediates myocardial
CC       contractility in an ERK1/2-dependent manner (PubMed:26611206).
