CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: At P02, in the organ of Corti, expressed in the
CC       stereocilia of outer hair cells and in the kinocilia of both outer and
CC       inner hair cells. Also concentrated at the cuticular plate level of
CC       auditory hair cells. However, before P07, expression in the inner hair
CC       cell stereocilia is very weak. By P07, in auditory hair cells, patchy
CC       expression detected along the length of stereocilia, but absent from
CC       the very tip. Later in development, detected in the stereocilia bundles
CC       and microvilli of sensory cells and also in the supporting cell bodies
CC       (at protein level).
