CC   -!- FUNCTION: SIII, also known as elongin, is a general transcription
CC       elongation factor that increases the RNA polymerase II transcription
CC       elongation past template-encoded arresting sites. Subunit A is
CC       transcriptionally active and its transcription activity is strongly
CC       enhanced by binding to the dimeric complex of the SIII regulatory
CC       subunits B and C (elongin BC complex) (By similarity). In embryonic
CC       stem cells, the elongin BC complex is recruited by EPOP to Polycomb
CC       group (PcG) target genes in order generate genomic region that display
CC       both active and repressive chromatin properties, an important feature
CC       of pluripotent stem cells (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P62869, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q15370}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: Core component of multiple cullin-2 and cullin-5-RING E3
CC       ubiquitin-protein ligase complexes (ECS complexes), which mediate the
CC       ubiquitination of target proteins. By binding to BC-box motifs it seems
CC       to link target recruitment subunits, like VHL and members of the SOCS
CC       box family, to Cullin/RBX1 modules that activate E2 ubiquitination
CC       enzymes. Component the von Hippel-Lindau ubiquitination complex
CC       CBC(VHL). A number of ECS complexes (containing either KLHDC2, KLHDC3,
CC       KLHDC10, APPBP2, FEM1A, FEM1B or FEM1C as substrate-recognition
CC       component) are part of the DesCEND (destruction via C-end degrons)
CC       pathway, which recognizes a C-degron located at the extreme C terminus
CC       of target proteins, leading to their ubiquitination and degradation.
CC       The ECS(ASB9) complex mediates ubiquitination and degradation of CKB.
CC       As part of a multisubunit ubiquitin ligase complex, polyubiquitinates
CC       monoubiquitinated POLR2A (By similarity). ECS(LRR1) ubiquitinates MCM7
CC       and promotes CMG replisome disassembly by VCP and chromatin extraction
CC       during S-phase (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P62869,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q15370}.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Protein modification; protein ubiquitination.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q15370}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Heterotrimer of an A (ELOA, ELOA2 or ELOA3P), ELOB and ELOC
CC       subunit (By similarity). The elongin BC complex interacts with EPOP;
CC       leading to recruit the elongin BC complex to Polycomb group (PcG)
CC       target genes, thereby restricting excessive activity of the PRC2/EED-
CC       EZH2 complex (By similarity). Component of multiple cullin-RING E3
CC       ubiquitin-protein ligase complexes composed of Elongin BC (ELOB and
CC       ELOC), a cullin (either CUL2 or CUL5), a catalytic subunit (either RBX1
CC       or RNF7/RBX2), as well as a substrate adapter protein that can be
CC       either ASB2, ASB9, ASB11, KLHDC2, KLHDC3, KLHDC10, APPBP2, FEM1A,
CC       FEM1B, FEM1C, LRR1, PCMTD1, SOCS1, SOCS2, SOCS5, SPSB1, SPSB3, ELOA,
CC       VHL or WSB1. As part of the Elongin BC E3 ubiquitin ligase complex;
CC       interacts with NRBP1. May also interact with DCUN1D1, DCUN1D2, DCUN1D3
CC       and DCUN1D5 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P62869,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q15370}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q15370}.
