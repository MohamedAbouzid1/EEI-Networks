CC   -!- FUNCTION: ATP-dependent 3'-5' DNA helicase/translocase; binds dsDNA
CC       rather than ssDNA, unzipping it in a translocase rather than classical
CC       helicase activity. Component of the general transcription and DNA
CC       repair factor IIH (TFIIH) core complex. When complexed to CDK-
CC       activating kinase (CAK), involved in RNA transcription by RNA
CC       polymerase II. The ATPase activity of XPB/ERCC3, but not its helicase
CC       activity, is required for DNA opening; it may wrap around the damaged
CC       DNA wedging it open, causing localized melting and twisting that allows
CC       XPD/ERCC2 helicase to anchor. The ATP-dependent helicase activity of
CC       XPB/ERCC3 may be required for promoter escape. Also involved in
CC       transcription-coupled nucleotide excision repair (NER) of damaged DNA.
CC       In NER, TFIIH acts by opening DNA around the lesion to allow the
CC       excision of the damaged oligonucleotide and its replacement by a new
CC       DNA fragment. The structure of the TFIIH transcription complex differs
CC       from the NER-TFIIH complex; large movements by XPD/ERCC2 and XPB/ERCC3
CC       are stabilized by XPA. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19447}.

CC   -!- ACTIVITY REGULATION: Phosphorylation on Ser-751 by CK2 controls the 5'-
CC       excision activity of ERCC1-XPF endonuclease; phosphorylated protein
CC       inhibits the excision activity and thus NER. ATPase activity is
CC       stimulated by TFIIH subunit p52 (GTF2H4). DNA translocase activity by
CC       this subunit in TFIIH is stimulated by XPA, ERCC5/XPG and XFP plus
CC       ERCC1. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19447}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the 7-subunit TFIIH core complex composed of
CC       XPB/ERCC3, XPD/ERCC2, GTF2H1, GTF2H2, GTF2H3, GTF2H4 and GTF2H5, which
CC       is active in NER. The core complex associates with the 3-subunit CDK-
CC       activating kinase (CAK) module composed of CCNH/cyclin H, CDK7 and
CC       MNAT1 to form the 10-subunit holoenzyme (holo-TFIIH) active in
CC       transcription. Interacts with PUF60. Interacts with ATF7IP. Interacts
CC       with KAT2A; leading to KAT2A recruitment to promoters and acetylation
CC       of histones. Part of TBP-based Pol II pre-initiation complex (PIC), in
CC       which Pol II core assembles with general transcription factors and
CC       other specific initiation factors including GTF2E1, GTF2E2, GTF2F1,
CC       GTF2F2, TCEA1, ERCC2, ERCC3, GTF2H2, GTF2H3, GTF2H4, GTF2H5, GTF2A1,
CC       GTF2A2, GTF2B and TBP; this large multi-subunit PIC complex mediates
CC       DNA unwinding and targets Pol II core to the transcription start site
CC       where the first phosphodiester bond forms.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19447}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylation on Ser-751 by CK2 controls the 5'-excision
CC       activity of ERCC1-XPF endonuclease; phosphorylated protein inhibits the
CC       excision activity and thus NER. Dephosphorylation reactivates the 5'-
CC       excision step. Phosphorylation has no effect on transcription or the
CC       3'-5' helicase activity. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19447}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the helicase family. RAD25/XPB subfamily.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
