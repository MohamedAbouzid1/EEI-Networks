CC   -!- FUNCTION: Proposed to be involved in regulation of apoptosis; the exact
CC       mechanism may differ between cell types/tissues. May be involved in
CC       hypoxia-induced cell death of transformed cells implicating cytochrome
CC       C release and caspase activation (such as CASP9) and inducing
CC       mitochondrial permeability transition. May be involved in hypoxia-
CC       induced cell death of neuronal cells probably by promoting release of
CC       AIFM1 from mitochondria to cytoplasm and its translocation to the
CC       nucleus; however, the involvement of caspases has been reported
CC       conflictingly. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9D6U8}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with HSP90AB1; HSP90AB1 is essential for FAM162A
CC       mitochondrial localization and pro-apoptotic activity. Interacts with
CC       VDAC2; the interaction is probably involved in inducing mitochondrial
CC       permeability transition. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q96A26}.
