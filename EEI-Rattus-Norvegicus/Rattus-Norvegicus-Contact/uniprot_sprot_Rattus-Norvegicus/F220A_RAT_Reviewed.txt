CC       promotes interaction of PTPN2 with STAT3 and mediates STAT3
CC       dephosphorylation by PTPN2, leading to negative regulation of STAT3

CC       or sperm function. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3ZN08}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with transcriptional activator STAT3; the
CC       interaction occurs in both the nucleus and the cytoplasm, is enhanced
CC       by IL6 and promotes STAT3 dephosphorylation, leading to negative
CC       regulation of STAT3 transcriptional activator activity. Can interact
CC       with both unphosphorylated and phosphorylated STAT3 but interacts
CC       preferentially with phosphorylated STAT3 in the nucleus. Interacts with
CC       protein phosphatase PTPN2/TC45; this promotes interaction of PTPN2 with
CC       STAT3, leading to dephosphorylation of STAT3 by PTPN2.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3ZN08}.
