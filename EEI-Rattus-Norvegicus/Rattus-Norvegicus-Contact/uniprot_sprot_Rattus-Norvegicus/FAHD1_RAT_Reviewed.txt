CC   -!- FUNCTION: Tautomerase that converts enol-oxaloacetate, a strong
CC       inhibitor of succinate dehydrogenase, to the physiological keto form of
CC       oxaloacetate. It is thereby required to maximize aerobic respiration
CC       efficiency by preventing succinate dehydrogenase inhibition. Also acts
CC       as a weak oxaloacetate decarboxylase (ODx), catalyzing the
CC       decarboxylation of oxaloacetate (OAA) to pyruvate and CO(2), and as
CC       such is likely a regulatory enzyme in the TCA cycle. Also displays
CC       acylpyruvase activity, being able to hydrolyze acetylpyruvate and
CC       fumarylpyruvate in vitro. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q6P587}.
