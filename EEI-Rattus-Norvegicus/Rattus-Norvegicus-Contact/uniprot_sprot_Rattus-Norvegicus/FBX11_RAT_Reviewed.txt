CC   -!- FUNCTION: Substrate recognition component of a SCF (SKP1-CUL1-F-box
CC       protein) E3 ubiquitin-protein ligase complex which mediates the
CC       ubiquitination and subsequent proteasomal degradation of target
CC       proteins, such as DTL/CDT2, BCL6, SNAI1 and PRDM1/BLIMP1. The
CC       SCF(FBXO11) complex mediates ubiquitination and degradation of BCL6,
CC       thereby playing a role in the germinal center B-cells terminal
CC       differentiation toward memory B-cells and plasma cells. The SCF(FBXO11)
CC       complex also mediates ubiquitination and degradation of DTL, an
CC       important step for the regulation of TGF-beta signaling, cell migration
CC       and the timing of the cell-cycle progression and exit. The SCF(FBXO11)
CC       complex also catalyzes ubiquitination and degradation of GSK3B-
CC       phosphorylated SNAI1. Binds to and neddylates phosphorylated p53/TP53,
CC       inhibiting its transcriptional activity. Plays a role in the regulatiom
CC       of erythropoiesis but not myelopoiesis or megakaryopoiesis.
CC       Mechanistically, activates erythroid genes by mediating the degradation
CC       of BAHD1, a heterochromatin-associated protein that recruits
CC       corepressors to H3K27me3 marks. Participates in macrophage cell death
CC       and inflammation in response to bacterial toxins by regulating the
CC       expression of complement 5a receptor 1/C5AR1 and IL-1beta. Acts as a
CC       critical regulator to determine the level of MHC-II by mediating the
CC       recognition of degron at the P/S/T domain of CIITA leading to its
CC       ubiquitination and subsequent degradation via the proteasome.
CC       Participates in the antiviral repsonse by initiating the activation of
CC       TBK1-IRF3-IFN-I axis. Mediates the 'Lys-63'-linked ubiquitination of
CC       TRAF3 to strengthen the interaction between TRAF3 and TBK1.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q86XK2}.
