CC   -!- FUNCTION: High-affinity Fc receptor for immunoglobulin M (IgM), both
CC       secreted and membrane-bound IgM (By similarity). Primarily regulates
CC       IgM transport and homeostasis. In lymphoid cells, enables exocytosis of
CC       membrane-bound IgM on the plasma membrane as well as endocytosis of
CC       IgM-antigen complexes toward lysosomes for degradation. In mucosal
CC       epithelium, mediates retrotranscytosis of antigen-IgM complexes across
CC       mucosal M cells toward antigen-presenting cells in mucosal lymphoid
CC       tissues (By similarity). Triggers costimulatory signaling and mediates
CC       most of IgM effector functions involved in B cell development and
CC       primary immune response to infection. Likely limits tonic IgM BCR
CC       signaling to self-antigens for proper negative selection of
CC       autoreactive B cells in the bone marrow and for the maintenance of
CC       regulatory B cell pool in peripheral lymphoid organs. Mediates antibody
CC       responses to T cell-dependent and T cell-independent antigens and
CC       promotes induction of an efficient neutralizing IgG response. Engages
CC       in cross-talk with antigen-receptor signaling via the non-canonical NF-
CC       kappa-B, MAP kinases and calcium signaling pathways (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60667}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (via Ig-like domain) with IGHM (via CH4/Cmu4
CC       domain), both secreted and membrane-bound IgM; the interaction is
CC       glycan-independent and multivalent theoretically involving up to eight
CC       binding sites for the IgM pentamer. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60667}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60667};
CC       Single-pass membrane protein {ECO:0000255}. Early endosome membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60667}; Single-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000255}. Golgi apparatus, trans-Golgi network membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60667}; Single-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000255}. Lysosome membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60667};
CC       Single-pass membrane protein {ECO:0000255}. Note=Continuously recycles
CC       between cytoplasmic pool and the plasma membrane to bind as much IgM as
CC       possible. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60667}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The Ig-like V-set domain comprises three loops analogous to the
CC       complementarity-determining regions (CDR) of Ig variable domains, which
CC       are responsible for engaging IgM. Mediates multivalent interactions
CC       with the CH4 domains of pentameric IgM, facilitating receptor
CC       clustering and signaling. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60667}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylated on both Tyr and Ser residues.
