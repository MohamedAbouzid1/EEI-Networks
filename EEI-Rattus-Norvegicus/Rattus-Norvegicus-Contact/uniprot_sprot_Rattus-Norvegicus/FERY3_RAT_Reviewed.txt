CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the FERRY complex (Five-subunit Endosomal Rab5
CC       and RNA/ribosome intermediary). The FERRY complex directly interacts
CC       with mRNAs and RAB5A, and functions as a RAB5A effector involved in the
CC       localization and the distribution of specific mRNAs most likely by
CC       mediating their endosomal transport. The complex recruits mRNAs and
CC       ribosomes to early endosomes through direct mRNA-interaction (By
CC       similarity). Plays a role in mast cell degranulation (PubMed:25122211).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NQ89, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25122211}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the FERRY complex composed of five subunits,
CC       TBCK, PPP1R21, FERRY3, CRYZL1 and GATD1 with a ratio of 1:2:1:2:4,
CC       respectively. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NQ89}.
