CC   -!- FUNCTION: Microtubule-severing enzyme that negatively regulates cell
CC       migration and wound healing (By similarity). In migrating cells,
CC       targets dynamic microtubules (MTs) at the leading edge and severs them,
CC       thereby suppressing motility (By similarity). Microtubule severing
CC       releases ARHGEF2 which activates RHOA, which in turn regulates focal
CC       ahesion turnover via focal adhesion kinase, as opposed to F-actin
CC       polymerization, to suppress cell motility (By similarity). Negative
CC       regulator of axon regeneration that suppresses axonal growth by
CC       selectively severing dynamic MTs in the distal axon shaft and growth
CC       cone (PubMed:33872220). Contributes to proper cell branching during
CC       endothelial and neuronal development (By similarity).
