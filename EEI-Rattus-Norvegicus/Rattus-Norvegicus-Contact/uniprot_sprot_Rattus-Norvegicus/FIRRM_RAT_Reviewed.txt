CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulates PLK1 kinase activity at kinetochores and promotes
CC       faithful chromosome segregation in prometaphase by bridging kinase and
CC       phosphatase activities. Phosphorylation of FIRRM by PLK1 negatively
CC       regulates its interaction with the phosphatase, PPP1CC, thus creating a
CC       negative feedback loop for maintaining proper PLK1 kinase activity
CC       during mitosis. In complex with FIGL1 may regulate homologous
CC       recombination. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NSG2}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (via its N-terminal region) with PLK1; controls PLK1
CC       kinase activity. Interacts (via the KVVXF motif) with PPP1CC; controls
CC       PLK1 kinase activity. Interacts with FIGNL1; may regulate homologous
CC       recombination. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NSG2}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Chromosome, centromere, kinetochore
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NSG2}. Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NSG2}.
CC       Chromosome, centromere {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NSG2}. Midbody
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NSG2}. Cytoplasm, cytoskeleton, spindle
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NSG2}. Note=Exhibits cell-cycle-dependent
CC       distribution during mitosis. Detected in the nucleus in interphase.
CC       Colocalizes with PLK1 to the centromeres in a nearby prometaphase
CC       cells. Relocates to the central spindle in anaphase and to the midbody
CC       in telophase cells. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NSG2}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylation at Ser-101 by PLK1 strengthens FIRRM-PLK1
