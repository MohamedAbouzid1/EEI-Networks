CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transmembrane protein which mediates synaptic endocytosis and
CC       fitness-based cell culling (By similarity). In response to different
CC       stimulus strengths, controls two major modes of synaptic vesicle (SV)
CC       retrieval in hippocampal neurons; Clathrin-mediated endocytosis (CME)
CC       in response to mild stimulation and activity-dependent bulk endocytosis
CC       (ADBE) in response to strong stimulation (PubMed:28414717). In
CC       cytotoxic T-lymphoocytes (CTLs) facilitates calcium-dependent
CC       endocytosis of cytotoxic granules (CGs) at the immuno synapse (By
CC       similarity). Different isoforms work as fitness fingerprints in 'loser'
CC       and 'winner' cells and thereby mediate win/lose decisions as part of
CC       the cell competition process (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BG21, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UGQ2,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:28414717}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with adaptor protein complex 2 (AP-2).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BG21}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000305}; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein {ECO:0000305}. Cytoplasmic vesicle, secretory vesicle, synaptic
CC       vesicle {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22813738, ECO:0000269|PubMed:28414717}.
CC       Golgi apparatus {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28414717}. Vesicle
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BG21}. Note=Enriched in synaptic vesicles at
CC       the presynpatic vesicles (PubMed:28414717). Detected in the Golgi
CC       apparatus of cultured hippocampal neurons (PubMed:28414717). In
CC       cytotoxic T-lymphoocytes, localizes to intracellular vesicles that move
CC       to the immuno synapse (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BG21,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:28414717}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in calyces in the brain (at protein
CC       level) (PubMed:22813738, PubMed:28414717). Detected in cultured
CC       hippocampal neurons (at protein level) (PubMed:28414717).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22813738, ECO:0000269|PubMed:28414717}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Knockdown in cultured hippocampal neurons impairs
CC       Clathrin-mediated endocytosis (CME) in response to mild stimulation
CC       (200 action potentials at 20 Hz) and activity-dependent bulk
CC       endocytosis (ADBE) in response to strong stimulation (1,600 action
CC       potentials delivered at 80 Hz). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28414717}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the calcium channel flower family.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
