CC   -!- FUNCTION: Integral mitochondrial outer-membrane protein that mediates
CC       the formation of mitochondria-associated endoplasmic reticulum
CC       membranes (MAMs). In turn, mediates angiogenesis and neoangiogenesis
CC       through interference with intracellular Ca(2+) communication and
CC       regulation of the vascular endothelial growth factor receptor
CC       KDR/VEGFR2 expression at both mRNA and protein levels. Acts also as an
CC       activator of hypoxia-induced mitophagy, an important mechanism for
CC       mitochondrial quality and homeostasis, by interacting with and
CC       recruiting LC3 protein family to mitochondria. Mechanistically,
CC       recruits DRP1 at ER-mitochondria contact sites leading to DRP1
CC       oligomerization and GTPase activity to facilitate mitochondrial fission
CC       during hypoxia. Additionally, plays a role in hepatic ferroptosis by
CC       interacting directly with glutathione peroxidase/GPX4 to facilitate its
CC       recruitment into mitochondria through TOM/TIM complex where it is
CC       degraded by mitophagy. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8IVP5}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (via YXXL motif) with MAP1 LC3 family proteins
CC       MAP1LC3A, MAP1LC3B and GABARAP. Interacts with DNM1L/DPR1. Interacts
CC       with GPX4. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8IVP5}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Mitochondrion outer membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8IVP5}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8IVP5}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The YXXL motif mediates the interaction with MAP1 LC3 family
CC       proteins MAP1LC3A, MAP1LC3B and GABARAP.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8IVP5}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylation at Ser-13 by CK2 and at Tyr-18 by SRC inhibits
CC       activation of mitophagy. Following hypoxia, dephosphorylated at Tyr-18,
CC       leading to interaction with MAP1 LC3 family proteins and triggering
CC       mitophagy. Dephosphorylation is mediated by PGAM5. Phosphorylated by
CC       ULK1 at Ser-17 which enhances FUNDC1 binding to LC3.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8IVP5}.
CC   -!- PTM: Ubiquitinated on Lys-119. Deubiquitinated by USP19; leading to
CC       hypoxia-induced DRP1 oligomerization and GTPase activity.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8IVP5}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the FUN14 family. {ECO:0000305}.
