CC   -!- FUNCTION: Associates with and regulates the activity of the
CC       sodium/potassium-transporting ATPase (NKA) which transports Na(+) out
CC       of the cell and K(+) into the cell. Reduces glutathionylation of the
CC       NKA beta-1 subunit ATP1B1, thus reversing glutathionylation-mediated
CC       inhibition of ATP1B1. Induces a hyperpolarization-activated chloride
CC       current when expressed in Xenopus oocytes.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14802}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Regulatory subunit of the sodium/potassium-transporting ATPase
CC       which is composed of a catalytic alpha subunit, a non-catalytic beta
CC       subunit and an additional regulatory subunit. Interacts with catalytic
CC       alpha subunit ATP1A1. Also interacts with non-catalytic beta subunit
CC       ATP1B1. Interacts with the alpha1-beta1, alpha2-beta1 and alpha3-beta1
CC       NKA isozymes. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14802,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q61835}.
