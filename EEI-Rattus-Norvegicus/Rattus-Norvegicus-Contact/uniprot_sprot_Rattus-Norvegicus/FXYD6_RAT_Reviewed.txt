CC   -!- FUNCTION: Associates with and regulates the activity of the
CC       sodium/potassium-transporting ATPase (NKA) which catalyzes the
CC       hydrolysis of ATP coupled with the exchange of Na(+) and K(+) ions
CC       across the plasma membrane (PubMed:17209044). Decreases the apparent
CC       affinity of the transporter for Na(+) (PubMed:17209044). In addition to
CC       modulating NKA kinetics, may also function as a regulator of NKA
CC       localization to the plasma membrane (PubMed:17209044).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17209044}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Regulatory subunit of the sodium/potassium-transporting ATPase
CC       which is composed of a catalytic alpha subunit, a non-catalytic beta
CC       subunit and an additional regulatory subunit. The regulatory subunit, a
CC       member of the FXYD protein family, modulates the enzymatic activity in
CC       a tissue- and isoform-specific way by changing affinities of the
CC       Na+/K+-ATPase toward Na(+), K(+) or ATP. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17209044}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17209044};
CC       Single-pass type I membrane protein {ECO:0000305}.
