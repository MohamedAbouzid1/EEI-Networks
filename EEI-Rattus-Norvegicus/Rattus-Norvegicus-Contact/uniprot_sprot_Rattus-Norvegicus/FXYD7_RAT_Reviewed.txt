CC   -!- FUNCTION: Associates with and regulates the activity of the
CC       sodium/potassium-transporting ATPase (NKA) which catalyzes the
CC       hydrolysis of ATP coupled with the exchange of Na(+) and K(+) ions
CC       across the plasma membrane (By similarity). Reduces the apparent
CC       affinity for external K(+), an effect that depends on the presence of
CC       external Na(+) and voltage (By similarity). Increases the apparent
CC       affinity for intracellular Na(+) (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P58549, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P59648}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Regulatory subunit of the sodium/potassium-transporting ATPase
CC       which is composed of a catalytic alpha subunit, a non-catalytic beta
CC       subunit and an additional regulatory subunit. The regulatory subunit, a
CC       member of the FXYD protein family, modulates the enzymatic activity in
CC       a tissue- and isoform-specific way by changing affinities of the
CC       Na+/K+-ATPase toward Na(+), K(+) or ATP.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P59648}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22535957};
CC       Single-pass type I membrane protein {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P59648}.
CC       Note=Able to translocate to the plasma membrane of Xenopus oocytes
CC       independent of its association with NKA (PubMed:22535957). Lacks a
CC       cleavable signal sequence (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P59648, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22535957}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed specifically in brain. Expressed in both
CC       neurons and glia. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12093728}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The transmembrane domain is important for the interaction with
CC       NKA and with the functional effect of FXYD7.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P59648}.
CC   -!- PTM: O-glycosylated; required for stabilization and translocation to
CC       the plasma membrane. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22535957}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the FXYD family. {ECO:0000305}.
