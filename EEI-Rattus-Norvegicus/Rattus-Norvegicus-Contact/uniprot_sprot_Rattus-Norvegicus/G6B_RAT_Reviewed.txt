CC   -!- FUNCTION: Inhibitory receptor that acts as a critical regulator of
CC       hematopoietic lineage differentiation, megakaryocyte function and
CC       platelet production. Inhibits platelet aggregation and activation by
CC       agonists such as ADP and collagen-related peptide. This regulation of
CC       megakaryocate function as well as platelet production ann activation is
CC       done through the inhibition (via the 2 ITIM motifs) of the receptors
CC       CLEC1B and GP6:FcRgamma signaling. Appears to operate in a calcium-
CC       independent manner. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:D7PDD4,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O95866}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (via ITIM motif) with PTPN6 and PTPN11. Binds to
CC       heparin. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:D7PDD4, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O95866}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O95866};
CC       Single-pass type I membrane protein {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O95866}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Contains both a transmembrane region and 2 copies of a
CC       cytoplasmic motif that is referred to as the immunoreceptor tyrosine-
CC       based inhibitor motif (ITIM). This motif is involved in modulation of
CC       cellular responses. The phosphorylated ITIM motif can bind the SH2
CC       domain of several SH2-containing phosphatases. The 2 ITIM motifs of
CC       isoform B are required for the inhibition of CLEC1B and GP6:FCER1G
CC       signaling and platelet activation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:D7PDD4}.
CC   -!- PTM: N-glycosylated. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:D7PDD4,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O95866}.
CC   -!- PTM: May be O-glycosylated. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O95866}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylated. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:D7PDD4}.
