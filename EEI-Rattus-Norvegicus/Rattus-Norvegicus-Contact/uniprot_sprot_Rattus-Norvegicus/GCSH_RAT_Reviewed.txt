CC       glycine. The H protein (GCSH) shuttles the methylamine group of glycine
CC       from the P protein (GLDC) to the T protein (GCST). Has a pivotal role
CC       in the lipoylation of enzymes involved in cellular energetics such as
CC       the mitochondrial dihydrolipoyllysine-residue acetyltransferase
CC       component of pyruvate dehydrogenase complex (DLAT), and the
CC       mitochondrial dihydrolipoyllysine-residue succinyltransferase component
CC       of 2-oxoglutarate dehydrogenase complex (DLST).
