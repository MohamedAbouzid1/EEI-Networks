CC   -!- FUNCTION: Neurotrophic factor that enhances survival and morphological
CC       differentiation of dopaminergic neurons and increases their high-
CC       affinity dopamine uptake (PubMed:10828539). Acts by binding to its
CC       coreceptor, GFRA1, leading to autophosphorylation and activation of the
CC       RET receptor (By similarity). May also modulate local neuronal effects
CC       in distal regions of the motor neuron (PubMed:10828539). Involved in
CC       the development of the neural crest (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P39905, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10828539}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer; disulfide-linked (PubMed:8493557). Interacts with
CC       GFRA1 coreceptor and RET: forms a 2:2:2 ternary complex composed of
CC       GDNF ligand, GFRA1 and RET receptor (By similarity). Interacts (via
CC       propeptide) with SORL1 (via N-terminal ectodomain); this interaction
CC       affects GDNF-regulated, but not constitutive secretion
CC       (PubMed:21994944). Also interacts with SORL1 in complex with GFRA1;
CC       this interaction leads to GDNF endocytosis and lysosomal degradation
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P39905,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21994944, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8493557}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P39905}.
