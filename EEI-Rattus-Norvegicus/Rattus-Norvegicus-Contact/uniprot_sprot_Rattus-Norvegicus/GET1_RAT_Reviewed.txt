CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required for the post-translational delivery of tail-anchored
CC       (TA) proteins to the endoplasmic reticulum (PubMed:23041287). Together
CC       with CAMLG/GET2, acts as a membrane receptor for soluble GET3/TRC40,
CC       which recognizes and selectively binds the transmembrane domain of TA
CC       proteins in the cytosol (PubMed:23041287). Required to ensure correct
CC       topology and ER insertion of CAMLG (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O00258, ECO:0000269|PubMed:23041287}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the Golgi to ER traffic (GET) complex, which is
CC       composed of GET1, CAMLG/GET2 and GET3 (PubMed:23041287). Within the
CC       complex, GET1 and CAMLG form a heterotetramer which is stabilized by
CC       phosphatidylinositol binding and which binds to the GET3 homodimer (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with CAMLG/GET2 (via C-terminus)
CC       (PubMed:23041287). GET3 shows a higher affinity for CAMLG than for GET1
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O00258,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23041287}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Endoplasmic reticulum membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O00258}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000255}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the WRB/GET1 family. {ECO:0000305}.
