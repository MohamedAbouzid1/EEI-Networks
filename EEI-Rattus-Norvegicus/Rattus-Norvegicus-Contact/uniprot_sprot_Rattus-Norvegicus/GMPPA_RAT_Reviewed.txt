CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulatory subunit of the GMPPA-GMPPB mannose-1-phosphate
CC       guanylyltransferase complex; reduces the catalytic activity of GMPPB
CC       when part of the complex. Mediates allosteric feedback inhibition of
CC       GMPPB catalytic activity upon binding GDP-alpha-D-mannose. Together
CC       with GMPPB regulates GDP-alpha-D-mannose levels.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q96IJ6}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the GMPPA-GMPPB mannose-1-phosphate
CC       guanylyltransferase complex composed of 4 GMPPA subunits and 8 GMPPB
CC       subunits; the complex is organized into three layers, a central layer
CC       made up of 2 GMPPA dimers sandwiched between two layers each made up of
CC       2 GMPPB dimers. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q96IJ6}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The N-terminal substrate-binding domain adopts a Rossman-like
CC       fold and has a binding pocket for GTP or GDP-alpha-D-mannose.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q96IJ6}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The C-terminal domain consists of a series of tandem
CC       hexapeptide repeats that adopt a beta-helix conformation. The beta-
CC       helix forms several protein interaction surfaces involved in assembly
CC       of the GMPPA-GMPPB mannose-1-phosphate guanylyltransferase complex. A
CC       loop extending from the C-terminal domain (C-loop) is involved in
CC       interaction with other subunits of the GMPPA-GMPPB complex and may be
CC       involved in allosteric inhibition of GMPPB catalytic activity by GMPPA.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q96IJ6}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the transferase hexapeptide repeat family.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
