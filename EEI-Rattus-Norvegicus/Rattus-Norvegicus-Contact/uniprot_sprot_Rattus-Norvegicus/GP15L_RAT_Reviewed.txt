CC       a chemotactic factor that mediates lymphocytes recruitment to epithelia
CC       through binding and activation of the G-protein coupled receptor GPR15

CC       growth inhibitory effect on colon cancer cells which includes G1 cell
CC       cycle arrest. May regulate keratinocyte proliferation. In addition,
CC       through activation of Mas-related G protein-coupled receptors (MRGPRs)
CC       contributes to pruritogenesis by activating itch-selective sensory
