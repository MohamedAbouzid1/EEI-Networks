CC   -!- FUNCTION: May be involved in neural development. Involved in regulation
CC       of osteoblast function and bone formation. Involved in matrix vesicle
CC       release by osteoblasts; this function seems to involve maintenance of
CC       the actin cytoskeleton. May be involved in cellular trafficking of SERT
CC       and thereby in regulation of serotonin uptake.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with SERT. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18581270}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Membrane {ECO:0000250}; Multi-pass membrane

CC       Note=Colocalizes with SERT at the plasma membrane.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Highly expressed in the ventral medullary surface,
CC       moderately in the cerebral cortex and cerebellum, poorly in lung and
CC       kidney, and not at all in heart, skeletal muscle, liver, stomach or
CC       stomach. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11002282}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: In the developing brain, expression begins by at
CC       least embryonic day 20, increases gradually through postnatal day 2 and
CC       day 50 and decreases by postnatal day 300.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11002282}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: By hypercapnic stimulation (CO2 inhalation) in ventral
CC       medullary surface neurons but not in the cerebral cortex.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11002282}.
