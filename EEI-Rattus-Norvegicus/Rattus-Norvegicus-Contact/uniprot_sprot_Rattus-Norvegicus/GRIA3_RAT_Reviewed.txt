CC   -!- FUNCTION: Ionotropic glutamate receptor that functions as a ligand-
CC       gated cation channel, gated by L-glutamate and glutamatergic agonists
CC       such as alpha-amino-3-hydroxy-5-methyl-4-isoxazolepropionic acid
CC       (AMPA), quisqualic acid, and kainic acid (PubMed:1699567,
CC       PubMed:1709304, PubMed:2166337, PubMed:2168579). L-glutamate acts as an
CC       excitatory neurotransmitter at many synapses in the central nervous
CC       system and plays an important role in fast excitatory synaptic
CC       transmission by inducing long-term potentiation (By similarity).
CC       Binding of the excitatory neurotransmitter L-glutamate induces a
CC       conformation change, leading to the opening of the cation channel, and
CC       thereby converts the chemical signal to an electrical impulse upon
CC       entry of calcium (PubMed:1709304). The receptor then desensitizes
CC       rapidly and enters a transient inactive state, characterized by the
CC       presence of bound agonist (By similarity). In the presence of CACNG8,
CC       shows resensitization which is characterized by a delayed accumulation
CC       of current flux upon continued application of glutamate (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P42263,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z2W9, ECO:0000269|PubMed:1699567,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:1709304, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2166337,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2168579}.

CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homotetramer or heterotetramer of pore-forming glutamate
CC       receptor subunits (PubMed:21317871). Tetramers may be formed by the
CC       dimerization of dimers (PubMed:21317871). Interacts with PICK1, GRIP1
CC       and GRIP2 (PubMed:10027300, PubMed:10414981, PubMed:9069286). Found in
CC       a complex with GRIA1, GRIA2, GRIA4, CNIH2, CNIH3, CACNG2, CACNG3,
CC       CACNG4, CACNG5, CACNG7 and CACNG8 (PubMed:19265014). Interacts with
CC       CACNG5 (PubMed:18817736). Found in a complex with GRIA1, GRIA2, GRIA4,
CC       DLG4, CACNG8 and CNIH2 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z2W9,
