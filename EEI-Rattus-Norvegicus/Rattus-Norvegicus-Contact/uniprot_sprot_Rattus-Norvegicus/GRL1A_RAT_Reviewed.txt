CC   -!- FUNCTION: Appears to be a stable component of the Pol II(G) complex
CC       form of RNA polymerase II (Pol II). Pol II synthesizes mRNA precursors
CC       and many functional non-coding RNAs and is the central component of the
CC       basal RNA polymerase II transcription machinery. May play a role in the
CC       Mediator complex-dependent regulation of transcription activation. Acts
CC       as a negative regulator of transcriptional activation; this repression
CC       is relieved by the Mediator complex, which restores Pol II(G)
CC       activator-dependent transcription to a level equivalent to that of Pol
CC       II. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P0CAP2}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the Pol II(G) complex, which contains the RNA
CC       polymerase II (Pol II) core complex subunits and POLR2M isoform 1. Pol
CC       II(G) appears to be an abundant form of Pol II.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P0CAP2}.
