CC   -!- FUNCTION: Essential for cardiac morphogenesis, particularly for the
CC       formation of the right ventricle and of the aortic arch arteries.
CC       Required for vascular development and regulation of angiogenesis,
CC       possibly through a VEGF signaling pathway. Also plays an important role
CC       in limb development, particularly in the establishment of anterior-
CC       posterior polarization, acting as an upstream regulator of sonic
CC       hedgehog (SHH) induction in the limb bud. Is involved in the
CC       development of branchial arches, which give rise to unique structures
CC       in the head and neck. Binds DNA on E-box consensus sequence 5'-CANNTG-
CC       3' (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Efficient DNA binding requires dimerization with another bHLH
CC       protein. Forms homodimers and heterodimers with TCF3 gene products E12
CC       and E47, HAND1 and HEY1, HEY2 and HEYL (hairy-related transcription
CC       factors) (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
