CC   -!- FUNCTION: GTPase component of the Pelota-HBS1L complex, a complex that
CC       recognizes stalled ribosomes and triggers the No-Go Decay (NGD)
CC       pathway. The Pelota-HBS1L complex recognizes ribosomes stalled at the
CC       3' end of an mRNA and engages stalled ribosomes by destabilizing mRNA
CC       in the mRNA channel. Following mRNA extraction from stalled ribosomes
CC       by the SKI complex, the Pelota-HBS1L complex promotes recruitment of
CC       ABCE1, which drives the disassembly of stalled ribosomes, followed by
CC       degradation of damaged mRNAs as part of the NGD pathway.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y450}.
