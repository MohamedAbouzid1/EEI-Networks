CC   -!- FUNCTION: Plasma membrane ferroxidase that mediates the extracellular
CC       conversion of ferrous/Fe(2+) iron into its ferric/Fe(3+) form. Couples
CC       ferroportin which specifically exports ferrous/Fe(2+) iron from cells
CC       to transferrin that only binds and shuttles extracellular ferric/Fe(3+)
CC       iron throughout the body. By helping iron transfer from cells to blood
CC       mainly contributes to dietary iron absorption by the intestinal
CC       epithelium and more generally regulates iron levels in the body.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z0Z4}.
