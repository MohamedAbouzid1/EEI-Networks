CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the intraflagellar transport (IFT) complex B:
CC       together with IFT74, forms a tubulin-binding module that specifically
CC       mediates transport of tubulin within the cilium. Binds tubulin via its
CC       CH (calponin-homology)-like region. Required for ciliogenesis. Required
CC       for proper regulation of SHH signaling (By similarity). Plays an
CC       important role during spermatogenesis by modulating the assembly and
CC       elongation of the sperm flagella (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O35594, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8WYA0}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the IFT complex B, at least composed of IFT20,
CC       IFT22, IFT25, IFT27, IFT46, IFT52, TRAF3IP1/IFT54, IFT57, IFT74, IFT80,
CC       IFT81, and IFT88. Interacts with IFT74; the interaction is direct:
CC       within the IFT complex B, IFT74 and IFT81 mediate the transport of
CC       tubulin within the cilium. Interacts with tubulin; the interaction is
CC       direct (By similarity). Interacts with IFT57 and IFT70B (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with RABL2/RABL2A; binding is equal in the
CC       presence of GTP or GDP. Interacts with IFT88 (By similarity). Interacts
CC       (via the IFT74/IFT81 heterodimer) with RABL2B (By similarity).
CC       Interacts with CFAP61 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O35594,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8WYA0}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell projection, cilium
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O35594}. Cytoplasm
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O35594}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The CH (calponin-homology)-like region shows high similarity to
CC       a CH (calponin-homology) domain and mediates binding to the globular
CC       domain of tubulin. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the IFT81 family. {ECO:0000305}.
