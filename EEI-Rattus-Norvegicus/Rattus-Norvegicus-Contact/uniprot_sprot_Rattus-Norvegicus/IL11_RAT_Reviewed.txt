CC   -!- FUNCTION: Cytokine that stimulates the proliferation of hematopoietic
CC       stem cells and megakaryocyte progenitor cells and induces megakaryocyte
CC       maturation resulting in increased platelet production. Also promotes
CC       the proliferation of hepatocytes in response to liver damage. Binding
CC       to its receptor formed by IL6ST and IL11RA activates a signaling
CC       cascade that promotes cell proliferation. Signaling leads to the
CC       activation of intracellular protein kinases and the phosphorylation of
CC       STAT3. The interaction with the membrane-bound IL11RA and IL6ST
CC       stimulates 'classic signaling', whereas the binding of IL11 and soluble
CC       IL11RA to IL6ST stimulates 'trans-signaling'.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P20809, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P47873}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with IL11RA to associate with IL6ST, giving rise to
CC       a multimeric signaling complex. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P20809}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P47873}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the IL-6 superfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
