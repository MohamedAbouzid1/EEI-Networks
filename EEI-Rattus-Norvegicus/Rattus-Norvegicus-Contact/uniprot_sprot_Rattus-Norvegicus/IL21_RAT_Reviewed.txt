CC       transition between innate and adaptive immunity. Induces the production
CC       of IgG(1) and IgG(3) in B-cells. Implicated in the generation and
CC       maintenance of T follicular helper (Tfh) cells and the formation of
CC       germinal-centers. Together with IL6, control the early generation of
CC       Tfh cells and are critical for an effective antibody response to acute
CC       viral infection (By similarity). May play a role in proliferation and
CC       maturation of natural killer (NK) cells in synergy with IL15. May
CC       regulate proliferation of mature B- and T-cells in response to
CC       activating stimuli. In synergy with IL15 and IL18 stimulates interferon
CC       gamma production in T-cells and NK cells (By similarity). During T-cell
CC       mediated immune response may inhibit dendritic cells (DC) activation
CC       and maturation (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9ES17,
