CC   -!- FUNCTION: Hematopoietic cytokine that plays an essential role in the
CC       development, expansion, and survival of naive and memory T-cells and B-
CC       cells thereby regulating the number of mature lymphocytes and
CC       maintaining lymphoid homeostasis. Mechanistically, exerts its
CC       biological effects through a receptor composed of IL7RA subunit and the
CC       cytokine receptor common subunit gamma/CSF2RG. Binding to the receptor
CC       leads to activation of various kinases including JAK1 or JAK3 depending
CC       on the cell type and subsequently propagation of signals through
CC       activation of several downstream signaling pathways including the
CC       PI3K/Akt/mTOR or the JAK-STAT5. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P13232}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with IL7R and CSF2RG.
