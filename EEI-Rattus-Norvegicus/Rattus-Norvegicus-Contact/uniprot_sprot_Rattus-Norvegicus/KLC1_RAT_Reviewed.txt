CC   -!- FUNCTION: Kinesin is a microtubule-associated force-producing protein
CC       that may play a role in organelle transport (PubMed:19861499). The
CC       light chain may function in coupling of cargo to the heavy chain or in
CC       the modulation of its ATPase activity (PubMed:19861499).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19861499}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Oligomeric complex composed of two heavy chains and two light
CC       chains (Probable). Interacts with SPAG9 (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       ATCAY; may link mitochondria to KLC1 and regulate mitochondria
CC       localization into neuron projections (PubMed:19861499). Interacts (via
CC       TPR repeats) with TOR1A; the interaction associates TOR1A with the
CC       kinesin oligomeric complex (PubMed:14970196). Interacts with BORCS5 (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with MAPK8IP3/JIP3 and NTRK2/TRKB; interaction
CC       with NTRK2/TRKB is mediated by MAPK8IP3/JIP3 (PubMed:21775604).
CC       Interacts with CLSTN1; phosphorylation at Ser-460 inhibits interaction
CC       with CLSTN1 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q07866,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:14970196, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19861499,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21775604, ECO:0000305}.
