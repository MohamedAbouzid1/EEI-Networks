CC   -!- FUNCTION: Probable serine lipid hydrolase associated with lipid
CC       droplets. Has low cholesterol esterase activity. Appears to lack
CC       triglyceride lipase activity. Involved in cholesterol and triglyceride
CC       homeostasis; stimulates cellular triglyceride accumulation and cellular
CC       cholesterol release. Acts antagonistically with PNPLA2/ATGL in
CC       regulation of cellular lipid stores. May regulate triglyceride
CC       accumulation indirectly through stimulation of PNPLA2/ATGL
CC       ubiquitination and proteasomal degradation. Promotes microtubule-
CC       dependent lipid droplet fusion. Highly expressed in macrophage-rich
CC       areas in atherosclerotic lesions, suggesting that it could promote
CC       cholesterol ester turnover in macrophages.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BVA5}.
