CC   -!- FUNCTION: Has a strict specificity for hydrolysis of asparaginyl bonds.
CC       Can also cleave aspartyl bonds slowly, especially under acidic
CC       conditions. Involved in the processing of proteins for MHC class II
CC       antigen presentation in the lysosomal/endosomal system (By similarity).
CC       Also involved in MHC class I antigen presentation in cross-presenting
CC       dendritic cells by mediating cleavage and maturation of Perforin-2
CC       (MPEG1), thereby promoting antigen translocation in the cytosol.
CC       Required for normal lysosomal protein degradation in renal proximal
CC       tubules. Required for normal degradation of internalized EGFR. Plays a
CC       role in the regulation of cell proliferation via its role in EGFR
CC       degradation (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O89017,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99538}.
