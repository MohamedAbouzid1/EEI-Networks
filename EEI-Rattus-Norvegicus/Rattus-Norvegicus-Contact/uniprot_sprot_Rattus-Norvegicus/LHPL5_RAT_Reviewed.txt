CC   -!- FUNCTION: Auxiliary subunit of the mechanotransducer (MET) non-specific
CC       cation channel complex located at the tips of the shorter stereocilia
CC       of cochlear hair cells and that mediates sensory transduction in the
CC       auditory system. The MET complex is composed of two dimeric pore-
CC       forming ion-conducting transmembrane TMC (TMC1 or TMC2) subunits, and
CC       aided by several auxiliary proteins including LHFPL5, TMIE, CIB2/3 and
CC       TOMT, and the tip-link PCDH15. Functionally couples PCDH15 to the
CC       transduction channel. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q4KL25}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Forms the MET channel composed of TMC (TMC1 or TMC2), TMIE,
CC       TOMT, CIB (CIB2 or CIB3), LHPL5 and PCDH15. Interaction with PCDH15 is
CC       required for efficient localization to hair bundles.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q4KL25}.
