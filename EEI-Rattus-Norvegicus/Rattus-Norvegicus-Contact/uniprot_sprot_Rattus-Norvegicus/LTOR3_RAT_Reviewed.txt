CC   -!- FUNCTION: As part of the Ragulator complex it is involved in amino acid
CC       sensing and activation of mTORC1, a signaling complex promoting cell
CC       growth in response to growth factors, energy levels, and amino acids.
CC       Activated by amino acids through a mechanism involving the lysosomal V-
CC       ATPase, the Ragulator plays a dual role for the small GTPases Rag
CC       (RagA/RRAGA, RagB/RRAGB, RagC/RRAGC and/or RagD/RRAGD): it (1) acts as
CC       a guanine nucleotide exchange factor (GEF), activating the small
CC       GTPases Rag and (2) mediates recruitment of Rag GTPases to the lysosome
CC       membrane. Activated Ragulator and Rag GTPases function as a scaffold
CC       recruiting mTORC1 to lysosomes where it is in turn activated (By
CC       similarity). Adapter protein that enhances the efficiency of the MAP
CC       kinase cascade facilitating the activation of MAPK2 (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O88653, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UHA4}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of the Ragulator complex composed of LAMTOR1, LAMTOR2,
CC       LAMTOR3, LAMTOR4 and LAMTOR5. LAMTOR4 and LAMTOR5 form a heterodimer
CC       that interacts, through LAMTOR1, with a LAMTOR2, LAMTOR3 heterodimer.
CC       Interacts with LAMTOR1 and LAMTOR2; the interaction is direct. The
CC       Ragulator complex interacts with both the mTORC1 complex and
CC       heterodimers constituted of the Rag GTPases RagA/RRAGA, RagB/RRAGB,
CC       RagC/RRAGC and RagD/RRAGD; regulated by amino acid availability. The
CC       Ragulator complex interacts with SLC38A9; the probable amino acid
CC       sensor. Component of the lysosomal folliculin complex (LFC), composed
CC       of FLCN, FNIP1 (or FNIP2), RagA/RRAGA or RagB/RRAGB GDP-bound,
CC       RagC/RRAGC or RagD/RRAGD GTP-bound, and Ragulator (By similarity).
CC       Interacts with MAP2K1/MEK1 and MAPK2 (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       MORG1 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O88653,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UHA4}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Late endosome membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O88653}; Peripheral membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O88653}; Cytoplasmic side
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O88653}. Note=Recruited to lysosome and endosome
CC       membranes by LAMTOR1. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O88653}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the LAMTOR3 family. {ECO:0000305}.
