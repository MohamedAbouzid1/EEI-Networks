CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required for mannose-6-phosphate-dependent trafficking of
CC       lysosomal enzymes. LYSET bridges GlcNAc-1-phosphate transferase
CC       (GNPTAB), to the membrane-bound transcription factor site-1 protease
CC       (MBTPS1), thus allowing proteolytic activation of the GNPTAB. GNPTAB is
CC       involved in the regulation of M6P-dependent Golgi-to-lysosome
CC       trafficking of lysosomal enzymes. LYSET is thus an essential factor for
CC       maturation and delivery of lysosomal hydrolases.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8N6I4}.
