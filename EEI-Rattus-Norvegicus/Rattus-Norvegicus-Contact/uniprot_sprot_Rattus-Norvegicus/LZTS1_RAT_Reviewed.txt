CC       Note=Associated with the plasma membrane and with microtubules (By

CC       postsynaptic density. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Highly expressed in brain, in particular in cortex,

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12415013}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Highly expressed in cerebellum in 1 to 3 week old

CC       constant high level after birth.

CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
