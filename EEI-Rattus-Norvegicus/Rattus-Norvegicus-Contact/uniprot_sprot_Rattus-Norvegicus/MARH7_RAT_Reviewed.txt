CC       E2 ubiquitin-conjugating enzyme in the form of a thioester and then
CC       directly transfer the ubiquitin to targeted substrates (By similarity).
CC       May be involved in T-cell proliferation by regulating LIF secretion (By
CC       similarity). May play a role in lysosome homeostasis. Promotes 'Lys-6',
CC       'Lys-11' and 'Lys-63'-linked mixed polyubiquitination on ATG14 leading
CC       to the inhibition of autophagy by impairing the interaction between

CC       regulation of the NLRP3 inflammasome by promoting its uibiquitination
