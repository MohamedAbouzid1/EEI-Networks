CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mediates pre-mRNA alternative splicing regulation. Acts
CC       either as activator or repressor of splicing on specific pre-mRNA
CC       targets. Inhibits cardiac troponin-T (TNNT2) pre-mRNA exon inclusion
CC       but induces insulin receptor (IR) pre-mRNA exon inclusion in muscle.
CC       Antagonizes the alternative splicing activity pattern of CELF proteins.
CC       Regulates the TNNT2 exon 5 skipping through competition with U2AF2.
CC       Inhibits the formation of the spliceosome A complex on intron 4 of
CC       TNNT2 pre-mRNA (By similarity). Binds to the stem-loop structure within
CC       the polypyrimidine tract of TNNT2 intron 4 during spliceosome assembly.
CC       Binds to the 5'-YGCU(U/G)Y-3'consensus sequence. Binds to the IR RNA.
CC       Binds to expanded CUG repeat RNA, which folds into a hairpin structure
CC       containing GC base pairs and bulged, unpaired U residues (By
CC       similarity). Together with RNA binding proteins RBPMS and RBFOX2,
CC       activates vascular smooth muscle cells alternative splicing events
CC       (PubMed:37548402). Regulates NCOR2 alternative splicing
CC       (PubMed:31283468). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NR56,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:31283468, ECO:0000269|PubMed:37548402}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with DDX1 and YBX1. Interacts with HNRNPH1; the
CC       interaction in RNA-independent (By similarity). Interacts with RBPMS;
CC       the interaction allows cooperative assembly of RNA-bound stable cell-
CC       specific alternative splicing regulatory complexes (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NR56}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NR56}. Cytoplasm
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NR56}. Cytoplasmic granule
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NR56}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the muscleblind family. {ECO:0000305}.
