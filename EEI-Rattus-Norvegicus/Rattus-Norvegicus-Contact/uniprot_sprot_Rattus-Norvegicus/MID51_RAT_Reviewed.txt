CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mitochondrial outer membrane protein which regulates
CC       mitochondrial fission/fusion dynamics. Promotes the recruitment and
CC       association of the fission mediator dynamin-related protein 1 (DNM1L)
CC       to the mitochondrial surface independently of the mitochondrial fission
CC       FIS1 and MFF proteins. Regulates DNM1L GTPase activity and DNM1L
CC       oligomerization. Binds ADP and can also bind GDP, although with lower
CC       affinity. Does not bind CDP, UDP, ATP, AMP or GTP. Inhibits DNM1L
CC       GTPase activity in the absence of bound ADP. Requires ADP to stimulate
CC       DNM1L GTPase activity and the assembly of DNM1L into long, oligomeric
CC       tubules with a spiral pattern, as opposed to the ring-like DNM1L
CC       oligomers observed in the absence of bound ADP. Does not require ADP
CC       for its function in recruiting DNM1L. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NQG6}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Interacts with DNM1L.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NQG6}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Mitochondrion outer membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NQG6}; Single-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NQG6}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the MID49/MID51 family. {ECO:0000305}.
