CC   -!- FUNCTION: Facilitates the ubiquitin-independent proteasomal degradation
CC       of stimulus-induced transcription factors such as FOSB, EGR1, NR4A1,
CC       and IRF4 to the proteasome for degradation (By similarity). Promotes
CC       also the degradation of other substrates such as CBX4 (By similarity).
CC       Plays a role in inhibiting the activity of glucokinase GCK and both
CC       glucose-induced and basal insulin secretion (PubMed:24187134).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3TPJ7, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q504T8}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with GCK; the interaction occurs preferentially at
CC       low glucose levels (PubMed:24187134). Interacts with the proteasome (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q504T8,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:24187134}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000269|PubMed:24187134}. Cytoplasm,

CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3TPJ7}. Note=Detected in the nucleus and
CC       nucleolus with no expression in the cytoplasm (By similarity). However,
