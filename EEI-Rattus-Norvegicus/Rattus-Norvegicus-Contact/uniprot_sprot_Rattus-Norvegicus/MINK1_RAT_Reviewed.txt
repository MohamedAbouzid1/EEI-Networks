CC   -!- FUNCTION: Serine/threonine kinase which acts as a negative regulator of
CC       Ras-related Rap2-mediated signal transduction to control neuronal
CC       structure and AMPA receptor trafficking (PubMed:21048137). Required for
CC       normal synaptic density, dendrite complexity, as well as surface AMPA
CC       receptor expression in hippocampal neurons (PubMed:21048137). Can
CC       activate the JNK and MAPK14/p38 pathways and mediates stimulation of
CC       the stress-activated protein kinase MAPK14/p38 MAPK downstream of the
CC       Raf/ERK pathway. Phosphorylates TANC1 upon stimulation by RAP2A, MBP
CC       and SMAD1 (By similarity). Has an essential function in negative
CC       selection of thymocytes, perhaps by coupling NCK1 to activation of JNK1
CC       (By similarity). Activator of the Hippo signaling pathway which plays a
CC       pivotal role in organ size control and tumor suppression by restricting
CC       proliferation and promoting apoptosis. MAP4Ks act in parallel to and
CC       are partially redundant with STK3/MST2 and STK4/MST2 in the
CC       phosphorylation and activation of LATS1/2, and establish MAP4Ks as
CC       components of the expanded Hippo pathway (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8N4C8, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9JM52,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21048137}.
