CC   -!- FUNCTION: Pore-forming subunit of the mitochondrial ATP-gated potassium
CC       channel (mitoK(ATP)). Together with ATP-binding subunit ABCB8/MITOSUR
CC       of the mitoK(ATP) channel, mediates ATP-dependent K(+) currents across
CC       the mitochondrial inner membrane. An increase in ATP intracellular
CC       levels closes the channel, inhibiting K(+) transport, whereas a
CC       decrease in ATP levels enhances K(+) uptake in the mitochondrial
CC       matrix. May contribute to the homeostatic control of cellular
CC       metabolism under stress conditions by regulating the mitochondrial
CC       matrix volume. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3URS9}.
