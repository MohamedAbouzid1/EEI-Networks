CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required for myoblast differentiation into myotubes, possibly
CC       acting as a transcriptional regulator of the myogenic program (By
CC       similarity). Required for cardiac adaptation to stress through
CC       integrated regulation of the AKT/mTOR pathways and FOXO1. Regulates
CC       cardiac homeostasis and plays a role in the protection against cardiac
CC       hypertrophy (PubMed:22343712, PubMed:26436652). Binds chromatin (By
CC       similarity). May act as a transcriptional cofactor for ISL1, repressing
CC       its transcriptional activity (By similarity). May also repress MYOCD
CC       transcriptional activity (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q5FW52, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22343712,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:26436652}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Directly interacts with LMNA (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       ISL1 (via N-terminal domain); the interaction represses ISL1
CC       transactivator activity (By similarity). Interactions of ISL1 with
CC       MLIP1 and GCN5/KAT2A may be mutually exclusive (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q5FW52}.
