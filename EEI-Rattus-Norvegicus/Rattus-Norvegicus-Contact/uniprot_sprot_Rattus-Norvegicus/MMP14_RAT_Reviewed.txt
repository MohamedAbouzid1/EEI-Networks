CC   -!- FUNCTION: Endopeptidase that degrades various components of the
CC       extracellular matrix such as collagen (By similarity). Essential for
CC       pericellular collagenolysis and modeling of skeletal and extraskeletal
CC       connective tissues during development (By similarity). Activates
CC       progelatinase A/MMP2, thereby acting as a positive regulator of cell
CC       growth and migration. Involved in the formation of the fibrovascular
CC       tissues in association with pro-MMP2. May be involved in actin
CC       cytoskeleton reorganization by cleaving PTK7. Acts as a regulator of
CC       Notch signaling by mediating cleavage and inhibition of DLL1. Cleaves
CC       ADGRB1 to release vasculostatin-40 which inhibits angiogenesis. Acts as
CC       a negative regulator of the GDF15-GFRAL aversive response by mediating
CC       cleavage and inactivation of GFRAL (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P50281, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P53690}.
