CC   -!- FUNCTION: Endopeptidase that degrades various components of the
CC       extracellular matrix, such as collagen type III and fibronectin.
CC       Activates progelatinase A. Involved in the matrix remodeling of blood
CC       vessels. The short isoform efficiently converts progelatinase A to the
CC       intermediate form but not to the mature one. It has no effect on type
CC       I, II, IV and V collagen. However, upon interaction with CSPG4, it may
CC       be involved in degradation and invasion of type I collagen by melanoma
CC       cells.
