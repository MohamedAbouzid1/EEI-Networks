CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium- and proton-independent thyroid hormones and aromatic
CC       acids transporter. Mediates both uptake and efflux of 3,5,3'-
CC       triiodothyronine (T3) and 3,5,3',5'-tetraiodothyronine (T4) with high
CC       affinity, suggesting a role in the homeostasis of thyroid hormone
CC       levels (By similarity). Responsible for low affinity bidirectional
CC       transport of the aromatic amino acids, such as phenylalanine, tyrosine,
CC       tryptophan and L-3,4-dihydroxyphenylalanine (L-dopa) (PubMed:11278508).
CC       Plays an important role in homeostasis of aromatic amino acids (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3U9N9,
