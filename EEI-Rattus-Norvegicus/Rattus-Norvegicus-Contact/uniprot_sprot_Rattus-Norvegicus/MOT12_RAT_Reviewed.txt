CC   -!- FUNCTION: Functions as a transporter for creatine and as well for its
CC       precursor guanidinoacetate. Transport of creatine and GAA is
CC       independent of resting membrane potential and extracellular Na(+),
CC       Cl(-), or pH. Contributes to the process of creatine biosynthesis and
CC       distribution. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:32781157,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:34075817}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
CC       Reaction=creatine(in) = creatine(out); Xref=Rhea:RHEA:73043,
CC         ChEBI:CHEBI:57947; Evidence={ECO:0000269|PubMed:32781157,
CC         ECO:0000269|PubMed:34075817};
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
CC       Reaction=guanidinoacetate(in) = guanidinoacetate(out);
CC         Xref=Rhea:RHEA:73047, ChEBI:CHEBI:57742;
CC         Evidence={ECO:0000269|PubMed:32781157};
CC   -!- ACTIVITY REGULATION: Creatine uptake is inhibited by carbonyl cyanide
CC       3-chlorophenylhydrazone (CCCP) and by valinomycin.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q6ZSM3}.
CC   -!- BIOPHYSICOCHEMICAL PROPERTIES:
CC       Kinetic parameters:
CC         KM=3.38 mM for guanidinoacetate {ECO:0000269|PubMed:32781157};
CC         KM=240 uM for creatinine {ECO:0000269|PubMed:32781157};
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with isoform 2 of BSG; this interaction is required
CC       for its localization to the plasma membrane.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q6ZSM3}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q6ZSM3};
CC       Multi-pass membrane protein {ECO:0000255}. Basolateral cell membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:34075817}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000255}. Note=Localized on the sinusoidal membrane of the
CC       hepatocyte. Colocalized with SLC6A13 in hepatocytes around the portal
CC       vein, (PubMed:34075817). Interaction with isoform 2 of BSG is required
CC       for its localization to the plasma membrane (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q6ZSM3, ECO:0000269|PubMed:34075817}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Detected in kidney, choroid plexus, testis, lung,
CC       stomach, large and small intestine, spleen, fat and parotid gland
CC       (PubMed:21778275, PubMed:34075817). In eye, expressed in cornea,
CC       ciliary epithelium, lens epithelium and lens fiber (PubMed:21778275).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21778275, ECO:0000269|PubMed:34075817}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Homozygous knockout rats are born to expected
CC       Mendelian ratios and no obvious ocular phenotype is observed.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21778275}.
