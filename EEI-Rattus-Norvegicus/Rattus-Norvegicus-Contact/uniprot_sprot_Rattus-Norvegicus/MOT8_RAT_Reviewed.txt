CC   -!- FUNCTION: Specific thyroid hormone transmembrane transporter, that
CC       mediates both uptake and efflux of thyroid hormone across the cell
CC       membrane independently of pH or a Na(+) gradient. Major substrates are
CC       the iodothyronines T3 and T4 and to a lesser extent rT3 and 3,3-
CC       diiodothyronine (3,3'-T2) (PubMed:12871948). Acts as an important
CC       mediator of thyroid hormone transport, especially T3, through the
CC       blood-brain barrier (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P36021,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12871948}.
