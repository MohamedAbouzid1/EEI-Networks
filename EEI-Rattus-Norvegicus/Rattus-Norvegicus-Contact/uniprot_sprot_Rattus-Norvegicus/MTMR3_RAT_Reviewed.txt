CC   -!- FUNCTION: Lipid phosphatase that specifically dephosphorylates the D-3
CC       position of phosphatidylinositol 3-phosphate and phosphatidylinositol
CC       3,5-bisphosphate, generating phosphatidylinositol and
CC       phosphatidylinositol 5-phosphate. Decreases the levels of
CC       phosphatidylinositol 3-phosphate, a phospholipid found in cell
CC       membranes where it acts as key regulator of both cell signaling and
CC       intracellular membrane traffic. Could also have a molecular
CC       sequestering/adapter activity and regulate biological processes
CC       independently of its phosphatase activity. It includes the regulation
CC       of midbody abscission during mitotic cytokinesis.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13615}.
