CC       smooth muscle cells (PubMed:11009567). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q5VZE5,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11009567, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16484612}.

CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q5VZE5}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in primary spermatocytes, basal

CC       crypts. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11009567}.
