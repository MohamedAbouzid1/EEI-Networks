CC   -!- FUNCTION: Calcium, potassium:sodium antiporter that transports 1 Ca(2+)
CC       and 1 K(+) in exchange for 4 Na(+). Critical component of the visual
CC       transduction cascade, controlling the calcium concentration of outer
CC       segments during light and darkness. Light causes a rapid lowering of
CC       cytosolic free calcium in the outer segment of both retinal rod and
CC       cone photoreceptors and the light-induced lowering of calcium is caused
CC       by extrusion via this protein which plays a key role in the process of
CC       light adaptation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60721}.
