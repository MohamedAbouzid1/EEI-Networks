CC   -!- FUNCTION: Possesses an intrinsic kinase activity. Displays 3'-5'
CC       exonuclease activity with a preference for single-stranded DNA (By
CC       similarity). Does not seem to have nucleoside diphosphate kinase
CC       activity (By similarity). Functional component of the gamma-tubulin
CC       ring complex, implicated in the regulation of the microtubule-
CC       nucleating activity of the gamma-tubulin ring complex in centrosomes,
CC       in a kinase activity-dependent manner (By similarity). Part of the
CC       dynein-decorated doublet microtubules (DMTs) in cilia axoneme, which is
CC       required for motile cilia beating (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9QXL8, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y5B8}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of sperm flagellar doublet microtubules (By
CC       similarity). Component of the gamma-tubulin ring complex (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9QXL8,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y5B8}.
