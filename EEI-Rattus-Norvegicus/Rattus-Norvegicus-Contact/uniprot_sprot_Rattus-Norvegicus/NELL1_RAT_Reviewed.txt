CC   -!- FUNCTION: Plays a role in the control of cell growth and
CC       differentiation. Promotes osteoblast cell differentiation and terminal
CC       mineralization. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q92832}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with ATRAID; the interaction promotes osteoblast
CC       cell differentiation and mineralization (By similarity). Homotrimer
CC       (PubMed:10548494). Binds to PKC beta-1 (PubMed:10548494). Interacts
CC       with ROBO3 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q92832,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Z2I4, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10548494}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q92832}. Nucleus
CC       envelope {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q92832}. Secreted
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10548494}. Note=Colocalizes with ATRAID on the
CC       nuclear envelope and the perinuclear region.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q92832}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: It has been demonstrated that ROBO3 binds to both NELL1
CC       and NELL2. However, NELL1 is not expressed in the spinal cord at the
CC       time of commissural axon growth to the midline and has no significant
CC       effect on commissural axon repulsion in vitro, suggesting that NELL1 is
CC       not a functional ligand for ROBO3 in commissural axons. It remains
CC       possible, however, that NELL1 functions as a ligand for ROBO3 at
CC       another spatiotemporal location. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q92832}.
