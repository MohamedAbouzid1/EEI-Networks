CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription factor required for the development of the
CC       heart and the spleen (By similarity). During heart development, acts as
CC       a transcriptional activator of NPPA/ANF in cooperation with GATA4 (By
CC       similarity). May cooperate with TBX2 to negatively modulate expression
CC       of NPPA/ANF in the atrioventricular canal (By similarity). Binds to the
CC       core DNA motif of NPPA promoter. Together with PBX1, required for
CC       spleen development through a mechanism that involves CDKN2B repression
CC       (By similarity). Positively regulates transcription of genes such as
CC       COL3A1 and MMP2, resulting in increased pulmonary endothelial fibrosis
CC       in response to hypoxia (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P42582,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P52952}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer (via the homeobox); binds DNA as homodimer.
CC       Interacts (via the homeobox) with TBX5 (via the T-box); this complex
CC       binds DNA. Interacts with HIPK1 and HIPK2, but not HIPK3. Interacts
CC       with the C-terminal zinc finger of GATA4 through its homeobox domain.
CC       Also interacts with JARID2 which represses its ability to activate
CC       transcription of ANF. Interacts with FBLIM1. Interacts with TBX18.
CC       Interacts with histone methyltransferase NSD2 (via HMG box) (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with NEDD9 (By similarity). Interacts with TBX1
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P42582,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P52952}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P42582}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The homeobox domain binds to double-stranded DNA.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P52952}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the NK-2 homeobox family. {ECO:0000305}.
