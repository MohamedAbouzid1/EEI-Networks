CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Neuromedin-U-23]: Ligand for receptors NMUR1 and NMUR2
CC       (PubMed:28874765). Receptor-binding is very tight if not irreversible
CC       and triggers an increase in the cytosolic Ca(2+) concentration
CC       (PubMed:28874765). Stimulates muscle contractions of specific regions
CC       of the gastrointestinal tract. In rat, NMU stimulates contractions of
CC       stomach circular muscle. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28874765}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Neuromedin precursor-related peptide 33]: Does not function
CC       as a ligand for either NMUR1 or NMUR2 (PubMed:28874765). Indirectly
CC       induces prolactin release although its potency is much lower than that
CC       of neuromedin precursor-related peptide 36 (PubMed:28874765).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28874765}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Neuromedin precursor-related peptide 36]: Does not function
CC       as a ligand for either NMUR1 or NMUR2. Indirectly induces prolactin
CC       release from lactotroph cells in the pituitary gland, probably via the
CC       hypothalamic dopaminergic system. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28874765}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: [Neuromedin-U-23]: Secreted
CC       {ECO:0000305|PubMed:28874765}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed throughout the gastrointestinal tract
CC       with highest levels in the duodenum and jejunum (PubMed:7916966). Low
CC       levels in spinal cord, hypothalamus, and stomach (PubMed:7916966).
CC       Neuromedin-U-23: Expressed in the small intestine and the pituitary
CC       gland (at protein level) (PubMed:28874765). Neuromedin precursor-
CC       related peptides: Expressed in pituitary gland and small intestine (at
CC       protein level) (PubMed:28874765). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28874765,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:7916966}.
