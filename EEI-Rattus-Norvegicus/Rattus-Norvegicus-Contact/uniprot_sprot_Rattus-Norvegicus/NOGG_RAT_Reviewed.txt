CC   -!- FUNCTION: Essential for cartilage morphogenesis and joint formation.
CC       Inhibitor of bone morphogenetic proteins (BMP) signaling which is
CC       required for growth and patterning of the neural tube and somite (By
CC       similarity). Inhibits chondrocyte differentiation through its
CC       interaction with GDF5 and, probably, GDF6 (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P97466, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13253}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Interacts with GDF5; inhibits chondrocyte
CC       differentiation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13253}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Prominently expressed in the CNS. High levels found
CC       in mitral and tufted cells in the olfactory bulb, piriform cortex of
CC       the brain and Purkinje cells in the cerebellum. Low level expression
CC       seen in the lung, skeletal muscle and skin.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: First detected at embryonic day 9 and in the
CC       brain, expression increases steadily from embryonic day 17 to postnatal
CC       day 19.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the noggin family. {ECO:0000305}.
