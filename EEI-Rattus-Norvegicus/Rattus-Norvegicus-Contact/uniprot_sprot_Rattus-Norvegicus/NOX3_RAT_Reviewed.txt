CC   -!- FUNCTION: NADPH oxidase that catalyzes the generation of superoxide
CC       from molecular oxygen utilizing NADPH as an electron donor, upon
CC       formation of a complex with CYBA/p22phox (By similarity). Plays a role
CC       in the biogenesis of otoconia/otolith, which are crystalline structures
CC       of the inner ear involved in the perception of gravity (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q672J9}.
