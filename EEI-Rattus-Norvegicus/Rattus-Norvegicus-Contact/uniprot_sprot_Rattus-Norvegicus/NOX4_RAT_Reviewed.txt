CC   -!- FUNCTION: NADPH oxidase that catalyzes predominantly the reduction of
CC       oxygen to H2O2 (By similarity). Can also catalyze to a smaller extent,
CC       the reduction of oxygen to superoxide (PubMed:15322091,
CC       PubMed:16135519). May function as an oxygen sensor regulating the
CC       KCNK3/TASK-1 potassium channel and HIF1A activity (By similarity). May
CC       regulate insulin signaling cascade (By similarity). May play a role in
CC       apoptosis, bone resorption and lipolysaccharide-mediated activation of
CC       NFKB (By similarity). May produce superoxide in the nucleus and play a
CC       role in regulating gene expression upon cell stimulation (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9NPH5,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15322091, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16135519}.
