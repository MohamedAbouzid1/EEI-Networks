CC   -!- FUNCTION: Proton-coupled metal ion symporter operating with a proton to
CC       metal ion stoichiometry of 1:1 (PubMed:16091957, PubMed:9242408).
CC       Selectively transports various divalent metal cations, in decreasing
CC       affinity: Cd(2+) > Fe(2+) > Co(2+), Mn(2+) >> Zn(2+), Ni(2+), VO(2+)
CC       (By similarity) (PubMed:16091957, PubMed:29317744, PubMed:9242408).
CC       Essential for maintenance of iron homeostasis by modulating intestinal
CC       absorption of dietary Fe(2+) and TF-associated endosomal Fe(2+)
CC       transport in erythroid precursors and other cells (By similarity)
CC       (PubMed:9242408). Enables Fe(2+) and Mn(2+) ion entry into
CC       mitochondria, and is thus expected to promote mitochondrial heme
CC       synthesis, iron-sulfur cluster biogenesis and antioxidant defense
CC       (PubMed:29317744). Can mediate uncoupled fluxes of either protons or
CC       metal ions. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P49281,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P49282, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16091957,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:29317744, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9242408}.
