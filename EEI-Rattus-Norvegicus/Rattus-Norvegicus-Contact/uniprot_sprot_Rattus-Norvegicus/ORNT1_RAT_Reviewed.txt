CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mitochondrial ornithine-citrulline antiporter. Catalyzes the
CC       exchange between cytosolic ornithine and mitochondrial citrulline plus
CC       an H(+), the proton compensates the positive charge of ornithine thus
CC       leading to an electroneutral transport. Plays a crucial role in the
CC       urea cycle, by connecting the cytosolic and the intramitochondrial
CC       reactions of the urea cycle (PubMed:10417335, PubMed:9359400). Lysine
CC       and arginine are also transported by the antiport mechanism (By
CC       similarity). In addition, catalyzes an electroneutral exchange of
CC       ornithine or lysine for H(+), a reaction driven by the pH gradient
CC       across the inner membrane (PubMed:10417335).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y619, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10417335,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9359400}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
