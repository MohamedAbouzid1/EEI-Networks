CC   -!- FUNCTION: Probably plays a role in the development of the brain and the
CC       sense organs. Can bind to the BCD target sequence (BTS): 5'-TCTAATCCC-
CC       3' (By similarity). May play a role in the specification or
CC       differentiation of neurons in the deep layers of the cerebral cortex,
CC       and also in cerebellar regionalization during early development.
CC       {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Brain: restricted regions of the developing rostral
CC       brain including the presumptive cerebral cortex and olfactory bulbs;
CC       expressed in the developing olfactory, auricolar and ocular systems,
CC       including the covering of the optic nerve.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Expressed throughout the forebrain and midbrain
CC       during development, and in addition is also seen in discrete spatial
CC       and temporal domains in the developing cerebral cortex and cerebellum.
CC       Confined to a subpopulation of neurons in layers 5 and 6 within the
CC       adult cerebral cortex and during development expression is high in the
CC       progenitors of these deep-layer cells. Expressed in the developing
CC       cerebellum in spatially restricted regions of the external granular
CC       layer.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the paired homeobox family. Bicoid subfamily.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
