CC   -!- FUNCTION: Intracellular mono-ADP-ribosyltransferase that plays a role
CC       in different processes, such as protein translation and unfolded
CC       protein response (UPR), through the mono-ADP-ribosylation of proteins
CC       involved in those processes. Acts as an inhibitor of protein
CC       translation by catalyzing mono-ADP-ribosylation of ribosomal subunits,
CC       such as RPL14 and RPS6, thereby inhibiting polysome assembly and mRNA
CC       loading. Mono-ADP-ribosylation of ribosomal subunits is promoted by
CC       NMNAT2. Involved in the unfolded protein response (UPR) by ADP-
CC       ribosylating and activating EIF2AK3 and ERN1, two important UPR
CC       effectors. May also mediate mono-ADP-ribosylation of karyopherin KPNB1
CC       a nuclear import factor. May not modify proteins on arginine or
CC       cysteine residues compared to other mono-ADP-ribosyltransferases.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8N5Y8}.
