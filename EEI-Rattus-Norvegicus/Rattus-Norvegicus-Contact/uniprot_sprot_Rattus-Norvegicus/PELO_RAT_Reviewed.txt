CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the Pelota-HBS1L complex, a complex that
CC       recognizes stalled ribosomes and triggers the No-Go Decay (NGD)
CC       pathway. In the Pelota-HBS1L complex, PELO recognizes ribosomes stalled
CC       at the 3' end of an mRNA and engages stalled ribosomes by destabilizing
CC       mRNA in the mRNA channel. Following mRNA extraction from stalled
CC       ribosomes by the SKI complex, the Pelota-HBS1L complex promotes
CC       recruitment of ABCE1, which drives the disassembly of stalled
CC       ribosomes, followed by degradation of damaged mRNAs as part of the NGD
CC       pathway. As part of the PINK1-regulated signaling, upon mitochondrial
CC       damage is recruited to the ribosome/mRNA-ribonucleoprotein complex
CC       associated to mitochondrial outer membrane thereby enabling the
CC       recruitment of autophagy receptors and induction of mitophagy.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRX2}.
