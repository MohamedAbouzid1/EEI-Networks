CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the PEX1-PEX6 AAA ATPase complex, a protein
CC       dislocase complex that mediates the ATP-dependent extraction of the
CC       PEX5 receptor from peroxisomal membranes, an essential step for PEX5
CC       recycling. Specifically recognizes PEX5 monoubiquitinated at 'Cys-11',
CC       and pulls it out of the peroxisome lumen through the PEX2-PEX10-PEX12
CC       retrotranslocation channel. Extraction by the PEX1-PEX6 AAA ATPase
CC       complex is accompanied by unfolding of the TPR repeats and release of
CC       bound cargo from PEX5. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13608}.

CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with PEX1; forming the PEX1-PEX6 AAA ATPase complex,
CC       which is composed of a heterohexamer formed by a trimer of PEX1-PEX6
CC       dimers. Interacts with PEX26; interaction is direct and promotes
CC       recruitment to peroxisomal membranes. Interacts with ZFAND6.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13608}.

CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13608}.
