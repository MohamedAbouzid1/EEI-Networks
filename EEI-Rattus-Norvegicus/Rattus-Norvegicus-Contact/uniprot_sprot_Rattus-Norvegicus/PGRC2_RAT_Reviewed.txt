CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required for the maintenance of uterine histoarchitecture and
CC       normal female reproductive lifespan. May serve as a universal non-
CC       classical progesterone receptor in the uterus. Intracellular heme
CC       chaperone required for delivery of labile, or signaling heme, to the
CC       nucleus. Plays a role in adipocyte function and systemic glucose
CC       homeostasis. In brown fat, which has a high demand for heme, delivery
CC       of labile heme in the nucleus regulates the activity of heme-responsive
CC       transcriptional repressors such as NR1D1 and BACH1.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80UU9}.
