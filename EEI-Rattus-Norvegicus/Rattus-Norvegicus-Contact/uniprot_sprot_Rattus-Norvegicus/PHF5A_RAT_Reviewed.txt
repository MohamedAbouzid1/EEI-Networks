CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the 17S U2 SnRNP complex of the spliceosome, a
CC       large ribonucleoprotein complex that removes introns from transcribed
CC       pre-mRNAs (By similarity). The 17S U2 SnRNP complex (1) directly
CC       participates in early spliceosome assembly and (2) mediates recognition
CC       of the intron branch site during pre-mRNA splicing by promoting the
CC       selection of the pre-mRNA branch-site adenosine, the nucleophile for
CC       the first step of splicing (By similarity). Within the 17S U2 SnRNP
CC       complex, PHF5A is part of the SF3B subcomplex, which is required for
CC       'A' complex assembly formed by the stable binding of U2 snRNP to the
CC       branchpoint sequence in pre-mRNA (By similarity). Sequence independent
CC       binding of SF3A and SF3B subcomplexes upstream of the branch site is
CC       essential, it may anchor U2 snRNP to the pre-mRNA (By similarity). Also
CC       acts as a component of the minor spliceosome, which is involved in the
CC       splicing of U12-type introns in pre-mRNAs (By similarity). Also
CC       involved in elongation by RNA polymerase II as part of the PAF1 complex
CC       (PAF1C) (By similarity). PAF1C is required for maintenance of embryonic
CC       stem cell (ESC) self-renewal and cellular reprogramming of stem cells
CC       (By similarity). Maintains pluripotency by recruiting and stabilizing
CC       PAF1C on pluripotency genes loci, and by regulating the expression of
CC       the pluripotency genes (By similarity). Regulates the deposition of
CC       elongation-associated histone modifications, including dimethylated
CC       histone H3 'Lys-79' (H3K79me2) and trimethylated histone H3 'Lys-36'
CC       (H3K36me3), on PAF1C targets, self-renewal and pluripotency genes (By
CC       similarity). Regulates RNA polymerase II promoter-proximal pause
CC       release of the PAF1C targets and self-renewal genes, and the levels of
CC       elongating ('Ser-2' phosphorylated) RNA polymerase II in their gene
CC       bodies (By similarity). Regulates muscle specification in adult stem
CC       cells by stabilizing PAF1C in chromatin to promote myogenic
CC       differentiation (By similarity). Acts as a transcriptional regulator by
CC       binding to the GJA1/Cx43 promoter and enhancing its up-regulation by
CC       ESR1/ER-alpha (PubMed:12810571). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P83870,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q7RTV0, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12810571}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the 17S U2 SnRNP complex, a ribonucleoprotein
CC       complex that contains small nuclear RNA (snRNA) U2 and a number of
CC       specific proteins (By similarity). Part of the SF3B subcomplex of the
CC       17S U2 SnRNP complex (By similarity). SF3B associates with the splicing
CC       subcomplex SF3A and a 12S RNA unit to form the U2 small nuclear
CC       ribonucleoproteins complex (U2 snRNP) (By similarity). Within the SF3B
CC       complex interacts directly with SF3B1 and SF3B3 (By similarity).
CC       Component of the minor spliceosome, which splices U12-type introns (By
CC       similarity). Within this complex, interacts with CRIPT (By similarity).
CC       Interacts (via N-terminus) with U2AF1 and SRSF5; acts to bridge the two
CC       (By similarity). Interacts (via C-terminus) with EP400 and DDX1; acts
CC       to bridge the two (By similarity). Interacts with the PAF1 complex
CC       (PAF1C) composed of CDC73, PAF1, LEO1, CTR9, RTF1 and SKIC8 (By
CC       similarity). Within the PAF1C interacts directly with CDC73 and SKIC8
CC       (By similarity). Interacts with RNA polymerase II (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P83870, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q7RTV0}.
