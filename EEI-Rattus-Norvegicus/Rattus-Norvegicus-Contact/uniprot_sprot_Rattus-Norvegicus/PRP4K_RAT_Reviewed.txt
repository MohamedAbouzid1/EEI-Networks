CC   -!- FUNCTION: Serine/threonine kinase involved in spliceosomal assembly as
CC       well as mitosis and signaling regulation. Connects chromatin mediated
CC       regulation of transcription and pre-mRNA splicing. During spliceosomal
CC       assembly, interacts with and phosphorylates PRPF6 and PRPF31,
CC       components of the U4/U6-U5 tri-small nuclear ribonucleoprotein (snRNP),
CC       to facilitate the formation of the spliceosome B complex. Plays a role
CC       in regulating transcription and the spindle assembly checkpoint (SAC).
CC       Associates with U5 snRNP and NCOR1 deacetylase complexes which may
CC       allow a coordination of pre-mRNA splicing with chromatin remodeling
CC       events involved in transcriptional regulation. Associates and probably
CC       phosphorylates SMARCA4 and NCOR1. Phosphorylates SRSF1. Associates with
CC       kinetochores during mitosis and is necessary for recruitment and
CC       maintenance of the checkpoint proteins such as MAD1L1 and MAD12L1 at
CC       the kinetochores. Phosphorylates and regulates the activity of the
CC       transcription factors such as ELK1 and KLF13. Phosphorylates nuclear
CC       YAP1 and WWTR1/TAZ which induces nuclear exclusion and regulates Hippo
CC       signaling pathway, involved in tissue growth control.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13523}.
