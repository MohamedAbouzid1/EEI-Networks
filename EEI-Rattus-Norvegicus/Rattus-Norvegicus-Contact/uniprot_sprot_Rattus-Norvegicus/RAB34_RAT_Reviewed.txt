CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transport protein involved in the redistribution of lysosomes
CC       to the peri-Golgi region (By similarity). Plays a role in the
CC       maturation of phagosomes that engulf pathogens, such as S.aureus and
CC       M.tuberculosis (By similarity). Plays a role in the fusion of
CC       phagosomes with lysosomes (By similarity). Required for the early steps
CC       of intracellular ciliogenesis, the cilium assembly pathway initiated by
CC       trafficking and docking of ciliary vesicles to the centrioles in the
CC       cytoplasm, followed by axoneme formation in the cytoplasm. After
CC       axoneme elongation, the centrioles migrate close to the cell surface so
CC       that ciliary vesicles can fuse with the plasma membrane to expose cilia
CC       to the extracellular space. It seems dispensable for ciliogenesis via
CC       the extracellular pathway where cilium assembly begins after migration
CC       and docking of the centriole to the plasma membrane. Acts also as a
CC       positive regulator of hedgehog signaling and regulates ciliary function
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q64008,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BZG1}.
