CC   -!- FUNCTION: The small GTPases Rab are key regulators of intracellular
CC       membrane trafficking, from the formation of transport vesicles to their
CC       fusion with membranes. Rabs cycle between an inactive GDP-bound form
CC       and an active GTP-bound form that is able to recruit to membranes
CC       different set of downstream effectors directly responsible for vesicle
CC       formation, movement, tethering and fusion. Mediates endosomal tethering
CC       and fusion through the interaction with RUFY1 and RAB14 (By
CC       similarity). Acts as a regulator of platelet alpha-granule release
CC       during activation and aggregation of platelets (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P20338, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P61018,
