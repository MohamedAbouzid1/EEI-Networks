CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulator of membrane traffic from the Golgi apparatus
CC       towards the endoplasmic reticulum (ER). Has a low GTPase activity.
CC       Involved in COPI-independent retrograde transport from the Golgi to the
CC       ER. Recruits VPS13B isoform 2 to the Golgi membrane. Plays a role in
CC       neuron projection development. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P20340}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with BICDL1; leads to its accumulation in the

CC       similarity). Interacts with VSP52 (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       RABGAP1 (By similarity). Interacts with GCC2 (via its GRIP domain) (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with RAB6IP1 (via its RUN 1 domain) (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with TMF1 (By similarity). Interacts with CIMAP3
CC       (By similarity). Interacts (GTP-bound) with APBA1/MINT1 isoform 2, also
CC       called Mint1_826, but not with isoform 1 (By similarity). Interacts
CC       with RIC1; the interaction is direct with a preference for RAB6A-GDP
CC       (By similarity). Interacts with RGP1; the interaction is direct with a

CC       interaction is direct (By similarity). Interacts with BICD1 (By
