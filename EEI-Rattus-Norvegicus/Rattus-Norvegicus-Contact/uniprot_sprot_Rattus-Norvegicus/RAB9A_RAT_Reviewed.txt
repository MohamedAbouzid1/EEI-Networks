CC       interacts with HPS4 and the BLOC-3 complex (heterodimer of HPS1 and
CC       HPS4) but does not interact with HPS1 alone (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P51151, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16885419}.
