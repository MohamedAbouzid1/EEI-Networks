CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Isoform 1]: RNA binding protein that mediates pre-mRNA
CC       alternative splicing (AS) regulation (PubMed:31283468,
CC       PubMed:37548402). Acts either as activator (FLNB, HSPG2, LIPA1, MYOCD,
CC       PTPRF and PPFIBP1) or repressor (TPM1, ACTN1, ITGA7, PIEZO1, LSM14B,
CC       MBNL1 and MBML2) of splicing events on specific pre-mRNA targets
CC       (PubMed:31283468, PubMed:37548402). Together with RNA binding proteins
CC       RBFOX2 and MBNL1/2, activates a splicing program associated with
CC       differentiated contractile vascular smooth muscle cells (SMC) by
CC       regulating AS of numerous pre-mRNA involved in actin cytoskeleton and
CC       focal adhesion machineries, suggesting a role in promoting a cell
CC       differentiated state (PubMed:31283468, PubMed:37548402). Binds to
CC       introns, exons and 3'-UTR associated with tandem CAC trinucleotide
CC       motifs separated by a variable spacer region, at a minimum as a dimer.
CC       The minimal length of RNA required for RBPMS-binding tandem CAC motifs
CC       is 15 nt, with spacing ranging from 1 to 9 nt. Can also bind to CA
CC       dinucleotide repeats (By similarity). Mediates repression of TPM1 exon
CC       3 by binding to CAC tandem repeats in the flanking intronic regions,
CC       followed by higher-order oligomerization and heterotypic interactions
CC       with other splicing regulators including MBNL1 and RBFOX2, which
CC       prevents assembly of ATP-dependent splicing complexes
CC       (PubMed:37548402). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q93062,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:31283468, ECO:0000269|PubMed:37548402}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Isoform 2]: Acts as a regulator of pre-mRNA alternative
CC       splicing (AS) (PubMed:31283468). Binds mRNA (By similarity). Regulates
CC       AS of ACTN1, FLNB, although with lower efficiency than isoform 1
CC       (PubMed:31283468). Acts as coactivator of SMAD transcriptional activity
CC       in a TGFB1-dependent manner, possibly through increased phosphorylation
CC       of SMAD2 and SMAD3 at the C-terminal SSXS regions and promotion of the
CC       nuclear accumulation of SMAD proteins (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q93062, ECO:0000269|PubMed:31283468}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer; each protein chain binds one RNA molecule via the
CC       external surface of the homodimer (PubMed:37548402). Forms higher-order
CC       oligomers (up to 21 monomers) (PubMed:37548402). Interacts with RNA
CC       binding proteins MBNL1, RBFOX2, RBM4 and RBM14; the interaction allows
CC       cooperative assembly of stable cell-specific alternative splicing
CC       regulatory complexes (PubMed:37548402). Also interacts with RBM47,
CC       MATR3 and ESRP2 (PubMed:37548402). Interacts with SMAD2, SMAD3 and
CC       SMAD4; the interactions are direct (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q93062, ECO:0000269|PubMed:37548402}.
