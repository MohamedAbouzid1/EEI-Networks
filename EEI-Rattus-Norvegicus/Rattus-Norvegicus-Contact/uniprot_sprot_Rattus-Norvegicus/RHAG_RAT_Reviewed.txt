CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the ankyrin-1 complex, a multiprotein complex
CC       involved in the stability and shape of the erythrocyte membrane.
CC       Heterotrimer with RHCE (RHAG)2(RHCE), that transports ammonium and its
CC       related derivative methylammonium, in both neutral and ionic forms,
CC       across the erythrocyte membrane. The transport of NH4(+) is
CC       electrogenic and masks the NH3 transport. Also, may act as a CO2
CC       channel. Moreover in erythrocyte, regulates RHD membrane expression and
CC       is associated with rhesus blood group antigen expression.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q02094}.
