CC   -!- FUNCTION: Ammonium transporter involved in the maintenance of acid-base
CC       homeostasis. Transports ammonium and its related derivative
CC       methylammonium across the plasma membrane of epithelial cells likely
CC       contributing to renal transepithelial ammonia transport and ammonia
CC       metabolism. Postulated to primarily mediate an electroneutral
CC       bidirectional transport of NH3 ammonia species according to a mechanism
CC       that implies interaction of an NH4(+) ion with acidic residues of the
CC       pore entry followed by dissociation of NH4(+) into NH3 and H(+). As a
CC       result NH3 transits through the central pore and is protonated on the
CC       extracellular side reforming NH4(+) (By similarity). May act as a CO2
CC       channel providing for renal acid secretion (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UBD6}.
