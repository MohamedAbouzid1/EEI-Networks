CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in microhomology-mediated end-joining (MMEJ) DNA
CC       repair by promoting recruitment of polymerase theta (POLQ) to DNA
CC       damage sites during mitosis. MMEJ is an alternative non-homologous end-
CC       joining (NHEJ) machinery that takes place during mitosis to repair
CC       double-strand breaks in DNA that originate in S-phase. Accumulates in
CC       M-phase; following phosphorylation by PLK1, interacts with POLQ,
CC       enabling its recruitment to double-strand breaks for subsequent repair.
CC       Also involved in the DNA damage response (DDR) signaling in response to
CC       genotoxic stresses such as ionizing radiation (IR) during the S phase.
CC       Recruited to sites of DNA damage through interaction with the 9-1-1
CC       cell-cycle checkpoint response complex and TOPBP1 in a ATR-dependent
CC       manner. Required for the progression of the G1 to S phase transition.
CC       Plays a role in the stimulation of CHEK1 phosphorylation.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSD3}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (when phosphorylated by PLK1) with POLQ; promoting
CC       POLQ recruitment to DNA damage sites. Interacts with RAD1; interaction
CC       is direct and promotes association with the 9-1-1 (RAD9-RAD1-HUS1)
CC       complex. Interacts with RAD18. Interacts with TOPBP1. Interacts with
CC       UBE2N. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSD3}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSD3}.
CC       Chromosome {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSD3}. Note=Localizes to sites of
CC       DNA damage in a H2AX-independent manner.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSD3}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The RAD1-binding motif mediates interaction with RAD1.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSD3}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylated at Ser-50 by PLK1, promoting interaction with
CC       polymerase theta (POLQ). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSD3}.
CC   -!- PTM: Ubiquitinated and degraded by the APC/C complex upon mitotic exit.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSD3}.
