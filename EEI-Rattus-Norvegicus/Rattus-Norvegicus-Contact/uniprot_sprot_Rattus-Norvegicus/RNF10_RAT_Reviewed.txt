CC   -!- FUNCTION: E3 ubiquitin-protein ligase that catalyzes monoubiquitination
CC       of 40S ribosomal proteins RPS2/us5 and RPS3/us3 in response to ribosome
CC       stalling. Part of a ribosome quality control that takes place when
CC       ribosomes have stalled during translation initiation (iRQC): RNF10 acts
CC       by mediating monoubiquitination of RPS2/us5 and RPS3/us3, promoting
CC       their degradation by the proteasome. Also promotes ubiquitination of
CC       40S ribosomal proteins in response to ribosome stalling during
CC       translation elongation. The action of RNF10 in iRQC is counteracted by
CC       USP10 (By similarity). May also act as a transcriptional factor
CC       involved in the regulation of MAG (Myelin-associated glycoprotein)
CC       expression (PubMed:18941509). Acts as a regulator of Schwann cell
CC       differentiation and myelination (PubMed:18941509).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8N5U6, ECO:0000269|PubMed:18941509}.
