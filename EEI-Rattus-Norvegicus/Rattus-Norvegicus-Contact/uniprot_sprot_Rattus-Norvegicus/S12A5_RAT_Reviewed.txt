CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mediates electroneutral potassium-chloride cotransport in
CC       mature neurons and is required for neuronal Cl(-) homeostasis
CC       (PubMed:11551954). As major extruder of intracellular chloride, it
CC       establishes the low neuronal Cl(-) levels required for chloride influx
CC       after binding of GABA-A and glycine to their receptors, with subsequent
CC       hyperpolarization and neuronal inhibition (PubMed:9930699). Involved in
CC       the regulation of dendritic spine formation and maturation
CC       (PubMed:22345354). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11551954,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:22345354, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9930699}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
CC       Reaction=chloride(in) + K(+)(in) = chloride(out) + K(+)(out);
CC         Xref=Rhea:RHEA:72427, ChEBI:CHEBI:17996, ChEBI:CHEBI:29103;
CC         Evidence={ECO:0000269|PubMed:11551954};
CC   -!- ACTIVITY REGULATION: Inhibited following phosphorylation by OXSR1/OSR1
CC       and STK39/SPAK: phosphorylation takes place downstream of WNK kinases
CC       (WNK1, WNK2, WNK3 or WNK4) in response to hyperosmotic stress and
CC       subsequent cell shrinkage. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22544747}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer; adopts a domain-swap conformation at the scissor
CC       helices connecting the transmembrane domain and C-terminal domain (By
CC       similarity). Heterodimer wHeterodimer with K-Cl cotransporters SLC12A6
CC       and SLC12A7 (By similarity). Interacts with AP2A1 (Probable).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H2X9, ECO:0000305|PubMed:11551954}.
CC   -!- INTERACTION:
CC       Q63633; Q7TPH6: Mycbp2; Xeno; NbExp=4; IntAct=EBI-1811510, EBI-1811542;
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q91V14};
CC       Multi-pass membrane protein {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q91V14}. Cell
CC       projection, dendrite {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q91V14}. Note=Detected on
CC       dendrites, but not on axons of spinal cord neurons and at GPHN-positive
CC       inhibitory synapses. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q91V14}.

CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Highly expressed in brain. Not detected in other
CC       tissues. Highly expressed in pyramidal neurons and in neurons
CC       throughout the cortex, hippocampus, the granular layer of the
CC       cerebellum and in groups of neurons throughout the brainstem. Barely
CC       detectable in dorsal-root ganglions. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9930699}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Detected in thalamus, but not in hippocampus and
CC       neocortex at 20 dpc. At birth barely detectable in hippocampus.
CC       Expression increases steeply from day 5 to day 9 and then stabilizes at
CC       adult levels. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9930699}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylated at Thr-929 and Thr-1030 by OXSR1/OSR1 and
CC       STK39/SPAK downstream of WNK kinases (WNK1, WNK2, WNK3 or WNK4),
CC       inhibiting the potassium-chloride cotransport activity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22544747}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the SLC12A transporter family. K/Cl co-
CC       transporter subfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
