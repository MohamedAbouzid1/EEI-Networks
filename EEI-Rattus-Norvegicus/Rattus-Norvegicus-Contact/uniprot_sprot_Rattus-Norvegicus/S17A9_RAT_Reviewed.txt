CC   -!- FUNCTION: Voltage-gated ATP nucleotide uniporter that can also
CC       transport the purine nucleotides ADP and GTP. Uses the membrane
CC       potential as the driving force to control ATP accumulation in lysosomes
CC       and secretory vesicles (PubMed:18375752). By controlling ATP storage in
CC       lysosomes, regulates ATP-dependent proteins of these organelles (By
CC       similarity). Also indirectly regulates the exocytosis of ATP through
CC       its import into lysosomes in astrocytes and secretory vesicles such as
CC       adrenal chromaffin granules, mucin granules and synaptic vesicles
CC       (PubMed:18375752). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8VCL5,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18375752}.
