CC   -!- FUNCTION: Proton-coupled polyamine antiporter involved in the
CC       translocation of polyamines from cytosol into secretory vesicles prior
CC       to their release via exocytosis. Uses the electrochemical proton
CC       gradient generated by a V-type proton-pumping ATPase to couple the
CC       efflux of protons with the uptake of a polyamine molecule (By
CC       similarity) (PubMed:25355561). Facilitates vesicular storage of
CC       spermine and spermidine in astrocytes with an impact on glutamatergic
CC       neuronal transmission and memory formation (By similarity)
CC       (PubMed:25355561). Upon antigen stimulation, regulates polyamine
CC       accumulation and release in mast cell secretory granules, which in turn
CC       potentiates mast cell degranulation and histamine secretion (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:D3Z5L6,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q6NT16, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25355561}.
