CC   -!- FUNCTION: Renal transmembrane organic anion/dicarboxylate exchanger
CC       that participates in the reabsorption of conjugated steroids, as well
CC       as bile acids, driven by an outward gradient of dicarboxylates such as
CC       glutarate or succinate (PubMed:16079298). Transports estrone 3-sulfate
CC       and estradiol-17-glucuronide (17beta-estradiol 17-O-(beta-D-
CC       glucuronate)), but not androstanediol glucuronide (5alpha-androstane-
CC       3alpha,17beta-diol 3-O-(beta-D-glucuronate)), nor taurocholate
CC       (PubMed:31553721). Prefers sulfate conjugates of steroids rather than
CC       glucuronide conjugates (PubMed:16079298). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16079298,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:31553721}.
