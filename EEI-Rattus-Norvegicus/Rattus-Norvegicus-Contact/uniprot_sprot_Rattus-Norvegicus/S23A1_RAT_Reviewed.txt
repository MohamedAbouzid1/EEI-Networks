CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium/ascorbate cotransporter. Mediates electrogenic uptake
CC       of vitamin C, with a stoichiometry of 2 Na(+) for each ascorbate
CC       (PubMed:10331392). Has retained some ancestral activity toward
CC       nucleobases such as urate, an oxidized purine. Low-affinity high-
CC       capacity sodium:urate cotransporter, may regulate serum urate levels by
CC       serving as a renal urate re-absorber (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UHI7, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10331392}.
