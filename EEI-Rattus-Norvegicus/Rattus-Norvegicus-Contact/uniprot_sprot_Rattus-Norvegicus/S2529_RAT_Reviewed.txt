CC       Does not transport carnitine nor acylcarnitines. Functions by both
CC       counter-exchange and uniport mechanisms. Plays a physiological role in
CC       the import of basic amino acids into mitochondria for mitochondrial
CC       protein synthesis and amino acid degradation.
