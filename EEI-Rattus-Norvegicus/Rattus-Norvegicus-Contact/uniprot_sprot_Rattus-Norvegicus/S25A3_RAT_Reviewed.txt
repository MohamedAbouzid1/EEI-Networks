CC   -!- FUNCTION: Inorganic ion transporter that transports phosphate or copper
CC       ions across the mitochondrial inner membrane into the matrix
CC       compartment (By similarity). Mediates proton-coupled symport of
CC       phosphate ions necessary for mitochondrial oxidative phosphorylation of

CC       of anionic copper(I) complexes to maintain mitochondrial matrix copper
CC       pool and to supply copper for cytochrome C oxidase complex assembly (By
CC       similarity). May also play a role in regulation of the mitochondrial
CC       permeability transition pore (mPTP) (PubMed:18667415).
