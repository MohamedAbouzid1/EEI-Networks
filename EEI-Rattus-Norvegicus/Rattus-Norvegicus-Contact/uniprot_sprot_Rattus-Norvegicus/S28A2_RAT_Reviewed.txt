CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium-dependent and purine-selective (PubMed:1315767,
CC       PubMed:7775409, PubMed:8967974). Exhibits the transport characteristics
CC       of the nucleoside transport system cif or N1 subtype (N1/cif)
CC       (selective for purine nucleosides and uridine) (PubMed:1315767,
CC       PubMed:7775409, PubMed:8967974). Accepts purine, analogs of purine
CC       nucleosides and uridine, and exhibits high affinity for adenosine
CC       (PubMed:7775409). May contribute to regulate the transport of organic
CC       compounds in testes across the blood-testis-barrier (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O43868, ECO:0000269|PubMed:1315767,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:7775409, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8967974}.
