CC   -!- FUNCTION: ATP:ADP antiporter that catalyzes the exchange of ATP and ADP
CC       across the endoplasmic reticulum (ER) membrane. Imports ATP from the
CC       cytosol to the ER lumen and exports ADP in the opposite direction.
CC       Regulates ER energy metabolism and protein biogenesis. Appears to be
CC       part of a calcium-dependent ER to cytosol low energy response axis,
CC       where calcium efflux from ER to the cytosol triggers ATP import into
CC       the ER lumen to maintain sufficient ATP supply. Provides ATP to ER
CC       chaperone HSPA5 that drives protein folding and trafficking in the ER.
CC       Can transport dATP, UTP or UDP in exchange for ATP, but the
CC       physiological relevance of this process remains to be established.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P78383}.
