CC   -!- FUNCTION: Symporter that cotransports short-chain neutral amino acids
CC       and sodium ions from the extraccellular to the intracellular side of
CC       the cell membrane (PubMed:10660562, PubMed:11692272, PubMed:11756489,
CC       PubMed:12684517). The transport is elctrogenic, pH dependent and driven
CC       by the Na(+) electrochemical gradient (PubMed:11692272,
CC       PubMed:11756489, PubMed:12684517). Participates in the astroglia-
CC       derived glutamine transport into GABAergic interneurons for
CC       neurotransmitter GABA de novo synthesis (PubMed:10660562,
CC       PubMed:11756489). May also contributes to amino acid transport in
CC       placental trophoblast (By similarity). Regulates synaptic plasticity
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8K2P7,
