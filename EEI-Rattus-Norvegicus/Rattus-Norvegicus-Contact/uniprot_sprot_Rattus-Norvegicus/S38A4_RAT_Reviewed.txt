CC   -!- FUNCTION: Symporter that cotransports neutral amino acids and sodium
CC       ions from the extraccellular to the intracellular side of the cell
CC       membrane (PubMed:11118514). The transport is electrogenic, pH dependent
CC       and partially tolerates substitution of Na(+) by Li(+)
CC       (PubMed:11118514). Preferentially transports smaller amino acids, such
CC       as glycine, L-alanine, L-serine, L-asparagine and L-threonine, followed
CC       by L-cysteine, L-histidine, L-proline and L-glutamine and L-methionine
CC       (PubMed:11118514). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11118514}.
