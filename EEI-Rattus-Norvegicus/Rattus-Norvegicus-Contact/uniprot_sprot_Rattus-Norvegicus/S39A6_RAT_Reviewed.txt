CC   -!- FUNCTION: Zinc-influx transporter which plays a role in zinc
CC       homeostasis and in the induction of epithelial-to-mesenchymal
CC       transition (EMT). When associated with SLC39A10, the heterodimer formed
CC       by SLC39A10 and SLC39A6 mediates cellular zinc uptake to trigger cells
CC       to undergo epithelial- to-mesenchymal transition (EMT) (By similarity).
CC       The SLC39A10-SLC39A6 heterodimer also controls NCAM1 phosphorylation
CC       and its integration into focal adhesion complexes during EMT (By
CC       similarity). Zinc influx inactivates GSK3B, enabling unphosphorylated
CC       SNAI1 in the nucleus to down-regulate adherence genes such as CDH1,
CC       causing loss of cell adherence. In addition, the SLC39A10-SLC39A6
CC       heterodimer plays an essentiel role in initiating mitosis by importing
CC       zinc into cells to initiate a pathway resulting in the onset of
CC       mitosis. Participates in the T-cell receptor signaling regulation by
CC       mediating cellular zinc uptake into activated lymphocytes. Regulates
CC       the zinc influx necessary for proper meiotic progression to metaphase
CC       II (MII) that allows the oocyte-to-egg transition (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13433, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8C145}.

CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with SLC39A10; which triggers cells to undergo EMT
CC       and mitosis. Found in a complex with SLC39A6, SLC39A10 and with the

CC       similarity). Found in a complex with SLC39A6, SLC39A10 and with NCAM1;
CC       this complex controls NCAM1 phosphorylation and integration into focal
CC       adhesion complexes during epithelial-to-mesenchymal transition (EMT).
