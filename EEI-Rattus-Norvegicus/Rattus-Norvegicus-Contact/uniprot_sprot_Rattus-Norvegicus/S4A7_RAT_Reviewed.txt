CC   -!- FUNCTION: Electroneutral sodium- and bicarbonate-dependent
CC       cotransporter with a Na(+):HCO3(-) 1:1 stoichiometry (PubMed:10850716,
CC       PubMed:14673192, PubMed:15075186). Mediates the sodium-dependent
CC       bicarbonate transport important for pH recovery after acid load as well
CC       as for regulation of steady-state pH in the duodenum and vascular
CC       smooth muscle cells (By similarity). Plays a key role in macrophage
CC       acidification, mediating bicarbonate import into the cytoplasm which is
CC       crucial for net acid extrusion and maintenance of cytoplasmic pH during
CC       phagocytosis (By similarity). Provides cellular bicarbonate for de novo
CC       purine and pyrimidine synthesis and is a key mediator of de novo
CC       nucleotide synthesis downstream of mTORC1 signaling in proliferating
CC       cells (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BTY2,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y6M7, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10850716,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:14673192, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15075186}.
