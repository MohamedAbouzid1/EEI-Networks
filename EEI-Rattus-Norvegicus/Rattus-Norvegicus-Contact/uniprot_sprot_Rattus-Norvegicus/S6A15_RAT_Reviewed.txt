CC   -!- FUNCTION: Functions as a sodium-dependent neutral amino acid
CC       transporter. Exhibits preference for the branched-chain amino acids,
CC       particularly leucine, valine and isoleucine and methionine. Can also
CC       transport low-affinity substrates such as alanine, phenylalanine,
CC       glutamine and pipecolic acid. Mediates the saturable, pH-sensitive and
CC       electrogenic cotransport of proline and sodium ions with a
CC       stoichiometry of 1:1. May have a role as transporter for
CC       neurotransmitter precursors into neurons. In contrast to other members
CC       of the neurotransmitter transporter family, does not appear to be
CC       chloride-dependent. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H2J7}.
