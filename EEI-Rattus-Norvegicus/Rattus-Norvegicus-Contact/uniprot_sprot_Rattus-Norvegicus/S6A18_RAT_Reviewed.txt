CC       with two sodium and one chloride ions in kidneys and plays a role in
CC       the neutral amino acids reabsorption. Preferentially transports neutral
CC       amino acids such as L-glycine and L-alanine but also other neutral
CC       amino acids. Required CLTRN for cell surface expression and for its
CC       amino acid transporter activity. The transport mechanism is pH-
CC       independent. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O88576}.
