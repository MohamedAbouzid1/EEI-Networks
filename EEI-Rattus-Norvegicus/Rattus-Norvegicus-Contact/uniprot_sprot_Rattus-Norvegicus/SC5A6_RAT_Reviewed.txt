CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium-dependent multivitamin transporter that mediates the
CC       electrogenic transport of pantothenate, biotin, lipoate and iodide
CC       (PubMed:9516450). Functions as a Na(+)-coupled substrate symporter
CC       where the stoichiometry of Na(+):substrate is 2:1, creating an
CC       electrochemical Na(+) gradient used as driving force for substrate
CC       uptake. Required for biotin and pantothenate uptake in the intestine
CC       across the brush border membrane (By similarity). Plays a role in the
CC       maintenance of intestinal mucosa integrity, by providing the gut mucosa
CC       with biotin (By similarity). Contributes to the luminal uptake of
CC       biotin and pantothenate into the brain across the blood-brain barrier
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q5U4D8,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y289, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9516450}.
