CC   -!- FUNCTION: High-affinity Na(+)-coupled choline transmembrane symporter
CC       (PubMed:10649566). Functions as an electrogenic, voltage-dependent
CC       transporter with variable charge/choline stoichiometry (By similarity).
CC       Choline uptake and choline-induced current is also Cl(-)-dependent
CC       where Cl(-) is likely a regulatory ion rather than cotransported ion
CC       (By similarity). Plays a critical role in acetylcholine (ACh) synthesis
CC       by taking up the substrate choline from the synaptic cleft into the
CC       presynaptic nerve terminals after neurotransmitter release (By
CC       similarity). SLC5A7/CHT1-mediated choline high-affinity transport in
CC       cholinergic neurons is the rate-limiting step for production of ACh,
CC       thereby facilitating communication by subsequent action potentials (By
CC       similarity). Localized predominantly in presynaptic terminal
CC       intracellular organelles, and translocated to the plasma membrane in
CC       active form in response to neuronal activity (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BGY9, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9GZV3,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10649566}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
CC       Reaction=choline(out) + n Na(+)(out) = choline(in) + n Na(+)(in);
CC         Xref=Rhea:RHEA:76443, ChEBI:CHEBI:15354, ChEBI:CHEBI:29101;
CC         Evidence={ECO:0000269|PubMed:10649566};
CC   -!- ACTIVITY REGULATION: Choline uptake activity is regulated by
CC       SLC5A7/CHT1 internalization (inactive form) from the cell surface and
CC       recycling of internalized SLC5A7/CHT1 into the cell surface (active
CC       form) (By similarity). Activated by extracellular chloride ion (By
CC       similarity). Specifically inhibited by nanomolar concentrations of
CC       hemicholinium 3 (PubMed:10649566). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9GZV3,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10649566}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homooligomerizes at cell surface (By similarity). Interacts
CC       with SEC14L1; may regulate SLC5A7 (PubMed:17092608).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9GZV3, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17092608}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Presynaptic cell membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9GZV3}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000305}. Cell projection, axon {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BGY9}.
CC       Early endosome membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9GZV3}; Multi-pass
CC       membrane protein {ECO:0000305}. Cytoplasmic vesicle, secretory vesicle,
CC       synaptic vesicle membrane {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12969261}; Multi-pass

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12969261}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in basal forebrain, brain stem, spinal
CC       chord, and striatum (PubMed:10649566). Specific for cholinergic neurons
CC       (PubMed:10649566). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10649566}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The C-terminal dileucine-like motif (DKTILV) controls
