CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the sodium-dependent cotransport of myo-inositol
CC       (MI) with a Na(+):MI stoichiometry of 2:1. Exclusively responsible for
CC       apical MI transport and absorption in intestine (PubMed:17932225). Can
CC       also transport D-chiro-inositol (DCI) but not L-fucose. Exhibits
CC       stereospecific cotransport of both D-glucose and D-xylose. May induce
CC       apoptosis through the TNF-alpha, PDCD1 pathway. May play a role in the
CC       regulation of MI concentration in serum, involving reabsorption in at
CC       least the proximal tubule of the kidney (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q28728, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8WWX8,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17932225}.
