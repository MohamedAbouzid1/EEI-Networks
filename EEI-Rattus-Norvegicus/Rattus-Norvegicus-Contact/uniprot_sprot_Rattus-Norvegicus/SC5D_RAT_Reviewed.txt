CC       cholesterol, the dehydrogenation of lathosterol into 7-
CC       dehydrocholesterol (7-DHC). Cholesterol is the major sterol component

CC       hormone synthesis (PubMed:4019441). In addition to its essential role
CC       in cholesterol biosynthesis, it also indirectly regulates ferroptosis
CC       through the production of 7-DHC. By diverting the spread of damage
CC       caused by peroxyl radicals from the phospholipid components to its
CC       sterol nucleus, 7-DHC prevents this form of cell death (By similarity).
