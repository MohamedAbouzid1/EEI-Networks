CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulatory subunit of multiple voltage-gated sodium (Nav)
CC       channels directly mediating the depolarization of excitable membranes.
CC       Navs, also called VGSCs (voltage-gated sodium channels) or VDSCs
CC       (voltage-dependent sodium channels), operate by switching between
CC       closed and open conformations depending on the voltage difference
CC       across the membrane. In the open conformation they allow Na(+) ions to
CC       selectively pass through the pore, along their electrochemical
CC       gradient. The influx of Na+ ions provokes membrane depolarization,
CC       initiating the propagation of electrical signals throughout cells and
CC       tissues. The accessory beta subunits participate in localization and
CC       functional modulation of the Nav channels (PubMed:1375395,
CC       PubMed:15178439, PubMed:28012039, PubMed:8021275, PubMed:8282123).
CC       Modulates the activity of SCN1A/Nav1.1, SCN2A/Nav1.2, SCN3A/Nav1.3,
CC       SCN4A/Nav1.4, SCN5A/Nav1.5, SCN8A/Nav1.6, SCN9A/Nav1.7 and
CC       SCN10A/Nav1.8 (By similarity) (PubMed:1375395, PubMed:15178439,
CC       PubMed:28012039, PubMed:8021275, PubMed:8282123).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q07699, ECO:0000269|PubMed:1375395,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15178439, ECO:0000269|PubMed:28012039,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8021275, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8282123}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Voltage-gated sodium (Nav) channel consists of an ion-
CC       conducting pore-forming alpha subunit functional on its own that is
CC       regulated by one or more beta subunits (PubMed:1375395,
CC       PubMed:15178439, PubMed:28012039). Interacts with SCN1A; regulatory
CC       subunit of SCN1A/Nav1.1 (By similarity). Interacts with SCN3A;
CC       regulatory subunit of SCN3A/Nav1.3 (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       SCN4A; regulatory subunit of SCN4A/Nav1.4 (PubMed:28012039). Interacts
CC       with SCN5A; regulatory subunit of SCN5A/Nav1.5 (By similarity).
CC       Interacts with SCN8A; regulatory subunit of SCN8A/Nav1.6 (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with SCN9A; regulatory subunit of SCN9A/Nav1.7
CC       (By similarity). Interacts with SCN10A; regulatory subunit of
CC       SCN10A/Nav1.8 (PubMed:15178439). Interacts with NFASC
CC       (PubMed:11470829). Interacts with TMEM65 (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P97952, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q07699,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11470829, ECO:0000269|PubMed:1375395,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15178439, ECO:0000269|PubMed:28012039}.
