CC   -!- FUNCTION: Cell surface proteoglycan that contains both heparan sulfate
CC       and chondroitin sulfate and that links the cytoskeleton to the
CC       interstitial matrix (By similarity). Regulates exosome biogenesis in
CC       concert with SDCBP and PDCD6IP (By similarity). Able to induce its own
CC       expression in dental mesenchymal cells and also in the neighboring
CC       dental epithelial cells via an MSX1-mediated pathway (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P18827, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P18828}.
