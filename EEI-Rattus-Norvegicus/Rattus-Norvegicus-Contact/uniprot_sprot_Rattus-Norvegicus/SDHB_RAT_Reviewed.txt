CC       dehydrogenase complex (mitochondrial respiratory chain complex II),
CC       responsible for transferring electrons from succinate to ubiquinone
CC       (coenzyme Q) (By similarity). SDH also oxidizes malate to the non-
CC       canonical enol form of oxaloacetate, enol-oxaloacetate. Enol-
CC       oxaloacetate, which is a potent inhibitor of the succinate
CC       dehydrogenase activity, is further isomerized into keto-oxaloacetate
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P21912,
