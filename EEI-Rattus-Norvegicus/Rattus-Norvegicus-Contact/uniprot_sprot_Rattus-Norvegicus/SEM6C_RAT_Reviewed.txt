CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in many regions of the developing nervous
CC       system, probably in neurons and their precursors, but also in nonneural
CC       tissue such as immature muscle and dermis. In adult, strong expression
CC       in the skeletal muscle and moderate expression in the brain, where
CC       cerebellum shows the highest expression. Also expressed in almost all
CC       areas of the CNS.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Detected at 12 dpc and found at markedly increased
