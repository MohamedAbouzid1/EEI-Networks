CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of prostatein which is the major secretory glycoprotein
CC       of ventral prostate gland. Steroid-binding protein; can bind non-polar
CC       steroids, cholesterol and a group of small proline-rich peptides.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Prostatein is composed of three different peptides called C1,
CC       C2 and C3. These form covalent C1:C3 (F) and C2:C3 (S) heterodimers
CC       whose non-covalent association forms tetrameric (C1:C3/C3:C2)
CC       prostatein molecules.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Highly expressed in ventral prostate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:6685625}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Androgen dependent, as shown by the decrease in the level of
CC       the protein following castration. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:6685625}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the secretoglobin family. Lipophilin subfamily.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
