CC   -!- FUNCTION: Physiological suppressor of IKK-epsilon and TBK1 that plays
CC       an inhibitory role in virus- and TLR3-triggered IRF3. Inhibits TLR3-
CC       mediated activation of interferon-stimulated response elements (ISRE)
CC       and the IFN-beta promoter. May act by disrupting the interactions of
CC       IKBKE or TBK1 with TICAM1/TRIF, IRF3 and RIGI. Does not inhibit NF-
CC       kappa-B activation pathways. Associates with the striatin-interacting
CC       phosphatase and kinase (STRIPAK) core complex, forming the extended
CC       (SIKE1:SLMAP)STRIPAK complex. The (SIKE1:SLMAP)STRIPAK complex
CC       dephosphorylates STK3 leading to the inhibition of Hippo signaling and
CC       the control of cell growth. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRV8}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with IKBKE and TBK1 via its coiled coil region.
CC       Interaction with TBK1 is disrupted upon viral infection or TLR3
CC       stimulation. Interacts with CDC42BPB. Interacts with SIKE1 which
CC       mediates association with the STRIPAK core complex composed of PP2A
CC       catalytic and scaffolding subunits, the striatins (PP2A regulatory
CC       subunits), the striatin-associated proteins MOB4, STRIP1 and STRIP2,
CC       PDCD10 and members of the STE20 kinases, such as STK24 and STK26.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRV8}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRV8}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the SIKE family. {ECO:0000305}.
