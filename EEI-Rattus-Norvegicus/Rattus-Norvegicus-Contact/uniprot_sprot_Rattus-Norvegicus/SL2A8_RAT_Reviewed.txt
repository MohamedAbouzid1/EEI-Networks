CC   -!- FUNCTION: Insulin-regulated facilitative hexose transporter that
CC       mediates the transport of glucose and fructose (PubMed:10671487).
CC       Facilitates hepatic influx of dietary trehalose, which in turn inhibits
CC       glucose and fructose influx triggering a starvation signal and hepatic
CC       autophagy through activation of AMPK and ULK1. Also able to mediate the
CC       transport of dehydroascorbate (PubMed:23396969).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10671487, ECO:0000269|PubMed:23396969}.
