CC   -!- FUNCTION: Plasma membrane Na(+)/H(+) antiporter. Mediates the
CC       electroneutral exchange of intracellular H(+) ions for extracellular
CC       Na(+) (PubMed:10678742, PubMed:8244988, PubMed:8244989). Major apical
CC       Na(+)/H(+) exchanger in the base of the colonic crypt. Controls in the
CC       colonic crypt intracellular pH (pHi) to direct colonic epithelial cell
CC       differentiation into the absorptive enterocyte lineage at the expense
CC       of the secretory lineage (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3ZAS0, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10678742,
