CC   -!- FUNCTION: Endosomal Na(+), K(+)/H(+) antiporter. Mediates the
CC       electroneutral exchange of endosomal luminal H(+) for a cytosolic Na(+)
CC       or K(+). By facilitating proton efflux, SLC9A6 counteracts the acidity
CC       generated by vacuolar (V)-ATPase, thereby limiting luminal
CC       acidification. Responsible for alkalizing and maintaining the endosomal
CC       pH, and consequently in, e.g., endosome maturation and trafficking of
CC       recycling endosomal cargo (By similarity). Plays a critical role during
CC       neurodevelopment by regulating synaptic development and plasticity (By
CC       similarity). Implicated in the maintenance of cell polarity in a manner
CC       that is dependent on its ability to modulate intravesicular pH (By
CC       similarity). Regulates intracelular pH in some specialized cells,
CC       osteoclasts and stereocilia where this transporter localizes to the
CC       plasma membrane (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:A1L3P4,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q92581}.

CC       gene editing display an early neurodegeneration of the cerebellum and a

CC       an early pathogenic event, preceding autophagic dysfunction. In aged
CC       rat SLC9A6-null brain show deposition of amyloid-beta and tau.
