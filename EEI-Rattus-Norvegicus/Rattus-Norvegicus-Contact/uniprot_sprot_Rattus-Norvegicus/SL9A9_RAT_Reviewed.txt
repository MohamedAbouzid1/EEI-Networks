CC   -!- FUNCTION: Endosomal Na(+), K(+)/H(+) antiporter. Mediates the
CC       electroneutral exchange of endosomal luminal H(+) for a cytosolic Na(+)
CC       or K(+). By facilitating proton efflux, SLC9A9 counteracts the acidity
CC       generated by vacuolar (V)-ATPase, thereby limiting luminal
CC       acidification. Regulates organellar pH and consequently, endosome
CC       maturation and endocytic trafficking of plasma membrane receptors and
CC       neurotransporters (By similarity). Promotes the recycling of
CC       transferrin receptors back to the cell surface to facilitate additional
CC       iron uptake in the brain (By similarity). Regulates synaptic
CC       transmission by regulating the luminal pH of axonal endosomes.
CC       Regulates phagosome lumenal pH, thus affecting phagosome maturation,
CC       and consequently, microbicidal activity in macrophages. Can also be
CC       active at the cell surface of specialized cells, e.g., in the inner ear
CC       hair bundles uses the high K(+) of the endolymph to regulate
CC       intracelular pH (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8BZ00,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8IVB4}.
