CC   -!- FUNCTION: Associates with the striatin-interacting phosphatase and
CC       kinase (STRIPAK) core complex, forming the extended
CC       (SIKE1:SLMAP)STRIPAK complex. The (SIKE1:SLMAP)STRIPAK complex
CC       dephosphorylates STK3 leading to the inhibition of Hippo signaling and
CC       the control of cell growth (By similarity). May play a role during
CC       myoblast fusion (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14BN4,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3URD3}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Interacts with myosin (By similarity). Interacts
CC       with SIKE1 and both associate with the STRIPAK core complex composed of
CC       PP2A catalytic and scaffolding subunits, the striatins (PP2A regulatory
CC       subunits), the striatin-associated proteins MOB4, STRIP1 and STRIP2,
CC       PDCD10 and members of the STE20 kinases, such as STK24 and STK26.
CC       Interacts (via FHA domain) with STK3 (when phosphorylated); the
CC       interaction associates STK3 with the STRIPAK complex (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14BN4, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3URD3}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane, sarcolemma
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15591093}; Single-pass type IV membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15591093}. Cytoplasm, cytoskeleton, microtubule
CC       organizing center, centrosome {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3URD3}.
CC       Endoplasmic reticulum membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14BN4}; Single-
CC       pass type IV membrane protein {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14BN4}.
CC       Mitochondrion membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14BN4}; Single-pass type
CC       IV membrane protein {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14BN4}. Note=Distributed in
CC       the transverse tubules and near the junctional sarcoplasmic reticulum.
CC       Detected along the Z-lines and M-lines in cardiomyocytes. Membrane-
CC       associated (By similarity). Localizes to the centrosomes in a
CC       microtubule- dependent manner (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Alternative spliced transmembrane domains determine subcellular
CC       targeting. Isoforms 1, 2, 3 and 4 are targeted to endoplasmic reticulum
CC       membrane as well as mitochondrion membrane. Isoform 5 is not targeted
CC       to mitochondrion membrane but to endoplasmic reticulum membrane.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q14BN4}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the SLMAP family. {ECO:0000305}.
