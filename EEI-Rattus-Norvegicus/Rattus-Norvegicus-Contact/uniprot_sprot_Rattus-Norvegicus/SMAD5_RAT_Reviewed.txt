CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcriptional regulator that plays a role in various
CC       cellular processes including embryonic development, cell
CC       differentiation, angiogenesis and tissue homeostasis. Upon BMP ligand
CC       binding to their receptors at the cell surface, is phosphorylated by
CC       activated type I BMP receptors (BMPRIs) and associates with SMAD4 to
CC       form a heteromeric complex which translocates into the nucleus acting
CC       as transcription factor. In turn, the hetero-trimeric complex
CC       recognizes cis-regulatory elements containing Smad Binding Elements
CC       (SBEs) to modulate the outcome of the signaling network. Non-
CC       phosphorylated SMAD5 has a cytoplasmic role in energy metabolism
CC       regulation by promoting mitochondrial respiration and glycolysis in
CC       response to cytoplasmic pH changes. Mechanistically, interacts with
CC       hexokinase 1/HK1 and thereby accelerates glycolysis.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99717}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Forms trimers with the co-SMAD SMAD4 (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with PEBP2-alpha subunit and SMURF1. Interacts
CC       with SUV39H1 and SUV39H2. Interacts (via MH2 domain) with LEMD3.
CC       Interacts with WWP1. Interacts with TMEM119 (By similarity). Interacts
CC       with ZNF8. Interacts with RANBP3L. Interacts with HK1. Interacts with
CC       HGS; this interaction attenuates BMP signaling (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P97454, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99717}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99717}. Nucleus
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99717}. Mitochondrion
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99717}. Note=Cytoplasmic in the absence of
CC       ligand. Migrates to the nucleus when complexed with SMAD4.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99717}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylated on serine by BMP (bone morphogenetic proteins) type
CC       1 receptor kinase. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99717}.
CC   -!- PTM: Ubiquitin-mediated proteolysis by SMAD-specific E3 ubiquitin
CC       ligase SMURF1. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99717}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the dwarfin/SMAD family. {ECO:0000305}.
