CC   -!- FUNCTION: G protein-coupled receptor which associates with the patched
CC       protein (PTCH) to transduce hedgehog protein signaling. Binding of
CC       sonic hedgehog (SHH) to its receptor patched prevents inhibition of
CC       smoothened (SMO) by patched. When active, SMO binds to and sequesters
CC       protein kinase A catalytic subunit PRKACA at the cell membrane,
CC       preventing PRKACA-mediated phosphorylation of GLI transcription factors
CC       which releases the GLI proteins from PRKACA-mediated inhibition and
CC       allows for transcriptional activation of hedgehog pathway target genes.
CC       Required for the accumulation of KIF7, GLI2 and GLI3 in the cilia.
CC       Interacts with DLG5 at the ciliary base to induce the accumulation of
CC       KIF7 and GLI2 at the ciliary tip for GLI2 activation.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56726, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99835}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Interacts (via C-terminus) with protein kinase A
CC       catalytic subunit PRKACA; interacts with free PRKACA subunits and the
CC       interaction leads to sequestration of PRKACA at the membrane,
CC       preventing PRKACA-mediated phosphorylation of GLI transcription
CC       factors. Interacts with ARRB2. Interacts with KIF7. Interacts with BBS5
CC       and BBS7; the interactions are indicative for the association of SMO
CC       with the BBsome complex to facilitate ciliary localization of SMO.
CC       Interacts with DLG5 and SDCBP. Interacts with GAS8/DRC4.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56726, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99835}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56726};
CC       Multi-pass membrane protein {ECO:0000255}. Cell projection, cilium
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56726}. Note=Cilium localization is promoted by
CC       SHH and is required for activity. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56726}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: In embryo, found in the early neural folds and
CC       neural tube, pre-somitic mesoderm and somites, developing limb bud,
CC       gut, eye, testes, cartilage, muscle, lung, epiglottis, thymus, tongue,
CC       jaw, taste buds, teeth, and skin. In adult, found in multiple tissues
CC       including heart, brain, liver, lung, skeletal muscle, kidney and
CC       testis.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The N-terminal extracellular domain mediates sterol-binding
CC       which is required for maximal activation of signaling. Contains a
CC       second sterol-binding site within the seven-transmembrane pocket which
CC       is also required for activation. The activating sterol is likely to be
CC       cholesterol. The extracellular site is required for SHH-induced
CC       activity while the site within the transmembrane pocket regulates basal
CC       signaling in the absence of SHH. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56726}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylation by GRK kinases is required for interaction with
CC       protein kinase A catalytic subunit PRKACA.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P56726}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the G-protein coupled receptor Fz/Smo family.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
