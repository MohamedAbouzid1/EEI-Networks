CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required for degradation of both endocytosed EGF and EGFR,
CC       but not for the EGFR ligand-mediated internalization (By similarity).
CC       Component of the endosomal sorting complex required for transport II
CC       (ESCRT-II), which is required for multivesicular body (MVB) formation
CC       and sorting of endosomal cargo proteins into MVBs, and plays a role in
CC       autophagy. The MVB pathway mediates delivery of transmembrane proteins
CC       into the lumen of the lysosome for degradation. The ESCRT-II complex is
CC       probably involved in the recruitment of the ESCRT-III complex. The
CC       ESCRT-II complex may also play a role in transcription regulation by
CC       participating in derepression of transcription by RNA polymerase II,
CC       possibly via its interaction with ELL. Required for the exosomal
CC       release of SDCBP, CD63 and syndecan (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q5U3V9, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q96H20,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10419521}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with TSG101 (via the C-terminal domain). Interacts
CC       with RILPL1 (via the N-terminal domain); which recruits ESCRT-II to the
CC       endosome membranes. Interacts with 14-3-3 proteins (By similarity).
CC       Component of the endosomal sorting complex required for transport II
CC       (ESCRT-II), composed of SNF8, VPS25 and VPS36 (PubMed:11278625). SNF8
CC       is essential for the stability of the ESCRT-II complex. ESCRT-II
CC       interacts with ELL (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q96H20,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11278625}.

CC       {ECO:0000250}. Note=Recruited to the endosome membrane to participate
CC       in vesicle formation. {ECO:0000250}.
