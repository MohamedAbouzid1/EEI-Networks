CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required for pre-mRNA splicing as component of the
CC       spliceosome. As a component of the minor spliceosome, involved in the
CC       splicing of U12-type introns in pre-mRNAs (By similarity). Down-
CC       regulates NF-kappa-B signaling by competing with RELA for CREBBP/EP300
CC       binding. Involved in the microRNA (miRNA) biogenesis. May be involved
CC       in cyclin-D1/CCND1 mRNA stability through the SNARP complex which
CC       associates with both the 3'end of the CCND1 gene and its mRNA.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8TAD8}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of activated spliceosome complexes. Component of the
CC       minor spliceosome, which splices U12-type introns (By similarity).
CC       Binds SMAD4 and CREBBP/EP300. Binds the SMAD1/OAZ1/PSMB4 complex.
CC       Interacts with DROSHA and SMARCA4. Component of the SNARP complex which
CC       consists at least of SNIP1, SNW1, THRAP3, BCLAF1 and PNN.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8TAD8}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8TAD8}.
CC   -!- PTM: Degraded by the proteasome upon binding to the SMAD1/OAZ1/PSMB4
