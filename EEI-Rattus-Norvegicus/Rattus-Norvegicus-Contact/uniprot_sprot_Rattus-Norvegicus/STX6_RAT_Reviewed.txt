CC   -!- FUNCTION: SNARE promoting movement of transport vesicles to target
CC       membranes (PubMed:30962439). Targets endosomes to the trans-Golgi
CC       network, and may therefore function in retrograde trafficking
CC       (PubMed:30962439). Together with SNARE STX12, promotes movement of
CC       vesicles from endosomes to the cell membrane, and may therefore
CC       function in the endocytic recycling pathway (PubMed:30962439).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:30962439}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Identified in a complex containing STX6, STX12, VAMP4 and
CC       VTI1A (By similarity). Binds EEA1 (PubMed:10506127). Interacts with
CC       VPS45A and GOPC (By similarity). Interacts with MARCHF2; the
CC       interaction promotes MARCHF2-mediated ubiquitination and degradation of
CC       CFTR (PubMed:15689499). Interacts with MARCHF3 (PubMed:16428329).
CC       Interacts with BLTP3B (via C-terminal coiled-coil domain)
CC       (PubMed:20163565). Interacts with BAIAP3; this interaction is increased

CC       with VPS51 (PubMed:30962439). Interacts with VPS13B (PubMed:30962439).
