CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in dendrite morphogenesis and maintenance by
CC       regulating lysosomal trafficking. May act as a molecular brake for
CC       retrograde transport of late endosomes/lysosomes, possibly via its
CC       interaction with MAP6 (PubMed:24357581). In neurons, may also play a
CC       role in the regulation of lysosomal size and responsiveness to stress
CC       (By similarity). Required for proper lysosomal acidification (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80X71,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:24357581}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: In neurons, involved in the transport of late
CC       endosomes/lysosomes (PubMed:24357581). May be involved in dendrite
CC       morphogenesis and maintenance by regulating lysosomal trafficking
CC       (PubMed:24357581). May act as a molecular brake for retrograde
CC       transport of late endosomes/lysosomes, possibly via its interaction
CC       with MAP6 (PubMed:24357581). In motoneurons, may mediate the axonal
CC       transport of lysosomes and axonal sorting at the initial segment (By
CC       similarity). It remains unclear whether TMEM106B affects the transport
CC       of moving lysosomes in the anterograde or retrograde direction in
CC       neurites and whether it is particularly important in the sorting of
CC       lysosomes in axons or in dendrites (By similarity). In neurons, may
CC       also play a role in the regulation of lysosomal size and responsiveness
CC       to stress (By similarity). Required for proper lysosomal acidification
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80X71,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:24357581}.
