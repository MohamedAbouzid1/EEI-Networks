CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcriptional repressor (By similarity). Acts on the C-type
CC       natriuretic peptide (CNP) promoter (By similarity). Acts to promote
CC       CASP3-mediated apoptosis (By similarity). Positively regulates TGF-beta
CC       signaling by interacting with SMAD7 which inhibits binding of SMAD7 to
CC       TGFBR1, preventing recruitment of SMURF ubiquitin ligases to TGFBR1 and
CC       inhibiting SMURF-mediated ubiquitination and degradation of TGFBR1 (By
CC       similarity). Contributes to enhancement of TGF-beta signaling by
CC       binding to and modulating the transcription activator activity of SMAD4
CC       (By similarity). Promotes TGF-beta-induced transcription of COL1A2; via
CC       its interaction with TFE3 at E-boxes in the gene proximal promoter (By
CC       similarity). Plays a role in the repression of hematopoietic precursor
CC       cell growth (By similarity). Promotes IL2 deprivation-induced apoptosis
CC       in T-lymphocytes, via repression of TSC22D3/GILZ transcription and
CC       activation of the caspase cascade (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P62500, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q15714}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Isoform 1]: May act to negatively regulate TGFB3 signaling
CC       and thereby inhibit cell death in mammary gland cells.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P62500}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Isoform 2]: Positively regulates cell death in response to
CC       TGFB3 during mammary gland involution. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P62500}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Forms homodimers (By similarity). Forms heterodimers (By
CC       similarity). Component of a complex composed of TSC22D1 (via N-
CC       terminus), TGFBR1 and TGFBR2; the interaction between TSC22D1 and
CC       TGFBR1 is inhibited by SMAD7 and promoted by TGFB1 (By similarity).
CC       Interacts with SMAD7; the interaction requires TGF-beta and the
CC       interaction is inhibited by TGFBR1 (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       TPT1/fortilin; interaction results in the destabilization of TSC22D1
CC       protein and prevents TSC22D1-mediated apoptosis (By similarity).
CC       Interacts with SMAD4 (via N-terminus) (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       ACVRL1/ALK1, ACVR1/ALK2, BMPR1A/ALK3, ACVR1B/ALK4, BMPR1B/ALK6,
CC       ACVR2A/ACTRII, and BMPR2 (By similarity). Interacts with SMAD6 (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with TFE3; the interaction is enhanced in the
CC       presence of TGF-beta (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P62500,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q15714}.
