CC   -!- FUNCTION: Acts as a chemokine-like protein by regulating cell
CC       proliferation and migration through activation of G protein-coupled
CC       receptors (GPCRs), such as S1PR2 and FPR2 (PubMed:29453251). Stimulates
CC       chemotactic migration of macrophages mediated by the MAPK3/ERK1 and
CC       AKT1 pathway (By similarity). Blocks TNFSF11/RANKL-induced osteoclast
CC       formation from macrophages by inhibiting up-regulation of osteoclast
CC       fusogenic and differentiation genes (By similarity). Stimulation of
CC       macrophage migration and inhibition of osteoclast formation is mediated
CC       through the GPCR FPR2 (By similarity). Acts as an adipokine by
CC       negatively regulating vascular smooth muscle cell (VSMC) proliferation
CC       and migration in response to platelet-derived growth factor stimulation
CC       via GPCR S1PR2 and G protein GNA12/GNA13-transmitted RHOA signaling
CC       (PubMed:29453251). Inhibits injury-induced cell proliferation and
CC       neointima formation in the femoral arteries (PubMed:29453251).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q91WE9, ECO:0000269|PubMed:29453251}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted {ECO:0000269|PubMed:29453251}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in the epididymal adipose tissue, in
CC       aortic adventitial fibroblasts and low expression in vascular smooth
CC       muscle cells (at protein level) (PubMed:29453251). Expressed in the
CC       central nervous system, highly expressed in the hypothalamic
CC       paraventricular nucleus (PubMed:29453251).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:29453251}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Repressed in adipocytes after stimulation with Tnfa, Il6 and
CC       Ins1 (PubMed:29453251). Specifically down-regulated in the hypothalamic
CC       paraventricular nucleus following water deprivation (PubMed:29453251).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:29453251}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the TAFA family. {ECO:0000305}.
