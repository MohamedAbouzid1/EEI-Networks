CC   -!- FUNCTION: Ubiquitin-binding adapter that participates in inflammatory,
CC       antiviral and innate immune processes as well as selective autophagy
CC       regulation. Plays a key role in the negative regulation of NF-kappa-B
CC       and IRF3 signalings by acting as an adapter for the ubiquitin-editing
CC       enzyme A20/TNFAIP3 to bind and inactivate its substrates. Disrupts the
CC       interactions between the E3 ubiquitin ligase TRAF3 and TBK1/IKBKE to
CC       attenuate 'Lys63'-linked polyubiquitination of TBK1 and thereby IFN-
CC       beta production. Recruits also A20/TNFAIP3 to ubiquitinated signaling
CC       proteins TRAF6 and RIPK1, leading to their deubiquitination and
CC       disruption of IL-1 and TNF-induced NF-kappa-B signaling pathways.
CC       Inhibits virus-induced apoptosis by inducing the 'Lys-48'-linked
CC       polyubiquitination and degradation of MAVS via recruitment of the E3
CC       ligase ITCH, thereby attenuating MAVS-mediated apoptosis signaling. As
CC       a macroautophagy/autophagy receptor, facilitates the xenophagic
CC       clearance of pathogenic bacteria such as Salmonella typhimurium and
CC       Mycobacterium tuberculosis. Upon NBR1 recruitment to the SQSTM1-
CC       ubiquitin condensates, acts as the major recruiter of RB1CC1 to these
CC       ubiquitin condensates to promote their autophagic degradation. Mediates
CC       the autophagic degradation of other substrates including TICAM1.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q86VP1}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homooligomer. Interacts with TNFAIP3. Interacts with STARD13.
CC       Interacts with MYO6. Interacts with TOM1; the interaction is indirect
CC       and is mediated by MYO6, which acts as a bridge between TOM1 and
CC       TAX1BP1. Interacts with MAVS; this interaction induces MAVS
CC       polyubiquitination. Interacts with TNIP1. Interacts with TRAF6; this
CC       interaction mediates deubiquitination of TRAF6 and inhibition of NF-
CC       kappa-B activation. Interacts with RIPK1; this interaction negatively
CC       regulates RIPK1 ubiquitination. Interacts with NBR1. Interacts with
CC       TBK1. Interacts with RB1CC1. Interacts with SQSTM1. Interacts with
CC       AZI2. Interacts with TICAM1 and TRIM32; these interactions target
CC       TICAM1 to TAX1BP1-mediated selective autophagic degradation.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q86VP1}.
