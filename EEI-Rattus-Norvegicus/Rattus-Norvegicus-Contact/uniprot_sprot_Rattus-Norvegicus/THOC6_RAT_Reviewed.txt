CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the THO subcomplex of the TREX complex which is
CC       thought to couple mRNA transcription, processing and nuclear export,
CC       and which specifically associates with spliced mRNA and not with
CC       unspliced pre-mRNA. Plays a key structural role in the oligomerization
CC       of the THO-DDX39B complex. TREX is recruited to spliced mRNAs by a
CC       transcription-independent mechanism, binds to mRNA upstream of the
CC       exon-junction complex (EJC) and is recruited in a splicing- and cap-
CC       dependent manner to a region near the 5' end of the mRNA where it
CC       functions in mRNA export to the cytoplasm via the TAP/NXF1 pathway.
CC       Plays a role in apoptosis negative control involved in brain
CC       development. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q86W42}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the THO subcomplex, which is composed of THOC1,
CC       THOC2, THOC3, THOC5, THOC6 and THOC7. The THO subcomplex interacts with
CC       DDX39B to form the THO-DDX39B complex which multimerizes into a 28-
CC       subunit tetrameric assembly. Component of the transcription/export
CC       (TREX) complex at least composed of ALYREF/THOC4, DDX39B, SARNP/CIP29,
CC       CHTOP and the THO subcomplex; in the complex interacts with THOC5;
CC       together with THOC5 and THOC7, plays a key structural role in the
CC       oligomerization of the THO-DDX39B complex. TREX seems to have a dynamic
CC       structure involving ATP-dependent remodeling.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q86W42}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250}. Nucleus speckle
CC       {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the WD repeat THOC6 family. {ECO:0000305}.
