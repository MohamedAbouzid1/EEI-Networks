CC   -!- FUNCTION: Plays a role during embryonic arterial endothelium
CC       differentiation and vascular morphogenesis through the ACVRL1 receptor-
CC       dependent signaling pathway upon stimulation by bone morphogenetic
CC       proteins, such as GDF2/BMP9 and BMP10. Involved in the regulation of
CC       nociception, acting as a modulator of the interaction between TRPA1 and
CC       TRPV1, two molecular sensors and mediators of pain signals in dorsal
CC       root ganglia (DRG) neurons. Mechanistically, it weakens their
CC       interaction, thereby releasing the inhibition of TRPA1 by TRPV1 and
CC       increasing the single-channel open probability of the TRPA1-TRPV1
CC       complex. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9CQG9}.
