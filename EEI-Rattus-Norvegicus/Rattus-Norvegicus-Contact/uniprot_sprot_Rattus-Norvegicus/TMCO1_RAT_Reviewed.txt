CC   -!- FUNCTION: Endoplasmic reticulum (ER) calcium-selective channel
CC       preventing intracellular Ca2(+) stores from overfilling and maintaining
CC       calcium homeostasis in the ER. In response to endoplasmic reticulum
CC       (ER) Ca2(+) overloading, assembles into a homotetramer, forming a
CC       functional calcium-selective channel facilitating Ca2(+) release (By
CC       similarity). Mediates ER Ca2(+) homeostasis in osteoblasts and plays a
CC       key role in bone formation, via the CaMKII-HDAC4-RUNX2 signaling axis
CC       (By similarity). Component of the multi-pass translocon (MPT) complex
CC       that mediates insertion of multi-pass membrane proteins into the lipid
CC       bilayer of membranes (By similarity). The MPT complex takes over after
CC       the SEC61 complex: following membrane insertion of the first few
CC       transmembrane segments of proteins by the SEC61 complex, the MPT
CC       complex occludes the lateral gate of the SEC61 complex to promote
CC       insertion of subsequent transmembrane regions (By similarity). Within
CC       the MPT complex, the GEL subcomplex may mediate insertion of
CC       transmembrane regions into the membrane (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q921L3, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UM00}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
CC       Reaction=Ca(2+)(in) = Ca(2+)(out); Xref=Rhea:RHEA:29671,
CC         ChEBI:CHEBI:29108; Evidence={ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UM00};
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer and homotetramer. Homodimer under resting
CC       conditions; forms homotetramers following ER calcium overload.
CC       Component of the GET- and EMC-like (GEL) complex, composed of
CC       RAB5IF/OPTI and TMCO1. The GEL complex is part of the multi-pass
CC       translocon (MPT) complex, composed of three subcomplexes, the GEL
CC       complex (composed of RAB5IF/OPTI and TMCO1), the BOS complex (composed
CC       of NCLN/Nicalin, NOMO1 and TMEM147) and the PAT complex (composed of
CC       WDR83OS/Asterix and CCDC47). The MPT complex associates with the SEC61
CC       complex. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UM00}.
