CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the signaling pathway of IL-1 and Toll-like
CC       receptors (By similarity). Inhibits cell activation by microbial
CC       products (By similarity). Recruits IRAK1 to the IL-1 receptor complex
CC       (By similarity). Inhibits IRAK1 phosphorylation and kinase activity.
CC       Connects the ubiquitin pathway to autophagy by functioning as a
CC       ubiquitin-ATG8 family adapter and thus mediating autophagic clearance
CC       of ubiquitin conjugates (By similarity). The TOLLIP-dependent selective
CC       autophagy pathway plays an important role in clearance of cytotoxic
CC       polyQ proteins aggregates (By similarity). In a complex with TOM1,
CC       recruits ubiquitin-conjugated proteins onto early endosomes (By
CC       similarity). Binds to phosphatidylinositol 3-phosphate (PtdIns(3)P) (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H0E2}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Oligomerizes. Binds to TLR2 and the TLR4-MD2 complex via its
CC       C-terminus. Exists as complex with IRAK1 in unstimulated cells. Upon
CC       IL-1 signaling, Tollip binds to the activated IL-1 receptor complex
CC       containing IL-1RI, IL-1RacP and the adapter protein MyD88, where it
CC       interacts with the TIR domain of IL-1RacP. MyD88 then triggers IRAK1
CC       autophosphorylation, which in turn leads to the dissociation of IRAK1
CC       from Tollip and IL-1RAcP. Found in a complex with TOM1; interacts (via
CC       N-terminus) with TOM1 (via GAT domain); the interactions leads to TOM1-
CC       recruitment to endosomes and inhibition of TOLLIP binding to PtdIns(3)P
CC       (By similarity). Interacts with TOM1L2 (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       ATG8 family proteins (via the AIM motifs), and ubiquitin (via the CUE
CC       domain) (By similarity). Interacts with LRBA (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H0E2}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H0E2}.
CC       Endosome {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H0E2}. Early endosome
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H0E2}. Note=Localized to endo/exosomal
CC       vesicles. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H0E2}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Both ATG8-interaction motifs (AIM1 and AIM2) are required for
CC       the association with ATG8 family proteins.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H0E2}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The N-terminal TOM1-binding domain (residues 1-53) is a
CC       disordered domain that partially folds when bound to the GAT domain of
CC       TOM1. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9H0E2}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the tollip family. {ECO:0000305}.
