CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the EKC/KEOPS complex that is required for the
CC       formation of a threonylcarbamoyl group on adenosine at position 37

CC       threonylcarbamoyl-AMP (TC-AMP) to the N6 group of A37. TPRKB acts as an
CC       allosteric effector that regulates the t(6)A activity of the complex.
