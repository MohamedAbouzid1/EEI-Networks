CC   -!- FUNCTION: E3 ubiquitin ligase that plays an important role in antiviral
CC       immunity by restricting different viral infections including dengue
CC       virus or vesicular stomatitis indiana virus. Ubiquitinates viral
CC       proteins such as dengue virus NS3 thereby limiting infection. In
CC       addition, acts as a key mediator of type I interferon induced
CC       microtubule stabilization by directly associating to microtubules
CC       independently of its E3 ligase activity. Plays also a role in cataract
CC       formation together with TP53. Mechanistically, inhibits UVB-induced
CC       cell apoptosis and reactive oxygen species (ROS) production by inducing
CC       TP53 ubiquitination. Regulates centrosome dynamics and mitotic
CC       progression by ubiquitinating STK3/MST2; leading to its redistribution
CC       to the perinuclear cytoskeleton and subsequent phosphorylation by PLK1.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q86WT6}.
