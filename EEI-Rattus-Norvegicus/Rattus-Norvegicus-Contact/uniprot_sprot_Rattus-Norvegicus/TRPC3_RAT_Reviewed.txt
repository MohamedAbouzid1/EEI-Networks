CC   -!- FUNCTION: Forms a receptor-activated non-selective calcium permeant
CC       cation channel. May be operated by a phosphatidylinositol second
CC       messenger system activated by receptor tyrosine kinases or G-protein
CC       coupled receptors. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13507}.
CC   -!- ACTIVITY REGULATION: Activated by diacylglycerol (DAG) in a membrane-
CC       delimited fashion, independently of protein kinase C. Activated by
CC       inositol 1,4,5-triphosphate receptors (ITPR) with bound IP3. May be
CC       activated by internal calcium store depletion. Inhibited by
CC       intracellular Ca(2+). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13507}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homotetramer. Interacts with ITPR1, ITPR3, MX1 and RNF24.
CC       Interacts with JPH2; the interaction is involved in maintaining Ca(2+)
CC       homeostasis in skeletal muscle and is mediated by JPH2 'Ser-165'
CC       phosphorylation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13507}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000305}; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein {ECO:0000255}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The cytoplasmic portion of the protein is required for channel
CC       assembly and gating. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13507}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the transient receptor (TC 1.A.4) family. STrpC
CC       subfamily. TRPC3 sub-subfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
