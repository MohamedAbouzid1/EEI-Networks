CC   -!- FUNCTION: Structural component of specialized membrane microdomains
CC       known as tetraspanin-enriched microdomains (TERMs), which act as
CC       platforms for receptor clustering and signaling. Participates thereby
CC       in diverse biological functions such as cell signal transduction,
CC       adhesion, migration and protein trafficking (By similarity). Regulates
CC       neuronal differentiation in response to NGF by facilitating NGF-
CC       mediated activation of NTRK1/TRKA receptor tyrosine kinase and
CC       subsequent downstream signaling pathways (PubMed:31440771). Plays a
CC       role in the inhibition of TNFalpha-induced apoptosis. Mechanistically,
CC       inhibits the NF-kappa-B signaling pathway by blocking phosphorylation
CC       of CHUK. Promotes also the stability of the thiamine transporter
CC       1/SLC19A2 in intestinal epithelial cells leading to an increase of
CC       thiamine uptake process (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60635,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:31440771}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with SLC19A2 (By similarity). Interacts with
CC       NTRK1/TRKA (PubMed:31440771). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O60635,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:31440771}.
