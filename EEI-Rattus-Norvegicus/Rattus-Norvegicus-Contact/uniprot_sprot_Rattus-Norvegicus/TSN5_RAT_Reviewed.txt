CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of TspanC8 subgroup, composed of 6 members that interact
CC       with the transmembrane metalloprotease ADAM10. This interaction is
CC       required for ADAM10 exit from the endoplasmic reticulum and for
CC       enzymatic maturation and trafficking to the cell surface as well as
CC       substrate specificity. Different TspanC8/ADAM10 complexes have distinct
CC       substrates. Promotes ADAM10-mediated cleavage of CD44. Seems to
CC       regulate VE-cadherin expression in endothelial cells probably through
CC       interaction with ADAM10, promoting leukocyte transmigration.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P62079}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with ADAM10; the interaction influences ADAM10
