CC   -!- FUNCTION: Protein associated with the U5 snRNP, during its maturation
CC       and its post-splicing recycling and which is required for spliceosomal
CC       tri-snRNP complex assembly in the nucleus. Has a molecular sequestering
CC       activity and transiently hinders SNRNP200 binding sites for
CC       constitutive splicing factors that intervene later during the assembly
CC       of the spliceosome and splicing. Together with its molecular
CC       sequestering activity, may also function as a molecular adapter and
CC       placeholder, coordinating the assembly of the U5 snRNP and its
CC       association with the U4/U6 di-snRNP. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y5U2}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts in a RNA-independent manner with distinct U5 snRNP-
CC       containing complexes, the mono-U5 snRNP and the post-splicing U5 snRNP-
CC       PRPF19 complex. Interacts with SNRNP200; the interaction is direct,
CC       excludes recruitment of C9ORF78 and WBP4 to SNRNP200 and negatively
CC       regulates its RNA helicase activity. Interacts with PRPF8; the
CC       interaction is direct. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y5U2}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y5U2}. Cytoplasm
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y5U2}. Note=Shuttles between the cytoplasm and
CC       the nucleus, associated with the U5 snRNP.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y5U2}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the TSSC4 family. {ECO:0000305}.
