CC       step of the polyglycylation reaction. Polyglycylates alpha-tubulin and
CC       beta-tubulin. Polyglycylates non-tubulin proteins such as nucleosome
CC       assembly protein NAP1. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:A4Q9F3}.
