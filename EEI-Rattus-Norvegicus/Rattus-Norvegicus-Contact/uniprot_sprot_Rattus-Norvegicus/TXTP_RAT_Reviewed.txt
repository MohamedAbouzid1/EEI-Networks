CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mitochondrial electroneutral antiporter that exports citrate
CC       from the mitochondria into the cytosol in exchange for malate. Also
CC       able to mediate the exchange of citrate for isocitrate,
CC       phosphoenolpyruvate, cis-aconitate and to a lesser extend cis-
CC       aconitate, maleate and succinate (PubMed:2804096). In the cytoplasm
CC       citrate is important in the regulation of glycolysis through a feedback
CC       mechanism and in the production of acetyl-CoA which is needed for the
CC       synthesis of fatty acids, sterols, prostaglandins, dolichol and
CC       coenzyme Q (CoQ). Required for proper neuromuscular junction formation
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P53007,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2804096}.
