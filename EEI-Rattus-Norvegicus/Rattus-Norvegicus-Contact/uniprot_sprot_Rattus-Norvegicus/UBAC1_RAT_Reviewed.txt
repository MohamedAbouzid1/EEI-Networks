CC   -!- FUNCTION: Non-catalytic component of the KPC complex, a E3 ubiquitin-
CC       protein ligase complex that mediates polyubiquitination of target
CC       proteins, such as CDKN1B and NFKB1. The KPC complex catalyzes
CC       polyubiquitination and proteasome-mediated degradation of CDKN1B during
CC       G1 phase of the cell cycle. The KPC complex also acts as a key
CC       regulator of the NF-kappa-B signaling by promoting maturation of the
CC       NFKB1 component of NF-kappa-B by catalyzing ubiquitination of the NFKB1
CC       p105 precursor. Within the KPC complex, UBAC1 acts as an adapter that
CC       promotes the transfer of target proteins that have been
CC       polyubiquitinated by RNF123/KPC1 to the 26S proteasome.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSL1}.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Protein modification; protein ubiquitination.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSL1}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the KPC complex composed of RNF123/KPC1 and
CC       UBAC1/KPC2. Interacts (via ubiquitin-like domain) with RNF123.
CC       Interacts (via ubiquitin-like and UBA domains) with the proteasome via
CC       its N-terminal domain. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSL1}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSL1}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The UBA domains recognize and bind polyubiquitinated proteins.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BSL1}.
