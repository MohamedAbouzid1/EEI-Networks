CC   -!- FUNCTION: Deubiquitinates histone H2A, a specific tag for epigenetic
CC       transcriptional repression, thereby acting as a coactivator.
CC       Deubiquitination of histone H2A releaves the repression of di- and
CC       trimethylation of histone H3 at 'Lys-4', resulting in regulation of
CC       transcriptional initiation. Regulates gene expression via histone H2A
CC       deubiquitination (By similarity). Deubiquitinates BAZ2A/TIP5 leading to
CC       its stabilization. Also capable of removing NEDD8 from NEDD8 conjugates
CC       but has no effect on Sentrin-1 conjugates. Also acts as a negative
CC       regulator of the ribosome quality control (RQC) by mediating
CC       deubiquitination of 40S ribosomal proteins RPS10/eS10 and RPS20/uS10,
CC       thereby antagonizing ZNF598-mediated 40S ubiquitination (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9QZL6,
