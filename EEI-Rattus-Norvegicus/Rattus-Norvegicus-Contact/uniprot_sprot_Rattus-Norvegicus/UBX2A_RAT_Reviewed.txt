CC   -!- FUNCTION: Acts to repress the ubiquitination and subsequent endoplasmic
CC       reticulum-associated degradation of CHRNA3 by the STUB1-VCP-UBXN2A
CC       complex in cortical neurons (PubMed:26265139). Also acts to promote the
CC       translocation of CHRNA3 to the plasma membrane and subsequently
CC       increases plasma membrane acetylcholine-gated ion-channel activation
CC       (By similarity). Plays a role in the inhibition of STUB1-mediated TP53
CC       degradation, via its interaction with HSPA9 which acts to inhibit TP53
CC       binding to HSPA9 (By similarity). Positively mediates the
CC       ubiquitination and proteosomal degradation of RICTOR, may thereby act
CC       as a negative regulator of the mTORC2 pathway (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P68543, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99KJ0,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:26265139}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of a complex composed of STUB1/CHIP, VCP/p97, CHRNA3, and
CC       UBXN2A that modulates the ubiquitination and endoplasmic reticulum-
CC       associated degradation (ERAD) of CHRNA3 (PubMed:26265139). Within the
CC       complex UBXN2A acts as a scaffold protein required for the interaction
CC       of CHRNA3 with VCP/p97, this interaction also inhibits CHRNA3
CC       ubiquitination by STUB1/CHIP and subsequently ERAD (PubMed:26265139).
CC       Interacts (via SEP domain) with CHRNA3 and interacts (via UBX domain)
CC       with VCP/P97; these interactions are required for the interaction of
CC       CHRNA3 with the STUB1-VCP-UBXN2A complex (PubMed:19474315,
CC       PubMed:26265139). Interacts with HSPA9/MOT-2 (via SBD domain); the
CC       interaction inhibits HSPA9/MOT-2 interaction with and degradation of
CC       p53, thereby promotes p53 translocation to the nucleus (By similarity).
CC       Interacts with RICTOR (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P68543,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19474315, ECO:0000269|PubMed:26265139}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Golgi apparatus {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19474315}.
CC       Endoplasmic reticulum {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19474315}. Perikaryon
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99KJ0}. Cell projection, dendrite
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99KJ0}. Nucleus {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P68543}.
