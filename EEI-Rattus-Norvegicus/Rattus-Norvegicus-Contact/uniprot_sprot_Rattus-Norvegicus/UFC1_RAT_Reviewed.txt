CC   -!- FUNCTION: E2-like enzyme which specifically catalyzes the second step
CC       in ufmylation. Accepts the ubiquitin-like modifier UFM1 from the E1
CC       enzyme UBA5 and forms an intermediate with UFM1 via a thioester
CC       linkage. Ufmylation is involved in various processes, such as ribosome
CC       recycling, response to DNA damage, interferon response or reticulophagy
CC       (also called ER-phagy). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y3C8}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with UBA5 (via C-terminus). Interacts with UFL1.
CC       Interacts with UFM1. Interacts with KIRREL3.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y3C8}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: In absence of UBA5, the active site is solvated by water

CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9Y3C8}.
