CC   -!- FUNCTION: Cell adhesion glycoprotein predominantly expressed on the
CC       surface of endothelial cells that plays an important role in immune
CC       surveillance and inflammation. Acts as a major regulator of leukocyte
CC       adhesion to the endothelium through interaction with different types of
CC       integrins. During inflammatory responses, binds ligands on the surface
CC       of activated endothelial cells to initiate the activation of calcium
CC       channels and the plasma membrane-associated small GTPase RAC1 leading
CC       to leukocyte transendothelial migration. Serves also as a quality-
CC       control checkpoint for entry into bone marrow by providing a 'don't-
CC       eat-me' stamping in the context of major histocompatibility complex
CC       (MHC) class-I presentation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19320}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Binds to ECMV-D capsid proteins and acts as a receptor for
CC       this virus. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: [Vascular cell adhesion protein 1]: Cell membrane
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19320}; Single-pass type I membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19320}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: [Soluble Vascular Cell Adhesion Molecule-1]:
CC       Secreted {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19320}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in aortic endothelial cells, with low
CC       expression in the descending thoracic aorta and the outer curvature of
CC       the aortic arch, where pulsatory shear stress exists, and high in the
CC       inner curvature of the aortic arch, where oscillatory shear stress
CC       prevails (at protein level) (PubMed:28167758). Expressed on inflamed
CC       vascular endothelium, as well as on macrophage-like and dendritic cell
CC       types in both normal and inflamed tissue (PubMed:1371918).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28167758, ECO:0000303|PubMed:1371918}.
CC   -!- PTM: Cleaved by the metalloproteinase ADAM17 to generate the soluble
CC       form. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19320}.
CC   -!- PTM: Sialoglycoprotein. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19320}.
CC   -!- PTM: Ubiquitinated by TRIM65 via 'Lys-48'-linked ubiquitination;
CC       leading to proteasomal degradation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P19320}.
