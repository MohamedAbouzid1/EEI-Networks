CC   -!- FUNCTION: Molecular scaffold protein for various multimeric protein
CC       complexes. Acts as a module in the assembly of a multicomponent
CC       scaffold for the ERK pathway, linking ERK responses to specific
CC       agonists. At low concentrations it enhances ERK activation, whereas
CC       high concentrations lead to the inhibition of ERK activation. Also
CC       involved in response to hypoxia by acting as a negative regulator of
CC       HIF1A/HIF-1-alpha via its interaction with EGLN3/PHD3. May promote
CC       degradation of HIF1A. May act by recruiting signaling complexes to a
CC       specific upstream activator (By similarity). May also be involved in
CC       pre-mRNA splicing. Participates in tight junction development by
CC       regulating apico-basal polarity, a key step in tissue development and
CC       organization. Mechanistically, regulates the translocation of PAR6-aPKC
CC       from the cytoplasm to the apical surface by acting as an adapter
CC       between PARD6B AND CRB3. Acts also as a negative regulator of mTORC1
CC       under nutrient-rich conditions by binding to the active Rag GTPases to
CC       inhibit mTORC1 localization to the lysosome and phosphorylation of
CC       downstream targets. This facilitates constitutive basal autophagy
CC       during nutrient availability (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRX9, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9DAJ4,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16407229}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with EGLN3/PHD3 (PubMed:16407229). Interacts with
CC       ERK signaling proteins MAP2K1/MEK1, MAP2K2/MEK2, LAMTOR3, ARAF/Raf-1,
CC       MAPK1/ERK2 and MAPK3/ERK1 (By similarity). Identified in the
CC       spliceosome C complex. Interacts with PARD6B and CRB3. Interacts
CC       strongly with GTP-bound RRAGA but not with inactive GDP-bound.
CC       Interacts with p62/SQSTM1 (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRX9, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9DAJ4,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16407229}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16407229}. Lysosome
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRX9}. Nucleus {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16407229}.
CC       Note=Predominantly cytoplasmic. Partially nuclear. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Highly expressed in testis and brain. Expressed at
CC       intermediate level in heart, liver and kidney. Weakly expressed in
CC       spleen and lung and absent in muscle. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16407229}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the WD repeat MORG1 family. {ECO:0000305}.
