CC   -!- FUNCTION: Protects actin filaments from depolymerization (By
CC       similarity). Required for correct morphology of cell membranes and
CC       maturation of intercalated disks of cardiomyocytes via facilitating
CC       localization of XIRP1 and CDH2 to the termini of aligned mature
CC       cardiomyocytes (By similarity). Thereby required for correct postnatal
CC       heart development and growth regulation that is crucial for overall
CC       heart morphology and diastolic function (By similarity). Required for
CC       normal electrical conduction in the heart including formation of the
CC       infranodal ventricular conduction system and normal action potential
CC       configuration, as a result of its interaction with the cardiac ion
CC       channel components Scn5a/Nav1.5 and Kcna5/Kv1.5 (By similarity).
CC       Required for regular actin filament spacing of the paracrystalline
CC       array in both inner and outer hair cells of the cochlea, thereby
CC       required for maintenance of stereocilia morphology (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:A4UGR9, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q4U4S6}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with ACTN2 (By similarity). Interacts with F-actin
CC       (By similarity). Interacts with NEBL (via SH3 domain) (By similarity).
CC       Interacts with Kcna5/Kv1.5 and Scn5a/Nav1.5; the interactions are
CC       required for normal action potential configuration in the heart (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:A4UGR9,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q4U4S6}.
