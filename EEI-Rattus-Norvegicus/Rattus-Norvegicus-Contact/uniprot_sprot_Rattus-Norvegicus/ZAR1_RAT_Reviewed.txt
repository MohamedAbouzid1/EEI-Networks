CC   -!- FUNCTION: mRNA-binding protein that mediates formation of MARDO
CC       (mitochondria-associated ribonucleoprotein domain), a membraneless
CC       compartment that stores maternal mRNAs in oocytes. MARDO assembly
CC       around mitochondria is directed by an increase in mitochondrial
CC       membrane potential during oocyte growth. Promotes formation of MARDO
CC       phase-separated membraneless compartment by undergoing liquid-liquid
CC       phase separation upon binding to maternal mRNAs. Binds to the 3'-UTR of
CC       maternal mRNAs. Maternal mRNAs stored in the MARDO are translationally
CC       repressed. Essential for female fertility and oocyte-to-embryo
CC       transition by coordinating maternal mRNA storage, translation and
CC       degradation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80SU3}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with YBX2. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80SU3}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm, Cytoplasmic ribonucleoprotein granule
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80SU3}. Cytoplasm
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80SU3}. Note=Specifically localizes to MARDO
CC       (mitochondria-associated ribonucleoprotein domain), a mitochondria-
CC       associated membraneless compartment that stores mRNAs in oocytes.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80SU3}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Disordered region at the N-terminus undergoes liquid-liquid
CC       phase separation (LLPS) for the formation of MARDO (mitochondria-
CC       associated ribonucleoprotein domain), a membraneless compartment that
CC       stores maternal mRNAs in oocytes. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80SU3}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The 3CxxC-type mediates binding to the 3'-UTR of mRNAs.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80SU3}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylation by CDK1 does not regulate formation of MARDO

CC       compartment. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80SU3}.
CC   -!- PTM: Ubiquitinated and degradaded by the proteasome during oocyte
CC       meiotic maturation, leading to MARDO (mitochondria-associated
CC       ribonucleoprotein domain) membraneless compartment dissolution.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80SU3}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the ZAR1 family. {ECO:0000305}.
