CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription factor that acts as a cardiac regulator and an
CC       effector of alpha1-adrenergic signaling. Binds to PE response elements
CC       (PERE) present in the promoter of genes such as ANF/NPPA and acts as a
CC       direct transcriptional activator of NPPA. Also acts as a cofactor with
CC       GATA4, a key cardiac regulator. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16166646}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Binds DNA. Interacts with GATA4.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16166646}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16166646}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in both embryonic, fetal and adult heart.
CC       Also expressed in lung, skeletal muscle and adrenal glands.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16166646}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Expressed in cardiomyocytes from 14 dpc.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16166646}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Up-regulated by activation of alpha1-adrenergic receptors.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16166646}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the krueppel C2H2-type zinc-finger protein
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
