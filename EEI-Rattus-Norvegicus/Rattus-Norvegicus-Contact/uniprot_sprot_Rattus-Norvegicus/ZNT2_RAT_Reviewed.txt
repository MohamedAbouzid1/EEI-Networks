CC   -!- FUNCTION: Electroneutral proton-coupled antiporter concentrating zinc
CC       ions into a variety of intracellular organelles including endosomes,
CC       zymogen granules and mitochondria. Thereby, plays a crucial role in
CC       cellular zinc homeostasis to confer upon cells protection against its
CC       potential cytotoxicity (PubMed:20133611, PubMed:8617223). Regulates the
CC       zinc concentration of milk, through the transport of zinc ions into
CC       secretory vesicles of mammary cells (By similarity). By concentrating
CC       zinc ions into lysosomes participates to lysosomal-mediated cell death
CC       during early mammary gland involution (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRI3, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20133611,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8617223}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
CC       Reaction=2 H(+)(out) + Zn(2+)(in) = 2 H(+)(in) + Zn(2+)(out);

CC         Evidence={ECO:0000305|PubMed:20133611, ECO:0000305|PubMed:8617223};
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Interacts (via lysosomal targeting motif) with
CC       AP3D1; in AP-3-mediated transport to lysosomes. Interacts with TMEM163.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRI3}.

CC       {ECO:0000255}. Note=Localization to lysosomes is induced by TNF-alpha.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRI3}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Detected in intestine, kidney, seminal vesicles and
CC       testis. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8617223}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Up-regulated by dexamethasone (at protein level).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20133611}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylated at Ser-283. Phosphorylation at Ser-283 prevents

CC       localization to lysosomes, accumulation of zinc into lysosomes and
CC       lysosomal-mediated cell death is induced by TNF-alpha.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BRI3}.
